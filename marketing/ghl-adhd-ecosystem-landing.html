Frequently asked questions

Quick answers before you start your free trial.

Is this another productivity app?

No. ADHD Business Ecosystem is not another planner, task list, or dashboard. It starts with what is happening right now and helps you take one doable next step.

What makes this different?

Most tools assume you are already clear, motivated, and organized. This ecosystem starts with real ADHD life: how you feel, what feels too loud, what is pulling your attention, and what support would help you move forward.

Who is this for?

It is built for ADHD entrepreneurs, solopreneurs, coaches, consultants, speakers, creators, business owners, multi-passionate people, and adults diagnosed later in life who want support without shame.

How does the 7-day free trial work?

You can try any text or voice tier free for 7 days. Choose the support style that fits you. If it does not feel like the right fit, cancel during the trial and you will not be charged.

What if I struggle with consistency?

That is exactly why Fresh Start is part of the experience. You can come back after inconsistent days, burnout, overwhelm, or avoidance without shame and simply begin again.

What if I procrastinate or avoid important work?

The ecosystem includes procrastination support, perfectionism support, Need A Nudge, Fresh Start, and Coach Mode to help you lower the pressure and begin with one doable move.

What is included with voice?

Voice tiers allow you to talk things through when typing is not enough. It can help with overwhelm, decision pressure, emotional processing, business momentum, and getting unstuck in the moment.

Which tier should I choose?

Choose Text Access if you like typing through your thoughts. Choose Voice Lite if you process better by talking sometimes. Choose Power Voice if you want deeper or more frequent voice support.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel if it is not the right fit. The purpose of the free trial is to let you experience the support before deciding.

Is this therapy or medical treatment?

No. ADHD Business Ecosystem is not therapy or medical treatment. It is a supportive ADHD business ecosystem designed for self-understanding, emotional support, momentum, focus, and business follow-through.
