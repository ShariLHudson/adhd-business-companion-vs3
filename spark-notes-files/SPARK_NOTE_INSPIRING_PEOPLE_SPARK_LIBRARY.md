# Spark Note™ Inspiring People Spark Library

## Purpose

This library contains people-focused Sparks that highlight individuals who created, discovered, changed, challenged, or inspired the world.

These Sparks should not simply list accomplishments.

They should tell the human story:

- who they were
- what challenge they faced
- what they created or changed
- why it mattered
- what lesson the user can take away

The goal:

Help users see possibility in their own lives.

# SPARK-PER-001

## Title
Thomas Edison: Persistence Through Failure

## Category
Inspiring People

## Tags
innovation, resilience, invention, persistence

## Short Teaser
Thousands of attempts can lead to one world-changing idea.

## Story

Thomas Edison is remembered for inventions like the practical electric light bulb, but his journey included many unsuccessful experiments.

He viewed failed attempts as information that helped him understand what did not work.

His approach was based on curiosity and persistence.

## Why It Matters

Progress rarely happens perfectly the first time.

Innovation often requires patience, experimentation, and learning.

## Spark It

What could you learn from something that did not work yet?

# Inspiring People Guidelines

Choose people who represent:

- curiosity
- creativity
- courage
- resilience
- innovation
- kindness
- leadership
- imagination

Include:

- challenges
- lessons learned
- human moments

Avoid creating only biographies.

Every story should answer:

"What can this teach me?"
