# Spark Estate™ Card Ecosystem Specification - Phase 16

## Purpose

This document defines the complete Spark Estate™ card ecosystem.

Cards are not just visual elements.

They are intelligent touchpoints that help members understand, act,
create, and continue progress.

The goal:

Show the right information at the right time without overwhelming the
member.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Principle

Cards should answer:

"What would be most helpful right now?"

Cards should not become:

-   notifications
-   clutter
-   endless dashboards
-   overwhelming information panels

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Card Design Philosophy

Every card should be:

-   simple
-   recognizable
-   purposeful
-   personalized
-   actionable when needed

The member should immediately understand:

"What is this for?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Card Ecosystem

Spark Estate™ uses specialized cards.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Spark Card™

## Purpose

Daily companion moment.

Provides:

-   curiosity
-   inspiration
-   reflection
-   encouragement
-   meaning

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Sources

May use:

-   discovery library
-   member interests
-   important dates
-   seasonal information
-   gratitude and meaning content

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Behavior

Changes daily.

Can reflect:

-   birthdays
-   anniversaries
-   trips
-   milestones
-   meaningful dates

Tone adapts appropriately.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Momentum Card™

## Purpose

Help the member move forward.

Shows:

-   current focus
-   next action
-   progress
-   encouragement

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Sources

Uses:

-   active projects
-   goals
-   Momentum history
-   wins

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Example

"Your next step for Website Launch is reviewing your homepage message."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Project Card™

## Purpose

Keep important work visible.

Shows:

-   project name
-   status
-   next action
-   progress

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Knowledge Card™

## Purpose

Deliver learning.

Shows:

-   concept
-   explanation
-   example
-   application

Used by:

Momentum Institute™

and other learning areas.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Reflection Card™

## Purpose

Help members pause and learn.

Examples:

-   What worked?
-   What did you learn?
-   What would you change?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Win Card™

## Purpose

Capture progress.

Shows:

-   accomplishment
-   milestone
-   evidence of capability

Connects to:

Evidence Bank

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Card Selection Intelligence

Cards should be selected based on:

-   member profile
-   current goals
-   active projects
-   recent activity
-   energy level
-   previous interactions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Card Priority Rules

When multiple cards are possible:

Priority:

1.  Important personal moments
2.  Immediate support needed
3.  Active progress
4.  Encouragement
5.  Learning opportunities
6.  Discovery

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Card Placement

Cards should appear where they naturally belong.

Examples:

Estate:

-   Spark Card™

Chamber of Momentum™:

-   Momentum Card
-   Project Card
-   Win Card

Learning areas:

-   Knowledge Card

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Card Interaction Rules

A card may allow:

-   open
-   save
-   reflect
-   continue
-   connect
-   complete

Avoid:

-   too many buttons
-   complex actions
-   forcing decisions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Card Memory

Cards should learn:

-   what members open
-   what they save
-   what helps them act
-   what they ignore

Use this to improve future suggestions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Card Language Support

All cards should support member language preferences.

The experience should translate:

-   text
-   labels
-   prompts
-   actions

while preserving meaning and tone.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# ADHD-Friendly Rules

Cards should:

-   reduce cognitive load
-   highlight one important thing
-   avoid too much information
-   support returning after time away

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Card Lifecycle

Every card follows:

Created

↓

Displayed

↓

Interacted With

↓

Saved or Completed

↓

Learning Updated

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Final Experience

Cards should feel like:

"A helpful note from Spark."

Not:

"Another notification."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Final Vision

Spark Estate™ cards create small moments of connection throughout the
member journey.

They help the member notice:

-   what matters
-   what comes next
-   what they have accomplished
-   where they can grow
