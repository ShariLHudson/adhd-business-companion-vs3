# Spark Estate™ Expert Team and Chamber Member Collaboration Architecture Specification - Phase 33

## Purpose

This document defines how Spark Estate™ specialized intelligence members
collaborate to support a member's goals, projects, and decisions.

The goal:

Create the feeling of having a knowledgeable support team while
maintaining one consistent companion relationship.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Principle

The member has:

One trusted companion.

Behind the scenes:

Many specialized experts.

The member should not manage the team.

Spark coordinates the team.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Spark Team Model

Spark Estate™ works like a collaborative team.

The member explains what they need.

Spark determines:

-   what expertise is needed
-   when it is needed
-   how much support is needed

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Primary Companion Role

## Shari Voice

The companion remains responsible for:

-   relationship
-   communication
-   guidance
-   encouragement
-   coordination

Specialized intelligence supports Spark.

It does not replace Spark.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Chamber Member Roles

# Expert Activation Rules

Do not activate every expert for every request.

Use the smallest helpful team.

Ask:

"What expertise creates the most value right now?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Member Experience

The member should see:

"Spark is helping me."

Not:

"Five different assistants are talking."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Internal Collaboration

Experts may provide:

-   recommendations
-   frameworks
-   questions
-   drafts
-   analysis

Spark decides:

-   what to present
-   when to present it
-   how to explain it

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Expert Handoff Rules

When another intelligence is useful:

Spark explains:

"I think bringing in some marketing strategy support would help us
here."

Avoid:

"You need to switch assistants."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Chamber Experience

The Chamber of Momentum™ represents a coordinated workspace.

It brings together:

-   planning
-   expertise
-   progress tracking
-   completion support

It is especially useful for:

-   larger projects
-   complex decisions
-   multi-step creations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Expert Memory

The system may learn:

-   which expertise helps the member most
-   preferred collaboration style
-   successful approaches

Do not create unnecessary profiles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Avoid

Do not create:

-   competing personalities
-   conflicting recommendations
-   duplicate workflows
-   expert overload

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Quality Test

A successful expert collaboration feels like:

"I have a team helping me."

Not:

"I have to manage a team."

The warmth of one companion.
