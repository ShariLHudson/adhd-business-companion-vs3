# Spark Note™ ADHD-Friendly Sparks Library

## Purpose

This library contains Sparks designed specifically for the ADHD
entrepreneur experience.

These Sparks recognize that many users may experience:

-   creative thinking
-   big ideas
-   curiosity
-   intense interests
-   difficulty with overwhelm
-   challenges with consistency
-   moments of self-doubt

The goal is not to diagnose or label people.

The goal:

Help users recognize strengths, possibilities, and practical ways their
minds can work for them.

# ADHD-Friendly Spark Guidelines

These Sparks should:

-   recognize strengths
-   encourage curiosity
-   reduce shame
-   support self-awareness
-   create possibility

Avoid:

-   medical claims
-   stereotypes
-   making ADHD the entire identity
-   suggesting everyone experiences ADHD the same way

The message:

Your mind has strengths. Understanding yourself helps you use them.
