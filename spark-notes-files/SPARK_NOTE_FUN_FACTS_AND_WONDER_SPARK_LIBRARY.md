# Spark Note™ Fun Facts and Wonder Spark Library

## Purpose

This library contains Sparks designed to create curiosity, surprise, and enjoyment.

These are the moments that make someone say:

"Wait... really?"

Fun Facts should not be random trivia.

Every fact should include:

- the interesting discovery
- why it is surprising
- why it matters
- a connection back to curiosity or life

The goal:

Create moments of wonder.

# SPARK-FACT-001

## Title
Honey Never Spoils

## Category
Fun Fact

## Tags
nature, science, curiosity

## Short Teaser
A food discovered thousands of years ago can still be preserved today.

## Story

Honey has natural properties that help prevent spoilage.

Its low moisture content and natural acidity make it difficult for many microorganisms to grow.

Archaeologists have discovered ancient honey that remained preserved.

## Why It Matters

Nature often contains solutions that inspire science and innovation.

## Spark It

What everyday thing around you might be more interesting than you realize?

# Fun Fact Spark Guidelines

Fun Facts should:

- create curiosity
- teach something surprising
- be accurate
- create a positive feeling

Avoid:

- useless trivia
- facts with no meaning
- shock value without purpose

Every Fun Fact should answer:

"Why would someone care?"
