# Spark Estate™ Knowledge and Asset Library Architecture Specification - Phase 19

## Purpose

This document defines how Spark Estate™ organizes, stores, retrieves,
and uses knowledge assets.

The goal:

Make sure Spark has access to the right knowledge at the right time
without creating confusion or overwhelming the member.

Knowledge should support the companion experience.

It should not become a disconnected information warehouse.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Principle

Knowledge exists to help members:

-   understand
-   create
-   decide
-   improve
-   complete

The question is not:

"What information do we have?"

The question is:

"How can this information help this person move forward?"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Knowledge Asset Categories

## 1. Frameworks

Purpose:

Provide repeatable ways of thinking.

Examples:

-   decision frameworks
-   planning frameworks
-   creation frameworks
-   business frameworks

Used for:

-   guidance
-   teaching
-   problem solving

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Templates

Purpose:

Help members start faster.

Examples:

-   email templates
-   project templates
-   strategy templates
-   planning templates
-   content templates

Templates should include:

-   purpose
-   instructions
-   examples
-   customization options

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Strategies

Purpose:

Provide approaches for accomplishing goals.

Examples:

-   marketing strategies
-   productivity strategies
-   customer strategies
-   business strategies

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Examples

Purpose:

Reduce blank-page friction.

Examples:

-   sample emails
-   sample plans
-   sample projects
-   sample messaging

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Guides and Learning Resources

Purpose:

Teach concepts.

Include:

-   explanations
-   examples
-   applications
-   next steps

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Member-Created Assets

Purpose:

Store what the member creates.

Examples:

-   documents
-   strategies
-   projects
-   templates
-   plans

Every asset should be:

-   accurate
-   useful
-   organized
-   understandable
-   actionable

Avoid:

-   duplicate information
-   outdated information
-   unnecessary complexity
