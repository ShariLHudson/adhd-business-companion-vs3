# Spark Note™ Innovation and Discovery Spark Library

## Purpose

This library contains Sparks about discoveries, breakthroughs,
experiments, and moments when curiosity changed the world.

These Sparks focus on the journey:

-   asking questions
-   exploring possibilities
-   testing ideas
-   learning from unexpected results
-   turning curiosity into discovery

The goal:

Help users see that innovation often begins with noticing something
ordinary in a new way.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# SPARK-INNOV-001

## Title

The Discovery of Penicillin

## Category

Innovation and Discovery

## Tags

science, discovery, curiosity, medicine

## Short Teaser

A forgotten experiment became one of the most important discoveries in
medicine.

## Story

Alexander Fleming noticed that mold had affected a bacterial culture in
his laboratory.

Instead of throwing away the experiment, he investigated what had
happened.

That observation eventually led to the development of penicillin.

## Why It Matters

Important discoveries are not always planned.

Sometimes progress comes from paying attention to unexpected results.

## Spark It

What unexpected result in your life or business might be worth
exploring?

# Innovation Spark Guidelines

Innovation Sparks should highlight:

-   curiosity
-   experimentation
-   discovery
-   problem solving
-   unexpected outcomes

Avoid:

-   making innovation seem impossible
-   focusing only on famous inventors
-   ignoring the process behind breakthroughs

The message:

Great ideas often begin with a simple question.
