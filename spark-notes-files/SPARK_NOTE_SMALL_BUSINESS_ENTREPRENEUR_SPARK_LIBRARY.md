# Spark Note™ Small Business and Everyday Entrepreneur Spark Library

## Purpose

This library contains Sparks about everyday entrepreneurs, small
businesses, creators, makers, and people who turned simple ideas into
meaningful work.

These Sparks are designed for the majority of entrepreneurs who are not
trying to build giant companies.

They are for people who:

-   create products
-   serve customers
-   build side businesses
-   solve problems
-   turn skills into income
-   make something meaningful

The goal:

Help users see that entrepreneurship is built one idea, one customer,
and one improvement at a time.

# SPARK-SMB-001

## Title

The Homemade Product That Became a Business

## Category

Small Business

## Tags

entrepreneurship, creativity, small-business, makers

## Short Teaser

Many businesses begin with someone creating something they love.

## Story

Some entrepreneurs begin by making something for themselves, friends, or
family.

A craft, recipe, design, or solution solves a small problem.

Over time, others see the value and the idea grows.

## Why It Matters

A business does not always begin with a large plan.

Sometimes it begins with one person creating something useful.

## Spark It

What do you already create, solve, or help with that others might value?

# Small Business Spark Guidelines

These Sparks should:

-   celebrate realistic entrepreneurship
-   honor small beginnings
-   recognize creativity
-   encourage experimentation
-   highlight customer connection

Avoid:

-   only focusing on billion-dollar companies
-   unrealistic overnight success stories
-   making entrepreneurship look effortless

The message:

Every meaningful business starts somewhere.
