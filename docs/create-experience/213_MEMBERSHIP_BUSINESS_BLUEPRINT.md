# 213 — Membership Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.membership`

## Purpose

Help a founder create, operate, retain, and grow a membership business that delivers ongoing value without overwhelming the owner or members.

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define membership purpose
- identify the right member
- choose a membership model
- design recurring value
- price and package membership
- create onboarding
- build engagement and retention
- manage community and content
- track health and profitability
- improve without content overload
