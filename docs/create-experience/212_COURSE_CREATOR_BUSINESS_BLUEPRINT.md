# 212 — Course Creator Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.course_creator`

## Purpose

Help a course creator validate, design, launch, deliver, improve, and scale learning experiences that produce meaningful student outcomes and sustainable business value.

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define learner and problem
- validate demand
- select course model
- design outcomes and curriculum
- create content efficiently
- price and package the course
- launch and enroll learners
- support completion and application
- measure outcomes
- improve and expand the course business

## Best-fit users

- coaches
- consultants
- authors
- speakers
- subject-matter experts
- trainers
- educators
- creators building digital learning products

## Business DNA adaptation

Adapt for:

- first course versus course portfolio
- live cohort versus evergreen
- self-paced versus facilitated
- low-ticket versus premium
- consumer versus business audience
- certification versus non-certification
- solo creator versus delivery team
