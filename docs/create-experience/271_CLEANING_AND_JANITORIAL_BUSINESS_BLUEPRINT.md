# 271 — Cleaning & Janitorial Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.cleaning_janitorial`

## Purpose

Help residential, commercial, specialty-cleaning, and janitorial businesses create consistent services, price work, schedule teams, manage sites and supplies, document quality, communicate with clients, and grow profitably.

## Best-fit users

- residential cleaners
- commercial janitorial businesses
- office-cleaning providers
- move-in and move-out cleaners
- short-term-rental cleaning businesses
- post-construction cleaning businesses
- carpet and floor-care businesses
- window-cleaning businesses
- specialty sanitation businesses operating within scope

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business operations.

It does not replace:

- hazardous-material training
- infection-control requirements
- occupational safety rules
- chemical handling requirements
- regulated remediation
- environmental rules
- insurance
- professional safety review

## Guardrails

- hazardous or regulated cleaning must route to qualified review
- chemical and safety requirements must be visible
- access and key security must be protected
- pricing calculations must show assumptions
- quality records must preserve site, date, and responsible team
