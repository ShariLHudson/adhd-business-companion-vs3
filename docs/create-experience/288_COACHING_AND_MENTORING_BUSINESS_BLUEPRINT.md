# 288 — Coaching & Mentoring Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.coaching_mentoring`

## Purpose

Help coaching and mentoring businesses define programs, enroll suitable clients, create reflective and goal-oriented experiences, manage sessions, track commitments, preserve boundaries, and support sustainable client progress.

## Best-fit users

- business coaches
- executive coaches
- leadership coaches
- career coaches
- accountability coaches
- life coaches operating within scope
- mentor programs
- group coaching businesses
- cohort-based coaching programs
- peer-support facilitators

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports coaching business operations and reflective client experiences.

It does not replace:

- therapy
- diagnosis
- medical treatment
- crisis care
- legal advice
- regulated financial advice
- licensed mental-health services
- emergency intervention

## Shari-style experience requirements

The experience should be:

- warm
- reflective
- nonjudgmental
- practical
- client-led
- clear about boundaries
- supportive without becoming dependent or prescriptive

Questions should help the user think rather than rushing to tell them what to do.
