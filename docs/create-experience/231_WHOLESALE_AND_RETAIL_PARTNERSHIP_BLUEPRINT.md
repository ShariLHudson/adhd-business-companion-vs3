# 231 — Wholesale & Retail Partnership Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.wholesale`

## Purpose

Help a product business sell successfully through retailers, distributors, corporate buyers, and other wholesale partners while protecting margin, service quality, inventory, and brand positioning.

## Best-fit users

- product brands entering wholesale
- makers selling to stores
- retail-ready consumer brands
- food and gift brands
- apparel and accessory brands
- publishers and stationery businesses
- corporate gift suppliers
- distributors and representatives
