# 242 — Home Service Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.home_service`

## Purpose

Help home-service businesses create a reliable operating model from inquiry through scheduling, service delivery, payment, follow-up, and recurring maintenance.

## Best-fit users

- cleaning businesses
- lawn and garden services
- handyman services
- pest-control businesses
- HVAC service businesses
- plumbing and electrical service businesses
- appliance services
- pool services
- window cleaning
- home maintenance providers
- specialty residential services
