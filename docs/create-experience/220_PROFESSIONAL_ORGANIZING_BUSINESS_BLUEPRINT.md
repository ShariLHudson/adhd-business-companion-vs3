# 220 — Professional Organizing Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.professional_organizing`

## Purpose

Help a professional organizer build, operate, and grow a credible organizing business that may serve homes, businesses, executives, teams, and organizations.

## Best-fit users

- residential organizers
- business organizers
- productivity organizers
- move and transition organizers
- paper-management organizers
- digital organizers
- executive organizers
- operations organizers
- strategic organizing consultants
- hybrid organizing professionals

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define organizing specialty
- identify ideal clients
- design services
- price work
- qualify engagements
- conduct assessments
- create organizing plans
- manage projects
- handle client resistance and decision fatigue
- protect privacy
- build maintenance services
- generate referrals
- operate sustainably
