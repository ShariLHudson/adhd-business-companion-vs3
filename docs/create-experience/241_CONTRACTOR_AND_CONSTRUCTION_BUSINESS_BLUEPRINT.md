# 241 — Contractor & Construction Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.contractor_construction`

## Purpose

Help contractors and construction businesses create, price, sell, schedule, deliver, document, and review projects with clear scopes, strong client communication, disciplined cost control, and safer operations.

## Best-fit users

- general contractors
- remodelers
- specialty contractors
- builders
- renovation firms
- carpentry businesses
- painting contractors
- flooring businesses
- roofing and exterior contractors
- subcontractor-led firms

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define services and project types
- qualify opportunities
- conduct site assessments
- create scopes and estimates
- calculate labor, material, overhead, contingency, and margin
- prepare proposals
- organize permits and professional-review flags
- schedule crews, subcontractors, and deliveries
- manage changes
- communicate project status
- close out work
- track project and company performance

## Guardrails

- do not provide unverified code, permit, engineering, safety, licensing, or legal conclusions
- clearly flag professional review needs
- preserve inspection and approval records
- do not calculate margin from incomplete cost inputs without showing assumptions

## Createability requirements

Every listed output must have a completed Output Createability Manifest and an actual direct, structured, composed, or connected creation path.
