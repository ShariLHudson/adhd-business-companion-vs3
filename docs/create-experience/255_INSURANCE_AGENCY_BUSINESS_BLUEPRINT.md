# 255 — Insurance Agency Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.insurance_agency`

## Purpose

Help insurance agencies, brokers, and independent producers organize prospecting, needs discovery, quoting, submissions, policy service, renewals, claims support, carrier relationships, compliance, and agency performance.

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports agency operations.

It does not:

- bind coverage
- interpret policy language as legal advice
- guarantee coverage
- replace carrier underwriting
- replace licensed producer judgment
- replace jurisdiction-specific insurance requirements

## Best-fit users

- independent agencies
- captive agencies
- brokers
- benefits agencies
- commercial-lines agencies
- personal-lines agencies
- specialty insurance businesses
- risk-advisory businesses operating within scope
