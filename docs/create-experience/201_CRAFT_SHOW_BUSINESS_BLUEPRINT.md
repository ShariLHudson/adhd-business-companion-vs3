# 201 — Craft Show Business Blueprint

Blueprint ID: business.craft_show

Purpose:
Build a complete operating system for handmade businesses that sell at craft shows, art fairs, festivals, vendor markets, holiday markets, and pop-up events.

This is NOT an Event Blueprint.

It is an ongoing Business Blueprint that can generate linked Event Work items.

Major domains:
- Business vision
- Products
- Inventory
- Pricing
- Booth design
- Show discovery
- Jury applications
- Annual calendar
- Travel
- Packing
- Vehicle loading
- POS (Square/Stripe)
- Customer interactions
- Lead capture
- Email list growth
- Repeat customers
- Expenses
- Profitability
- Taxes
- Vendor documents
- Restocking
- Seasonal planning
- Analytics
- Post-show reviews

Automatically supports creation of linked Event plans for:
- Craft show
- Holiday market
- Vendor fair
- Pop-up shop
- Art festival

Must reuse Universal Work Engine, Projects, Research, Cartography, Chamber, Board, Body Doubling, and Shari.
No separate runtime.
