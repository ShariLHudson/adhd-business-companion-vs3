# 253 — Legal Services Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.legal_services`

## Purpose

Help law firms, solo attorneys, legal consultants, and legal-support businesses organize client intake, matter management, service delivery, communication, billing, knowledge, referrals, and firm operations without replacing jurisdiction-specific professional judgment.

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business operations and workflow design.

It must not:

- provide legal conclusions without qualified review
- create unauthorized legal advice
- override attorney-client privilege rules
- replace conflict checks
- replace jurisdiction-specific professional obligations
- present drafts as final legal documents without attorney approval

## Best-fit users

- solo attorneys
- small law firms
- legal consultants
- mediation practices
- legal document-support businesses operating within lawful scope
- paralegal-led businesses where permitted
- compliance-support businesses
- alternative legal-service providers

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define practice areas and matter types
- qualify inquiries
- organize conflict-check workflows
- create intake and engagement processes
- manage matters and deadlines
- standardize client communication
- track work, billing, and collections
- build templates and knowledge assets
- coordinate referrals and co-counsel
- review firm capacity and profitability

## Guardrails

- all legal content must be clearly marked as draft until attorney-approved
- conflict checks are mandatory where applicable
- confidentiality and privilege must be protected
- local professional rules remain authoritative
- no fabricated citations, authorities, deadlines, or filing requirements

## Createability requirements

Every output must have a real creation path, persistence, editability, exact resume, validation, and truthful availability status.

## Certification requirements

- conflict-check integrity
- matter-level source of truth
- deadline and review controls
- professional-approval gates
- billing and profitability support
- confidentiality
- canonical Universal Work
- optional Project handoff
