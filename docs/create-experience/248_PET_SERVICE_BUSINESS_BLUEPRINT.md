# 248 — Pet Service Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.pet_service`

## Purpose

Help pet-service businesses create safe, organized, trustworthy operations from inquiry and animal intake through scheduling, service delivery, owner communication, incident handling, recurring service, and growth.

## Best-fit users

- pet sitters
- dog walkers
- groomers
- pet daycare businesses
- boarding businesses
- mobile pet services
- pet transport businesses
- non-veterinary pet-care providers
- pet enrichment businesses
- pet-product and service hybrids

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business and non-veterinary care operations.

It does not replace veterinary diagnosis, treatment, emergency care, behavior medicine, licensing, boarding regulations, or animal-control requirements.

## Guardrails

- veterinary concerns must route to qualified care
- emergency instructions must be explicit
- animal welfare and safety take priority
- medication handling must be documented and within lawful scope
- access, keys, home privacy, and owner data must be protected
- local boarding, transport, and animal-care rules require review

## Createability requirements

All outputs require manifests, real creation flows, persistence, editability, exact resume, validation, tests, and truthful status.
