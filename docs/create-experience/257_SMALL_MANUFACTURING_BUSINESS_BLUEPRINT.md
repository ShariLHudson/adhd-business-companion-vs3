# 257 — Small Manufacturing Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.small_manufacturing`

## Purpose

Help small and growing manufacturers design, document, operate, and improve production from product definition and sourcing through scheduling, quality, inventory, fulfillment, maintenance, and profitability.

## Best-fit users

- small manufacturers
- makers scaling into repeatable production
- custom-product manufacturers
- light industrial businesses
- assembly businesses
- contract manufacturers
- private-label producers
- specialty fabricators
- food or consumer-goods producers operating within applicable regulations

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business and operating-system design.

It does not replace:

- engineering review
- occupational safety requirements
- regulated manufacturing standards
- food, pharmaceutical, chemical, or environmental compliance
- product certification
- legal or insurance review
- licensed professional judgment

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define products and production requirements
- create bills of materials
- organize suppliers
- calculate product cost
- plan production
- manage work orders
- create quality checkpoints
- track inventory and waste
- schedule maintenance
- document traceability
- coordinate fulfillment
- review capacity and profitability
