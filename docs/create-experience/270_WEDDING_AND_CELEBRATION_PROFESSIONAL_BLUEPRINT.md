# 270 — Wedding & Celebration Professional Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.wedding_celebration_professional`

## Purpose

Help wedding planners, celebration professionals, coordinators, designers, and related service providers organize clients, budgets, vendors, timelines, logistics, communication, contingency plans, and event delivery.

## Best-fit users

- wedding planners
- day-of coordinators
- celebration planners
- party planners
- event designers
- proposal planners
- destination-event coordinators
- wedding-consulting businesses
- independent celebration professionals

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business and event operations.

It does not replace:

- venue contracts
- legal review
- permits
- insurance
- alcohol-service requirements
- transportation law
- food-safety rules
- crowd safety
- emergency services
- jurisdiction-specific requirements
