# 267 — Rental & Equipment Hire Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.rental_equipment_hire`

## Purpose

Help rental businesses manage rentable assets, availability, pricing, reservations, pickup and delivery, condition, maintenance, deposits, damage, returns, utilization, and profitability.

## Best-fit users

- equipment rental
- party and event rental
- tool rental
- furniture rental
- costume and apparel rental
- recreation rental
- mobility equipment rental operating within scope
- camera and production rental
- vehicle-adjacent rental support businesses
- short-term business asset rental

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business operations.

It does not replace:

- vehicle or transportation licensing
- product safety certification
- medical-device regulations
- insurance requirements
- consumer leasing law
- contract review
- hazardous-equipment training
- professional inspection

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- create an asset catalog
- manage reservations
- price rental periods
- calculate deposits and fees
- inspect assets
- plan pickup or delivery
- track maintenance
- manage damage and loss
- create customer instructions
- monitor utilization and profitability

## Guardrails

- safety, insurance, eligibility, and training requirements must be flagged
- condition records must preserve dates and evidence
- deposit and damage decisions require defined authority
- calculations must expose assumptions
- regulated assets require professional review

## Createability requirements

All outputs require real creation paths, persistence, editability, exact resume, validation, and truthful availability status.

## Certification requirements

- asset-level source of truth
- availability and reservation integrity
- condition history
- reproducible pricing
- maintenance and out-of-service controls
- deposit and damage workflow
- canonical Work
- optional Project handoff
