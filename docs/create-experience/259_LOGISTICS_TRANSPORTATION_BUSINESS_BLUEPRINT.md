# 259 — Logistics & Transportation Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.logistics_transportation`

## Purpose

Help logistics, transportation, delivery, and freight businesses organize customers, loads, routes, drivers, vehicles, documentation, communication, safety, maintenance, billing, and performance.

## Best-fit users

- local delivery businesses
- courier companies
- freight brokers operating within lawful scope
- trucking businesses
- moving businesses
- last-mile delivery companies
- route-based delivery services
- specialty transport businesses
- logistics coordinators
- small fleet operators

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business and operational planning.

It does not replace:

- driver licensing
- vehicle regulations
- hazardous-material requirements
- hours-of-service rules
- customs or freight law
- safety inspections
- insurance requirements
- qualified dispatch, legal, compliance, or transportation judgment

## Guardrails

- no fabricated route, traffic, legal, or regulatory data
- safety and lawful operation take priority over schedule or margin
- compliance requirements must route to qualified review
- cost calculations must expose assumptions
- incident and claims records must preserve provenance

## Createability requirements

Every output requires a real creation path, persistence, editing, exact resume, validation, test coverage, and truthful status.

## Certification requirements

- route and dispatch support
- reproducible cost model
- driver and vehicle controls
- proof-of-delivery and exception handling
- safety and compliance flags
- billing and claims workflow
- canonical Universal Work
- optional Project handoff
