# 269 — Photography & Videography Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.photography_videography`

## Purpose

Help photographers, videographers, production teams, and visual-content businesses design services, qualify clients, price projects, plan shoots, manage assets, deliver finished work, protect usage rights, and grow through repeat business and referrals.

## Best-fit users

- portrait photographers
- wedding photographers
- commercial photographers
- brand photographers
- videographers
- content-production studios
- event photographers
- real-estate photographers
- school and sports photographers
- drone-media businesses operating within lawful scope

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business and production operations.

It does not replace:

- copyright or licensing counsel
- model-release review
- drone or aviation rules
- privacy law
- venue permissions
- insurance requirements
- child-protection requirements
- professional safety review
