# 208 — Coaching Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.coaching`

## Purpose

Help a coach define, build, deliver, and grow a trustworthy coaching business with clear positioning, strong client fit, ethical boundaries, effective delivery, and sustainable operations.

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define coaching focus and boundaries
- identify ideal clients
- design coaching offers
- price and package services
- build discovery and enrollment
- create onboarding and delivery
- track client progress
- manage scope and boundaries
- generate referrals and renewals
- operate the business consistently

## Guardrails

This Blueprint must:

- distinguish coaching from therapy, legal, medical, and regulated professional services
- prohibit unsupported outcome guarantees
- preserve confidentiality
- support referral when outside scope
- keep user and client agency central

## Certification

- ethical boundaries
- client-fit logic
- pricing and capacity
- end-to-end client journey
- progress evidence
- renewal without pressure
- low-energy owner mode
- canonical Work and Project handoff
