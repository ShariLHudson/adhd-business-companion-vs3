# 132A — Product Launch Domain Specification

A successful product launch creates awareness, trust, excitement, conversions, and long-term customer success.

Domains:
- Positioning
- Audience fit
- Messaging
- Demonstration
- Marketing
- Sales
- Customer onboarding
- Support
- Analytics
- Follow-through

Formats:
- Live launch
- Webinar launch
- Hybrid launch
- VIP preview
- Public launch

KPIs:
registrations, attendance, engagement, conversion, revenue, onboarding completion, customer satisfaction, retention.
