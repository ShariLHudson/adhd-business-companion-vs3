# 232 — Subscription Commerce Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.subscription_commerce`

## Purpose

Help a business design, launch, operate, retain, and grow a physical or digital subscription offering with recurring value, reliable fulfillment, healthy economics, and ethical cancellation.

## Best-fit users

- subscription boxes
- replenishment programs
- curated product subscriptions
- digital product subscriptions
- hybrid physical and digital subscriptions
- membership-plus-product businesses
- seasonal subscription programs

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define recurring customer value
- choose subscription model
- build pricing and unit economics
- design acquisition and onboarding
- forecast inventory
- organize recurring fulfillment
- reduce churn
- manage skips, pauses, and cancellations
- improve lifetime value
- protect owner capacity

## Guardrails

- cancellation must be understandable and accessible
- avoid manipulative retention
- preserve clear renewal terms
- protect customer data
- do not scale before fulfillment and economics are stable
