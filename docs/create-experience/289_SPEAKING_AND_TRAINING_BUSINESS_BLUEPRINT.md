# 289 — Speaking & Training Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.speaking_training`

## Purpose

Help speakers, trainers, facilitators, and workshop businesses position expertise, develop talks and programs, book engagements, prepare delivery, manage events, create learning materials, and grow through repeat bookings and intellectual-property reuse.

## Best-fit users

- keynote speakers
- conference speakers
- corporate trainers
- workshop facilitators
- seminar leaders
- professional-development providers
- virtual-event presenters
- panelists and moderators
- speaker-coaches operating within scope
- training firms
