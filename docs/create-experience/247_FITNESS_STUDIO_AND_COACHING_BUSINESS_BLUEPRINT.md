# 247 — Fitness Studio & Coaching Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.fitness_studio_coaching`

## Purpose

Help fitness professionals and studios create a sustainable business with clear programs, safe onboarding, scheduling, pricing, client progress, retention, team operations, and scope boundaries.

## Best-fit users

- personal trainers
- fitness coaches
- group fitness studios
- yoga studios
- Pilates studios
- strength and conditioning businesses
- online fitness businesses
- small gyms
- mobility and movement educators operating within scope

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business operations and general program organization.

It does not provide medical diagnosis, rehabilitation, emergency care, or individualized treatment outside the professional’s qualifications and lawful scope.
