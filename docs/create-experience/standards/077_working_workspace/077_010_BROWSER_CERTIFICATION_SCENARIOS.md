# 077 — Browser Certification Scenarios

## Required Scenario 1 — Open Not Started

1. Open an Event.
2. Open Full Workshop Map.
3. Click Purpose — Not Started.
4. Confirm Purpose editor opens.
5. Enter content.
6. Save.
7. Return to map.
8. Confirm status is In Progress.

## Required Scenario 2 — Open Complete

1. Click Event Type — Complete.
2. Confirm section opens.
3. Confirm content is editable.
4. Change content.
5. Save.
6. Confirm member can keep complete or reopen.

## Required Scenario 3 — Give Me Ideas

1. Open Audience.
2. Click Give Me Ideas.
3. Confirm ideas are audience-specific.
4. Insert one.
5. Save.
6. Refresh browser.
7. Confirm inserted content remains.

## Required Scenario 4 — I’m Not Sure

1. Open Purpose.
2. Click I’m Not Sure.
3. Confirm explanation and options appear.
4. Choose a recommended option.
5. Confirm it inserts into Purpose.

## Required Scenario 5 — Leave and Return

1. Edit Budget.
2. Navigate to the map.
3. Open another section.
4. Return to Budget.
5. Confirm latest content remains.

## Required Scenario 6 — Reopen Completed Work

1. Mark Audience complete.
2. Leave work.
3. Return later.
4. Open Audience.
5. Confirm full edit access.
6. Reopen.
7. Confirm status changes to In Progress.

## Required Scenario 7 — Project Link

1. Turn Marketing section into Project tasks.
2. Open linked task in Projects.
3. Open source.
4. Confirm exact Marketing section opens.

## Required Scenario 8 — Save Failure

1. Simulate persistence failure.
2. Edit section.
3. Save.
4. Confirm platform does not show “Saved.”
5. Confirm recovery copy remains.
6. Retry successfully.

## Release Gate

No workspace is release-ready until all scenarios pass in a real browser.
