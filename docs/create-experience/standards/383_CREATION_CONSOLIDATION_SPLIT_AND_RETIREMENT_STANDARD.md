# 383 — Creation Consolidation, Split & Retirement Standard

## Purpose

Keep the user's body of work understandable and maintainable.

## Consolidation candidates

Recommend review when:

- several artifacts serve the same audience and moment
- users repeatedly choose among nearly identical files
- the same facts are maintained in several places
- one connected set would improve use
- adaptations have drifted from the source

## Split candidates

Recommend separation when:

- one artifact serves conflicting audiences
- sections need independent updating
- different parts have different distribution paths
- a long guide hides a frequently used checklist
- professional-review material should be isolated

## Retirement candidates

May include:

- obsolete versions
- replaced materials
- unused near-duplicates
- stale adaptations
- abandoned drafts
- artifacts tied to ended offers or processes

## Safe behavior

The system may recommend:

- keep
- merge
- split
- archive
- replace
- mark obsolete
- delete

It must not take destructive action without clear authorization.

## Preservation

Retain lineage and provenance when consolidation or replacement occurs.
