# 290 — Membership & Community Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.membership_community`

## Purpose

Help paid and free communities, membership businesses, associations, networks, mastermind groups, and recurring-support communities design value, onboard members, create engagement, run programs, support belonging, manage retention, and measure community health.

## Best-fit users

- paid membership communities
- professional associations
- mastermind groups
- peer networks
- learning communities
- creator communities
- alumni communities
- business communities
- accountability communities
- subscription-based support groups operating within scope

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports community and membership business operations.

It does not replace:

- crisis care
- therapy
- legal review
- safeguarding requirements
- nonprofit governance
- membership association law
- regulated professional supervision
- emergency response
