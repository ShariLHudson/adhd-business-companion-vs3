# 222 — Digital & Information Organization Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`organizing.digital_information`

## Purpose

Help a user create an understandable, searchable, maintainable structure for digital files, email, documents, notes, photos, research, and business information.

## Applies to

- local files
- cloud drives
- shared drives
- email
- notes
- photos
- research
- client documents
- business records
- templates
- intellectual property
- archives

## Core sections

- who needs it

- sensitivity

- current frustrations

- Information Inventory

- Risk Flags

## Guardrails

- do not delete without confirmation
- preserve backups before migration
- flag regulated retention decisions
- protect credentials and sensitive information
- preserve provenance and version history where needed
