# 592 — Content, Engagement & Programming Engine

## Content pillars
Choose three to five from education, discussion, implementation, support, inspiration, connection, accountability, resources, celebration, expertise, feedback, events, behind the scenes, and opportunities.

## Formats
Weekly question, office hours, live teaching, implementation session, spotlight, case discussion, poll, resource drop, AMA, challenge, co-working, wins thread, feedback thread, curated discussion, theme week, expert session, or community project.

## Healthy mix
Founder-led value, member discussion, connection, applied action, recognition, listening, and invitations to contribute.

## Engagement hierarchy
Reactions and generic comments are weak signals. Stronger signals include useful replies, peer support, shared experience, implementation, member-to-member connection, trust, collaboration, progress, and advocacy.

## Calendar options
30-day launch calendar, weekly rhythm, monthly theme, quarterly arc, event-centered sequence, campaign-centered sequence, or low-capacity plan.

## Low-capacity example
- Monday: focus question
- Wednesday: resource or teaching
- Friday: wins and reflection

## Anti-burnout rules
Do not require daily founder posts. Reuse strong discussions, invite member leadership, batch assets, use repeatable formats, stop low-value formats, and protect response capacity.

## Output
Pillars, programming menu, weekly rhythm, 30-day calendar, first posts, engagement prompts, events, rituals, member-led plan, and reuse system.
