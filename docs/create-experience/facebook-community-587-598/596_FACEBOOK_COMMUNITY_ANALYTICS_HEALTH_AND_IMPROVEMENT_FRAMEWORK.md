# 596 — Analytics, Health & Improvement Framework

## Measurement layers
**Growth:** discovery, invitations, requests, approvals, member growth, source.

**Activation:** welcome, introduction, first comment/post/event/useful interaction.

**Engagement:** active members, contributors, comments, discussion depth, events, member-to-member interaction.

**Retention:** return participation, quiet members, drop-off, repeat attendance, reasons for leaving.

**Value:** progress, problems solved, useful connections, resources used, stories, referrals, advocacy.

**Safety:** spam, reports, moderation, conflict, response time, unanswered posts, founder/moderator burden.

## Safeguards
Do not confuse member count with health, reactions with transformation, posting frequency with value, silence with dissatisfaction, or founder activity with belonging.

## Platform insights
Use Facebook Group Insights where available for activity and engagement. Some metrics depend on group size, device, or feature availability; never assume all insights exist.

## Monthly review
Ask whether purpose is clear, right people join, new members activate, members help one another, formats create value, questions go unanswered, moderation is sustainable, and what should start, stop, continue, or simplify.

## Health signals
Healthy: relevance, trust, peer support, contribution, repeat engagement, manageable moderation, clear value.

Needs attention: declining participation, unanswered posts, promotion, founder-only conversation, conflict, poor fit, unclear purpose, moderator fatigue.

## Output
Measurement plan, dashboard specification, monthly review, experiment tracker, member survey, health scorecard, improvement backlog, and quarterly review.
