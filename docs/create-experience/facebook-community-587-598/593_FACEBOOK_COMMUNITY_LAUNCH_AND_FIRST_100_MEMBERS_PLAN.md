# 593 — Launch & First 100 Members Plan

## Stage 1 — Seed
Invite trusted, relevant people who can establish the tone. Create a seed list, personal invitation, expectations, early prompts, and feedback request.

## Stage 2 — Soft launch
Invite current/past clients, subscribers, partners, event attendees, followers, peers, and program participants.

## Stage 3 — Public launch
Use email, Facebook profile/Page, Instagram, LinkedIn, website, podcast, webinar, lead magnet, collaboration, speaking, and client onboarding.

## Milestones
**First 10:** relevance, listening, trust, welcome testing.

**First 25:** recurring participation, expectations, introductions, one useful event.

**First 50:** member connection, content rhythm, moderation, contribution.

**First 100:** scalable welcome, retention, member leaders, measurement, operating systems.

## Invitation principles
Invite for member value, explain fit, do not add people without consent, avoid deceptive scarcity and spam, and make declining easy.

## Launch assets
Founding-member invitation, personal DM, email, social announcement, launch-week content, FAQ, welcome video, referral invitation, partner outreach, thank-you.

## Measurement
Invitations, acceptance, approvals, welcome completion, introductions, participation, event attendance, feedback, referrals, and early retention.

## Output
30-Day Community Launch Plan with timeline, channels, assets, roles, metrics, risks, and fallback plan.
