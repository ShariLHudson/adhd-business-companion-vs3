# 243 — Property Management Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.property_management`

## Purpose

Help property-management businesses organize owner relationships, property onboarding, resident or tenant service, maintenance coordination, vendor management, inspections, financial visibility, and portfolio growth.

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business operations and general organization.

It must not replace jurisdiction-specific legal, leasing, fair-housing, tax, accounting, insurance, or regulatory advice.

## Best-fit users

- residential property managers
- small commercial property managers
- short-term rental managers
- association managers
- independent managers
- owners building an internal management operation

## Core sections and creatable outputs

### 1. Management Model

Outputs:

- Property Management Business Snapshot
- Service Portfolio
- Property-Type Fit Profile
- Management Fee Model
- Scope and Exclusions

### 2. Owner Acquisition and Onboarding

Outputs:

- Owner Qualification Guide
- Property Intake Form
- Management Proposal

- Authorization and Document Checklist

### 3. Property Onboarding

Outputs:
