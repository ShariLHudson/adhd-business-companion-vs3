# 258 — Wholesale & Distribution Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.wholesale_distribution`

## Purpose

Help wholesale and distribution businesses manage product lines, suppliers, purchasing, inventory, customer accounts, pricing, fulfillment, returns, sales performance, and working capital.

## Best-fit users

- wholesalers
- distributors
- importers
- specialty product distributors
- regional distributors
- business-to-business product sellers
- multi-brand distributors
- warehouse-based product businesses
- manufacturers with wholesale channels

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define territories and channels
- organize product catalogs
- create wholesale pricing
- qualify accounts
- manage purchasing
- plan stock
- create order workflows
- coordinate warehouse operations
- manage returns and credits
- track sales, margin, and inventory
- improve supplier and customer performance

## Guardrails

- pricing and margin must show assumptions
- credit and terms decisions require authorized review
- import, customs, tax, labeling, and product rules require professional verification
- no fabricated supplier, freight, regulatory, or product information
- inventory calculations must be reproducible

## Createability requirements
