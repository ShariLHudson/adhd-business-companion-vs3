# 244 — Mobile & Field Service Operations Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`operations.mobile_field_service`

## Purpose

Help any business delivering work at customer, job-site, community, or remote locations organize dispatch, routing, field execution, documentation, communication, safety, completion, and performance.

## Best-fit users

- mobile service businesses
- repair businesses
- inspectors
- installers
- delivery and setup teams
- mobile pet services
- mobile beauty services
- event setup crews
- technicians
- multi-stop service teams

## Core sections and creatable outputs

### 1. Field-Service Model

Outputs:

- Field Service Profile
- Service Territory
- Job-Type Library
- Standard Duration and Resource Assumptions
- Field-Service Rules

### 2. Job Intake

Outputs:

- Job Intake Form
- Job Readiness Checklist
- Access and Contact Record
- Required Tools and Materials List
- Risk Flags

### 3. Scheduling and Dispatch

Outputs:

- Dispatch Board Structure
- Daily Route
- Technician Assignment
- Priority Rules
- Buffer and Emergency Capacity Plan
- Rescheduling Workflow

### 4. Pre-Visit Preparation

Outputs:

- Customer Preparation Message
- Technician Job Brief
- Equipment Checklist
- Parts and Supply Checklist
- Access Confirmation
- Weather or Site Risk Check

### 5. Arrival and Check-In

Outputs:

- Arrival Procedure
- Identity and Permission Check
- Site Condition Record
- Customer Confirmation
- Work Authorization Record

### 6. Field Execution

Outputs:

- Job Checklist
- Diagnostic Record
- Work Performed Record
- Material Usage Log
- Photo Documentation Plan
- Exception and Approval Request

### 7. Communication

Outputs:

- On-the-Way Message
- Delay Notice
- Additional-Work Approval
- Completion Summary
- Follow-Up Message
- Escalation Communication

### 8. Completion and Verification

Outputs:

- Completion Checklist
- Customer Acceptance
- Payment or Invoice Trigger
- Warranty or Care Instructions
- Return-Visit Requirement
- Job Closeout Record

### 9. Safety and Incident Response

Outputs:

- Field Safety Checklist
- Stop-Work Conditions
- Incident Report
- Emergency Communication Plan
- Equipment Damage Report
- Professional-Review Flags

### 10. Vehicle, Equipment, and Stock

Outputs:

- Vehicle Inventory
- Tool Assignment Register
- Reorder Points
- Maintenance Calendar
- Loss and Damage Log
- Daily Restock Checklist

### 11. Team Performance and Training

Outputs:

- Technician Role Profile
- Skills Matrix
- Training Plan
- Quality Scorecard
- Coaching Record
- Backup Coverage Map

### 12. Analytics and Improvement

Track:

- jobs completed
- first-time completion
- travel time
- on-time arrival
- job duration
- repeat visits
- material usage
- revenue per route
- customer satisfaction
- technician capacity

Outputs:

- Field Operations Dashboard
- Route Efficiency Review
- Repeat-Visit Analysis
- Monthly Improvement Plan

## Required contributors

- Operations
- Execution

All outputs require manifest entries, working creation paths, persistence, editability, exact resume, and truthful availability states.
