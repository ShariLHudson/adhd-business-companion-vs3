# 268 — Dealer, Reseller & Channel Partner Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.dealer_reseller_channel_partner`

## Purpose

Help dealers, resellers, representatives, distributors, affiliates, and channel-led businesses manage products, partners, territories, pricing, leads, opportunities, enablement, orders, incentives, support, and channel performance.

## Best-fit users

- authorized dealers
- value-added resellers
- technology resellers
- product representatives
- channel partners
- referral partners
- affiliates
- manufacturer representatives
- equipment dealers
- service-channel businesses

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business and channel operations.

It does not replace:

- competition law
- franchise law
- distribution agreements
- pricing law
- territory law
- licensing
- product certification
- contract review
- tax and regulatory advice

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define channel strategy
- qualify partners
- create partner tiers
- organize onboarding
- manage products and pricing
- route leads
- create opportunity and deal-registration workflows
- deliver training and assets
- track incentives
- support customers
- review channel performance

## Guardrails

- channel pricing and territory rules require professional review
- do not fabricate authorization or certification
- preserve lead and deal ownership history
- incentives must be calculated reproducibly
- customer and partner data must be protected
- unavailable integrations must be labeled honestly

## Createability requirements

Every output must have a Createability Manifest entry and a real creation, persistence, approval, resume, and validation path.

## Certification requirements

- partner data and status integrity
- lead and deal-registration controls
- pricing and approval workflow
- training and certification support
- incentive calculation
- partner performance reporting
- canonical Universal Work
- intentional Project handoff
