# 254 — Accounting, Bookkeeping & Tax Practice Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.accounting_bookkeeping_tax`

## Purpose

Help accounting, bookkeeping, payroll, tax-preparation, and financial-administration practices organize client onboarding, recurring work, document collection, reconciliations, reviews, deadlines, communication, billing, and practice growth.

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports practice operations.

It does not replace:

- licensed accounting judgment
- tax advice
- audit standards
- payroll compliance
- financial reporting standards
- jurisdiction-specific filing rules
- professional review

## Best-fit users

- bookkeepers
- accounting firms
- tax-preparation businesses
- payroll service businesses
- virtual CFO practices
- financial administration firms
- client accounting service firms
