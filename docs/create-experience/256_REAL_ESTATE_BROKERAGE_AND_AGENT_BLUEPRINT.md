# 256 — Real Estate Brokerage & Agent Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.real_estate_brokerage_agent`

## Purpose

Help real estate agents, brokers, teams, and transaction-support businesses organize prospecting, client discovery, property preparation, listings, buyer journeys, offers, transactions, follow-up, referrals, and business performance.

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business operations.

It does not replace:

- licensed real estate judgment
- legal advice
- appraisal
- inspection
- lending
- title work
- fair-housing obligations
- jurisdiction-specific forms or disclosures

## Best-fit users

- residential agents
- commercial agents
- brokers
- real estate teams
- transaction coordinators operating within lawful scope
- relocation specialists
- investor-focused agents
- listing and buyer specialists
