# 287 — Consulting & Advisory Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.consulting_advisory`

## Purpose

Help consultants, advisors, strategists, fractional leaders, and expert service firms create clear offers, qualify engagements, diagnose client needs, design recommendations, manage delivery, communicate decisions, and build repeatable intellectual property.

## Best-fit users

- independent consultants
- strategy firms
- operational consultants
- organizational consultants
- marketing consultants
- technology advisors
- fractional executives
- business advisors
- niche expert firms
- research-led advisory businesses

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business and advisory operations.

It does not replace:

- legal advice
- regulated financial advice
- medical advice
- licensed engineering judgment
- tax advice
- professional certification requirements
- client executive authority
- domain-specific compliance review

## Profile-context requirements

Automatically load and apply:

- active business
- consulting specialty
- selected client avatar
- selected offer
- brand voice
- business values
- existing frameworks
- relevant prior client-safe intellectual property
- current Create or Project context

Do not expose one client’s confidential information to another client or use restricted client material as reusable IP.

## Createability requirements

Every output must have a real creation path, authoritative save destination, editability, exact resume, provenance, review gates, tests, and truthful availability status.

## Certification requirements

- client-context isolation
- evidence and assumption integrity

- recommendation traceability

- exact resume
