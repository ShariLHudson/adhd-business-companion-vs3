# 272 — Automotive Repair & Detailing Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.automotive_repair_detailing`

## Purpose

Help automotive repair, maintenance, detailing, and specialty vehicle-service businesses organize customers, vehicles, inspections, estimates, approvals, work orders, parts, scheduling, quality, communication, and profitability.

## Best-fit users

- independent repair shops
- mobile mechanics operating within lawful scope
- automotive detailing businesses
- tire and maintenance shops
- body and cosmetic repair businesses
- fleet-service businesses
- specialty vehicle services
- motorcycle or recreational-vehicle service businesses where applicable

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business and operating workflows.

It does not replace:

- licensed mechanic judgment
- vehicle safety inspection requirements
- emissions or environmental rules
- hazardous-material requirements
- manufacturer procedures
- warranty administration
- insurance estimating
- legal or regulatory review

## Guardrails

- vehicle safety takes priority over schedule or margin
- qualified inspection and repair judgment remains authoritative
- no fabricated specifications, procedures, parts, or safety conclusions
- customer authorization must be preserved
- estimates and pricing must expose assumptions
- vehicle condition and damage records require provenance

## Createability requirements

All outputs require real creation paths, authoritative persistence, editing, exact resume, validation, test coverage, and truthful status.

## Certification requirements

- customer and vehicle source-of-truth integrity
- estimate reproducibility
- approval controls
- work order and parts flow
- quality and release checks
- safety and environmental flags
- canonical Universal Work
- Project handoff
