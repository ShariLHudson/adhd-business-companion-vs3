# 246 — Beauty & Personal Care Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.beauty_personal_care`

## Purpose

Help beauty and personal-care businesses design profitable services, deliver consistent client experiences, manage scheduling and supplies, maintain professional boundaries, and grow through retention and referrals.

## Best-fit users

- salons
- independent stylists
- barbers
- nail businesses
- esthetic businesses
- makeup artists
- lash and brow businesses
- mobile beauty providers
- spas operating within non-medical scope
- beauty studios

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business and service operations.

It does not replace licensing, sanitation, dermatology, medical-esthetics, occupational safety, or jurisdiction-specific professional requirements.

## Guardrails

- flag licensing, sanitation, and medical-esthetics requirements
- do not create unsupported medical claims
- require consent for images and testimonials
- preserve client privacy
- clearly route reactions or emergencies to appropriate care

## Createability requirements
