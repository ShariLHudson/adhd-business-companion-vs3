# 211 — Author Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.author`

## Purpose

Help an author build a sustainable author business around books, readers, intellectual property, speaking, products, services, partnerships, and long-term platform growth.

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define their author identity and publishing goals
- choose a publishing path
- build a book portfolio
- understand audience and reader segments
- develop an author platform
- create book marketing systems
- connect books to speaking, coaching, consulting, courses, memberships, or licensing
- manage launch and post-launch operations
- track revenue, reach, and strategic value
- create repeatable author-business rhythms

## Best-fit users

- first-time authors
- multi-book authors
- independently published authors
- traditionally published authors
- hybrid authors
- experts writing authority-building books
- speakers, coaches, and consultants adding books
- fiction and nonfiction authors

## Business DNA adaptation

Adapt for:

- fiction versus nonfiction
- first book versus established catalog
- independent versus traditional publishing
- book as primary product versus authority asset
- local versus international audience
- print, ebook, audiobook, workbook, or companion products
- solo author versus team-supported author
