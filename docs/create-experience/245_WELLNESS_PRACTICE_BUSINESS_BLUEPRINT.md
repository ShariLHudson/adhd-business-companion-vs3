# 245 — Wellness Practice Business Blueprint

## Blueprint ID

`business.wellness_practice`

## Purpose

Help a non-emergency wellness practice build, operate, and grow a trustworthy business with clear scope, client intake, service design, pricing, scheduling, documentation, follow-up, referrals, and professional boundaries.

## Best-fit users

- holistic wellness practitioners
- massage practices
- meditation and mindfulness businesses
- breathwork facilitators
- wellness educators
- health coaches operating within lawful scope
- non-clinical lifestyle support businesses
- integrative wellness studios
- wellness retreat providers

## Important boundary

This Blueprint supports business design and operations.

It does not replace medical, mental-health, legal, diagnostic, prescribing, emergency, or regulated clinical judgment.

## Primary outcomes

The user can:

- define scope and services
- identify ideal clients
- create ethical offers
- price and package services
- organize intake and consent
- schedule and deliver sessions
- document client goals and follow-up
- recognize referral or escalation needs
- create retention without dependency
- manage risk, privacy, and records
- track business performance

## Guardrails

- no diagnosis, treatment, prescribing, or emergency advice
- no unsupported health claims
- no guaranteed outcomes
- clear referral to licensed care when needed
- informed consent and privacy must be visible
- regulated documents require professional review
