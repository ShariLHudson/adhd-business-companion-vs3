## Purpose

The Planning Table‚Ñ¢ is where **one day becomes manageable** ‚Äî not where life is optimized, quarterly-planned, or guilt-scheduled.

## Signature Feature‚Ñ¢

### The handcrafted planning desk

One hero only. A **handcrafted planning surface** ‚Äî honey-toned wood, honest joinery, built for handwriting and human scale. Not a SaaS calendar. Not a glass standing desk.

The desk holds an **open leather planner** at center like a gentle contract with today. Pen ready. Pages that expect imperfection. The craftsmanship says: *someone made space for your day on purpose*.

The analog clock on the wall may exist, but it is **not** the hero ‚Äî soft tick or silent, never countdown urgency.

## Companion Presence‚Ñ¢

**Nearby ‚Äî not hovering.**

Shari works **alongside** planning, not above it. She helps shrink the list, name the one next thing, and refuse fake urgency. She does not lecture time management.

Warmth within arm's reach. Questions rare. Structure offered, never imposed.

**Why not Host:** Planning is co-thinking, not greeting.

**Why not Absent:** Complete absence would feel abandoned on hard overwhelm days.
