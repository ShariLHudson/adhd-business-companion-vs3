## Purpose

The Living Room™ is the **heart of the homestead** — where guests arrive, exhale, and remember they are not alone in the work.

It exists to hold **relationship before tools**. Chat lives here. Arrival lives here. The first conversation happens here before any workspace opens.

**Emotional need served:** belonging, safety, being welcomed without performing readiness.

**ADHD challenge reduced:** isolation shame, cold-start anxiety, the feeling that you must already know what you need before you are allowed to ask.

This is not a dashboard. It is Shari's Iowa farmhouse living room — generous windows, honest clutter, warmth that does not interrogate.

---

## Emotional Promise™

**When someone leaves this room…** they feel **steadier** — less alone with whatever they carried in, grounded enough to face the next hour without being pushed into a tool.

---

## Signature Feature™

### The welcoming fireplace

One hero only. A **stone fireplace** at the room's emotional center — birch logs in a basket, optional flame by season, never required to feel welcome.

The fireplace says: *you can stay*. Heat without hustle. Light without a deadline. In winter it burns; in summer it holds flowers in the hearth — the architecture of welcome stays, the fire adapts.

Nothing else competes for hero status — not the TV, not a feature grid, not Kinsey on the mantel performing for the camera.

## Lighting

| Condition | Character |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Morning** | Full window light across cream plaster and braided rug; fireplace cool, honest, present |
| **Afternoon** | Sun pooled on the deep sofa; oak shadows move on the floor |
| **Golden hour** | Room turns amber — white trim honey, fields glow through glass; fireplace mantle catches last sun |
| **Evening** | Lamps with linen shades; fireplace lit when season allows; windows go dark mirror |
| **Rain** | Curtains partway, rain on glass, lamp on — cocoon with weather visible |
| **Snow** | Frost at window corners; fire encouraged; lamplight and ember warmth dominate |
| **Cloudy** | Soft even fill from tall windows; lamps supplement without drama — gray Iowa sky honest, not gloomy |

**Rule:** Every condition is photographable. No impossible sun-and-blizzard combinations. No neon productivity glare.

## Companion Presence™

**Present — Host level.**

The Living Room™ is where Shari **may lead with warmth** because being welcomed is the work. She sets the mug down when she hears the screen door. Her voice can arrive before the guest finds theirs — but still **one invitation, one next thing**.

She is not a tour guide. She is not a feature narrator. She is someone who was already here and is glad you came.

**Why not Nearby or Absent:** Arrival requires a host. Invisible Shari in the living room would feel like an empty rental, not a homestead.

---

## Kinsey™

**Greeting — occasionally present.**

Kinsey belongs to this house. She may cross the rug, sigh on the sofa, or investigate a guest's bag — then settle. She is **not** the signature feature and **not** a mascot animation loop.

- **Sleeping:** Curled on the braided rug or by the fireplace — breathing adds domestic truth
- **Greeting:** Tail thump, sniff, return to nap — one beat, not a performance
- **Not in this room:** When the guest needs deep focus elsewhere — Kinsey stays off-camera

Never guilt the guest for Kinsey's absence. Never use Kinsey to manufacture charm.

## UI Language™

Software disappears into the room. The guest should feel they sat down on the sofa, not opened an app.

| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| **Cards** | Paper-warm — cream surface, soft shadow, rounded corners, barn-red or wheat border tint. Minimal at arrival. |
| **Buttons** | Warm solid fills or soft outlines — never neon productivity UI |
| **Spacing** | Generous — breathing room like the sofa; never a wall of six equal tiles |
| **Overlay** | Soft warm gradient behind copy — **never raw text on busy photography** |
| **Typography** | Legible serif/sans pairing; conversation text feels spoken, not ticketed |
| **Glass** | Avoid glass-on-photo for working layers — frost the world behind copy when needed |
| **Interaction** | One primary invitation; conversation lower third or sidebar chip; House Map and Toolbelt stable but visually deferential at arrival |

**Hero environment:** Up to ~95% immersion permitted **only here** for full arrival — the one room where the photograph may nearly fill the frame until the guest chooses to move.

## Never In This Room™

- Feature mall grids ("six doors to six tools")
- Streak counters, guilt copy, absence shame
- Dashboard KPIs, progress bars, engagement scores
- Auto-opening workspaces or surprise routing
- Clinical diagnostic tone from task language
- Cartoon emoji as feature identity
- Multiple competing heroes (fireplace + spinning widget + celebration banner)
- Corporate office aesthetics, glass towers, coastal resort cosplay
- Kinsey or Shari performing constantly for attention

## ADHD Rules™

| Challenge | How the room helps |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Executive function** | One invitation at a time — no parallel asks on arrival |
| **Working memory** | Conversation holds context; guest does not need to remember where they were |
| **Overwhelm** | Sofa-scale space — no dense control surface at entry |
| **Decision fatigue** | Shari offers one door confidently; silence and "not today" are complete |
| **Attention** | Fireplace and window give soft anchoring without demanding focus |
| **Emotional regulation** | Warmth first — regulate before planning or producing |

---

## Seasonal Notes™

Life changes; architecture does not.

- **Spring:** Windows openable; lighter throws; pale green through glass; hearth may hold flowers not fire
- **Summer:** Fan slow; curtains billow; late gold lasting; fire optional off
- **Autumn:** Throws multiply; amber light slants across the rug; fire more likely
- **Winter:** Fire encouraged; lamps by four; frost on window corners; room contracts gently around warmth

No seasonal "events" or redesign skins — only light, objects, and temperature shifting honestly.
