## Purpose

Peaceful place to **build** what the member is imagining — workshops, content, offers, emails, plans. Shari **facilitates**; the member owns the outcome.

Not an instant content generator.

---

## Transformation

| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| Blank page paralysis | Structured draft in progress |
| Vague idea | Named sections with member's words |
| Fear of "wrong" first draft | Working draft + review when ready |

---

## Emotional feeling

Creative safety · momentum without pressure · partnership · drafts welcome

Never: finished-for-you surprise · homework pile · tool-hopping

---

## Primary experiences

- Conversation-first facilitation (explore before split)
- Split workspace when clear build intent **or** permission granted
- Section-by-section build (Audience, Problem, Promise, Price, …)
- *"Show me what we have so far"* artifact summary
- Review gate before export / print / finalize

---

## What members do here

- Explore intent in chat when uncertain
- Answer one facilitation question at a time
- Edit sections; skip, pause, revise
- Review when ready; export with permission

---

## What members create here

Workshops · newsletters · offers · sales pages · SOPs · content plans · emails · presentations — **tangible business artifacts** tracked in artifact state.

---

## When Estate Intelligence should recommend this room

**High:** clear create/build/write intent — workshop, newsletter, offer, launch content

**Blocked / stay in chat:** brainstorming only (*"I might want to…"*) — facilitated gate

**After Institute:** Business Lab™ output may continue here

**Invitation:** "The Creative Studio™ is the perfect place… Would you like me to take us there?"

---

## Conversation examples
