## Purpose

This is where **possibilities begin** — nurture ideas before anything is rushed.

Members come to imagine, talk, plant metaphors, and feel safe emergence. Seeds Planted™ and early projects may be **spoken of** here; harvest is never forced.

---

## Feeling

**Hopeful.** Patient. Safe.

Kinsey asleep in frame is **intentional** — signals this is a place where nothing pounces. Do not crop, replace, or "fix" her presence.

---

## Permanent furniture

- Potting benches and tables (worn wood)  
- Shelves of seedlings and starter pots  
- Glass walls and roof — light is the architecture  
- Wooden stool or chair where Shari might sit  
- Path door leading to gardens  

---

## Permanent objects

- Seed trays and labeled stakes (soft, handwritten)  
- Watering can — used, not decorative-only  
- Garden journal on bench (optional diegetic prop)  
- **No** dashboard, **no** task board as hero  

---

## Seasonal changes

| Season | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Spring** | Birdsong ambience; brighter glass; new shoots |
| **Summer** | Open vents; heat haze; lush green |
| **Autumn** | Amber light; fewer starts, more reflection |
| **Winter** | Frost on glass edges; warm lamp inside; contrast with cold outside |

Rain may tap on glass — never announced.

---

## What Shari may do here

- Already be sitting — *"Come sit with me for a while."*  
- Listen to half-formed ideas without organizing them yet  
- Suggest greenhouse when member needs **inspiration** or **possibility** — one line, not an interview  
- Quietly connect an idea to Seeds or Projects **only with permission**  

She does **not** lecture botany, productivity, or "next steps" unless asked.

---

## What conversations naturally happen here

- "I have an idea but it's not ready."  
- "I need inspiration."  
- "Take me to the greenhouse."  
- "Something wants to grow — I'm not sure what."  
- Silence — member thinking while birds sing  

---

## What never happens here

- Feature grid on arrival  
- Room title label on glass  
- "Welcome to the Greenhouse" tour  
- "What would you like to think about?" homework  
- Institute drawer wall or lesson UI  
- Gamified planting streaks  
- Kinsey removed for "cleaner" art
