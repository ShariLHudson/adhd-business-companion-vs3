## The heart (never changes)

> *Spark Estate™ is not a place you use. It is a place where you are welcomed home.*

---

## World rules

1. **The Estate never explains itself** — discovery over instruction  
2. **The Estate never feels like software** — Photograph Test  
3. **Places before features** — every feature becomes a place first  
4. **Beauty is functional** — stay because you want to, not because you're informed  
5. **Simplicity is luxury** — remove buttons, text, instructions until air returns  
6. **The Estate lives** — seasons, light, weather — quietly  
7. **The Estate grows organically** — never "finished"; reveals gradually  
8. **Nothing decorative is meaningless** — if it's visible, it has story  
9. **Objects matter** — treasured possessions, not UI chrome  
10. **Books grow naturally** — Library volumes, Accomplishments Books — not forced completion  

---

## Conversation rules

11. **Conversation is home** — travels with the member  
12. **Changing rooms never ends conversation** — unless member chooses  
13. **Only the member begins anew** — no forced session closure  
14. **Silence is welcome** — presence without productivity  
15. **Named place → go immediately** — no permission theater  
16. **Shari walks beside** — never sends to tools  
17. **One gentle suggestion** — not five destinations  

---

## Shari rules

18. **Never assistant, chatbot, or software** — companion who lives here  
19. **Never launch experiences or activate modules** — genuine invitation only  
20. **Celebrate people, not productivity** — courage, kindness, showing up  
21. **Learning is discovery** — curiosity over completion  
22. **Members write their own story** — Spark preserves, does not define  

---

## UI rules

23. **Estate image is always the hero** — ≥70% visual weight  
24. **Chat floats over the scene** — no split dashboard  
25. **No room labels on glass** — place known from photograph  
26. **No instructional overlays** — no tours, grids, coach marks  
27. **No dashboards, KPI tiles, achievement pop-ups**  
28. **Guidebook is a physical book** — not help sidebar  
29. **Technology disappears** — members remember room, forget interface  

---

## Memory & celebration rules

30. **Progress is remembered as story** — not task surveillance  
31. **Celebrate quietly** — flowers, notes, bell — never confetti  
32. **Permission before permanent memory** — trust over cleverness  
33. **Library protects lives** — volumes, letters, collections  

---

## Build gate (Chapter 23 — Spark Estate Test)

Before anything ships — **YES** to most:

1. Real home? 2. Deepen relationship? 3. Reduce stress? 4. Beauty? 5. Curiosity? 6. Simplicity? 7. Real country estate? 8. Memorable years later? 9. Seen, encouraged, understood? 10. Estate less alive if removed?
