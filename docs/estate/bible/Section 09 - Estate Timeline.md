## Time of day

| Phase | Estate character | Examples |
|-------|------------------|----------|
| **Morning** | Fresh light, birds, steam | Greenhouse bright; sunroom welcome; coffee appears |
| **Afternoon** | Full warmth, work in pockets | Studio light; institute halls |
| **Evening** | Glow, hearth, slower speech | Back deck; porch swing; lanterns |
| **Night** | Quiet, lamps, snow or crickets beyond glass | Library nook; gentle closure without "session end" |

**Never:** "Good morning!" system banners. Changes are **seen**, not declared.

---

## Spring

- Blossoms, birdsong (greenhouse ambience)  
- Orchard buds, path mud drying  
- Open greenhouse vents  
- Invitation to plant ideas — metaphor and real scene  

---

## Summer

- Curtains move in breeze  
- Windows open; dock and porch primary  
- Apple orchard shade  
- Lighter conversation pace  

---

## Fall

- Warm blankets, candles  
- Pumpkins, apple cider (coffee house)  
- Orchard harvest tone  
- Reading nook and tea room affinity  

---

## Winter

- Wreaths, evergreen garlands, white lights  
- Snow beyond windows — hearth within  
- Slower movement; stables and conservatory calm  
- Evidence Vault and Library — reflection indoors  

---

## Christmas

- Elegant cedar, velvet ribbon, soft lights  
- Hot cocoa replaces coffee  
- Kinsey beside tree  
- Nothing commercial; nothing announced

## Birthdays & anniversaries

- Note, cake, flowers — quiet  
- Member-specific; permission-based memory  
- May align with any season's light  

---

## Years (member life on estate)

| Year 1 | Discovery — paths, nooks, first volumes |
| Year 2+ | Bookshelf fills; ribbon markers; favorite place emerges |
| Mature | Library holds many volumes; Accomplishments Books stack |
| Always | Estate recognizable — details mature |

**No leveling up.** Maturation only.

---

## Every visit is different (micro-timeline)

Rotating subtle variance — same room, not frozen:

- Kinsey asleep vs greeting at door  
- Coffee steaming vs cup empty  
- Fire high vs embers  
- Rain on glass vs clear  
- Greenhouse brightness shift  

Tiny differences → alive.
