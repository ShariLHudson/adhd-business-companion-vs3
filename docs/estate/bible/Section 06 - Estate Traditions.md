## Christmas

| Element | Expression |
|---------|------------|
| **Tone** | Elegant — not commercial |
| **Visual** | Cedar garlands, soft white lights, velvet ribbons, evergreen |
| **Warmth** | Hot cocoa replaces coffee |
| **Kinsey** | Naps beside the tree |
| **Announcement** | None — members notice |

## Birthdays

- Handwritten note may wait on a table  
- Small cake — not a modal  
- Shari remembers **if member shared** — permission for memory  
- No age surveillance or public broadcast  

---

## Anniversaries

- Business anniversaries, membership milestones, "one year since…"  
- Fresh flowers, chalkboard warm message  
- Optional letter from Shari  
- Bell **may** ring — see below  

---

## Finishing Volume I (Accomplishments Book)

- New volume placed in Library  
- Quiet pride — not "Achievement unlocked"  
- Shari acknowledges courage and consistency  
- Member owns the story of what Volume I meant  

---

## First Client

- Gentle celebration — coffee house or garden  
- Evidence may be filed in Vault **with permission**  
- Never metricized as funnel conversion  

---

## Launching a Book / Creative work

- Creative Studio → Portfolio → Library volume  
- Celebration Room or garden — flowers, note  
- Conversation drives next step — not launch checklist UI  

---

## Graduations (Institute)

- Curiosity over completion — Article XII  
- Return & Share lifecycle — permission  
- Cabinet reference, not duplicate lesson  
- Bell optional for meaningful closure  

---

## The Bell

| When | How |
|------|-----|
| Meaningful accomplishment | Shari may ring — soft, singular |
| Never | Notification sound + banner |
| Feeling | "Something mattered" — member remembers |
