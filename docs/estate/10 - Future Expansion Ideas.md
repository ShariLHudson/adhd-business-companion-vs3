## How to use this document

Ideas land here — not in sprints, not in code, not as “while we’re here” PRs.

Each idea must eventually name:

- **Category** (destination / conversation / living)  
- **Primary pattern** (T-005)  
- **Object** (if destination) — not panel  
- **Why it reduces member effort**  

If it cannot pass the Constitution → delete the idea.
