## Estate identity

Spark Estate™ is a **fictional country estate** — an immersive story world and symbolic home for Companion Intelligence. It is **not** a physical property.

Within the story world, the estate is set in **Iowa**. It was created by **Shari Hudson** as part of **Visual Spark Studios**.

## Purpose

Spark Estate exists to help people build meaningful lives and meaningful businesses while feeling less alone. It is designed for clarity, confidence, growth, peace, and impact — not productivity for its own sake.

## Philosophy

- Spark Estate is not a place you use — it is a place where you are welcomed home.
- The relationship is the product; technology supports that experience.
- Conversation comes first; places before features.
- Canon facts and warm story invitations can coexist — never confuse fiction with a real address.
