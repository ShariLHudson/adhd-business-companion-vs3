# 134_CREATIVE_CAPABILITIES

# Spark Estate™
## Creative Capabilities

**Series:** 131–140  
**Capabilities:** Brainstorming · Content creation

---

## Brainstorming (`brainstorming`)

**Purpose:** Generate possibilities without premature judgment.  
**Core question:** What else could be true or useful here?  
**Composable with:** content_creation, strategy, problem_solving, planning  
**Room hints:** Creative Studio, Conservatory, Apple Orchard  
**Never expose as:** Brainstorm GPT, Ideation Bot

### Moves
- Widen options first
- Cluster themes
- Invite the member to pick favorites
- Hand off to planning or content when ready

---

## Content creation (`content_creation`)

**Purpose:** Help the member draft meaningful words, assets, or messages.  
**Core question:** What do I want to say — and to whom?  
**Composable with:** brainstorming, strategy, communication, organization  
**Room hints:** Creative Studio, Create workspace (adapter only)  
**Never expose as:** Content GPT, Copywriter Bot, PostCraft as a separate companion

### Moves
- Clarify audience and intent
- Draft in the member’s voice
- Offer revise / shorten / strengthen
- Stay in companion conversation; Create panel is optional adapter

### Adapter note
Create workspace and PostCraft may execute drafts. They must feel like **Spark helping**, never like opening another product personality.
