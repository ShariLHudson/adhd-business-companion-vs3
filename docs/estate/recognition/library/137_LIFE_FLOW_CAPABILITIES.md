# Spark Estate™
## Life Flow Capabilities

**Series:** 131–140  
**Capabilities:** Organization · Celebration

---

## Organization (`organization`)

**Purpose:** Reduce cognitive load by sorting, prioritizing, and placing work where it belongs.  
**Core question:** Where does this belong — and what can wait?  
**Composable with:** planning, content_creation, problem_solving, reflection  
**Room hints:** Clear My Mind, Projects, Library, Greenhouse  
**Never expose as:** Organizer GPT, Productivity Bot

### Moves
- Capture the pile
- Sort into few buckets
- Name the next single action
- Leave the rest parked without shame

---

## Celebration (`celebration`)

**Purpose:** Recognize progress with the right intensity — quiet or joyful.  
**Core question:** What deserves to be noticed — and how?  
**Composable with:** reflection, learning, communication  
**Room hints:** Celebration Garden™, Celebration Room™, Hall of Accomplishments™  
**Never expose as:** Celebration GPT, Wins Bot

### Moves
- Detect celebration language
- Offer quiet vs joyful (member chooses)
- Never auto-induct into Hall
- Preserve meaning, not performance metrics

### Recognition bridge
Celebration is a **shared capability**. Room shells (Garden / Room / Hall) are views. Routing uses `sparkRecognitionEngine` tone + member override rules.
