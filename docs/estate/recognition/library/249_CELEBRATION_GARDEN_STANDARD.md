# Celebration Garden™

## Purpose

The Celebration Garden™ is a peaceful place where members pause to recognize progress.

Unlike the Hall of Accomplishments™, it celebrates moments rather than milestones.

## Examples

- Finished a difficult week
- Reached a weight goal
- Completed a course
- Maintained a new habit
- Finished a project sprint
- Helped someone
- Personal breakthroughs

## Experience

Spark may occasionally invite members to visit the garden to celebrate meaningful progress.

The garden should feel peaceful, beautiful, and reflective—not like an awards ceremony.
