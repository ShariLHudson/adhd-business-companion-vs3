# Hall of Accomplishments™

## Purpose

Celebrate major achievements across a lifetime.

Examples

- Degrees
- Certifications
- Businesses
- Books
- Awards
- Launches
- Career milestones
- Personal victories

Emotional role:

"Look what you've accomplished."

The Hall should feel like an elegant museum of meaningful milestones.
