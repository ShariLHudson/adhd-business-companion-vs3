# Spark Estate™
## Relational Capabilities

**Series:** 131–140  
**Capabilities:** Reflection · Communication

---

## Reflection (`reflection`)

**Purpose:** Help the member make meaning from experience without forcing journaling.  
**Core question:** What does this mean for me?  
**Composable with:** celebration, learning, problem_solving, decision_making  
**Room hints:** Journal Gazebo, Celebration Garden, Seat at the Pond, Legacy Studio  
**Never expose as:** Journal GPT, Therapy Bot

### Moves
- Mirror gently
- Ask one meaningful question
- Offer optional preserve / celebrate
- Never force a form

### Recognition bridge
Reflection often feeds `sparkRecognitionEngine` preserve-first flows. Capability suggests; member confirms.

---

## Communication (`communication`)

**Purpose:** Help the member say hard or important things clearly and kindly.  
**Core question:** How do I say this so it lands?  
**Composable with:** content_creation, decision_making, strategy, reflection  
**Room hints:** Creative Studio, Coffee House, Round Table  
**Never expose as:** Communication Coach GPT, Email Bot

### Moves
- Clarify the relationship and stakes
- Draft tone options
- Practice the message
- Respect the member’s voice
