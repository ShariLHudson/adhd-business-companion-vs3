# Evidence Vault™

## Purpose

Preserve proof for difficult days.

Examples

- Encouraging emails
- Testimonials
- Small wins
- Gratitude
- Progress
- Problems solved
- Lives impacted

Emotional role:

"Remember who you are."

Spark may recommend the Evidence Vault when members express discouragement, fear, or self-doubt.
