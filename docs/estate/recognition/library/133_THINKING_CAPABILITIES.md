# 133_THINKING_CAPABILITIES

# Spark Estate™
## Thinking Capabilities

**Series:** 131–140  
**Capabilities:** Decision making · Planning · Problem solving · Strategy

---

## Decision making (`decision_making`)

**Purpose:** Help the member choose among real options without overwhelm.  
**Core question:** What choice serves me best right now?  
**Composable with:** planning, problem_solving, reflection, communication  
**Room hints:** Decision Compass, Round Table, Coffee House  
**Never expose as:** Decision GPT, Choice Bot

### Moves
- Clarify the decision
- Surface 2–3 options
- Name tradeoffs gently
- Confirm the member’s pick

---

## Planning (`planning`)

**Purpose:** Turn intention into a humane sequence of next steps.  
**Core question:** What is the smallest plan that still moves me forward?  
**Composable with:** decision_making, organization, problem_solving, content_creation  
**Room hints:** Plan My Day, Momentum Institute, Creative Studio  
**Never expose as:** Planner GPT

### Moves
- Capture the outcome
- Break into few steps
- Protect energy and ADHD load
- Leave room to adjust

---

## Problem solving (`problem_solving`)

**Purpose:** Untangle stuck situations into understandable parts and workable moves.  
**Core question:** What is actually in the way — and what can we try?  
**Composable with:** decision_making, research, strategy, reflection  
**Room hints:** Momentum Institute, Study Hall, Conservatory  
**Never expose as:** Problem Solver GPT

### Moves
- Name the problem in the member’s words
- Separate facts from fear
- Offer one experiment, not ten
- Preserve discoveries in Evidence Vault when meaningful

---

## Strategy (`strategy`)

**Purpose:** Connect today’s action to a longer direction without drowning in plans.  
**Core question:** How does this move fit the bigger picture?  
**Composable with:** planning, research, content_creation, organization  
**Room hints:** Strategy Studio, Round Table, Observatory  
**Never expose as:** Strategy GPT, Business Coach Bot

### Moves
- Restate the goal
- Identify leverage
- Choose one strategic focus
- Defer detail until needed
