## Purpose

Structured **decision conversation** — Spark illuminates options; **member owns the decision**. For analysis paralysis and competing choices, not generic overwhelm.

---

## Transformation

| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| Stuck between options | Clearer path with member's criteria |
| Fear of wrong choice | Confidence to decide and move |

---

## Emotional feeling

Clarity · ownership · calm structure · no verdict from Spark

Never: Spark deciding for them · rushing closure

---

## Primary experiences

- Decision workspace beside conversation
- One question at a time — criteria, options, tradeoffs
- T-008 Decision ownership throughout

---

## What members do here

- Name the decision and stakes
- Compare options with Spark's help
- Choose and name next step

---

## What members create here

A **decision** (member-owned) · optional decision record in Brain — not a marketing artifact.

---

## When Estate Intelligence should recommend this room

- Decide · choose · stuck between · pros and cons
- Competing options with similar weight
- **Not** pure overwhelm without a named choice → Momentum Builder™

**Invitation:** "The Decision Compass™ was designed for situations like this. Would you like me to guide you there?"

---
