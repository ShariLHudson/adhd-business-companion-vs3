## Purpose

Coaching conversation in a **planning studio** — turn uncertainty into **one meaningful next step**. Recovery before productivity when overwhelmed.

Not a task manager or guilt engine.

---

## Transformation

| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| Paralyzed by too much | One honest next action |
| Overwhelm | Clarity on what matters today |

---

## Emotional feeling

Partnership · relief · forward motion · dignity

Never: shame · giant task lists · surveillance

---

## Primary experiences

- Full-screen estate room + frosted chat
- Path forward from dialogue
- Momentum Builder orchestrator — not dumped todos

---

## What members do here

- Name what's overwhelming
- Co-create the smallest real next step
- Leave with one action they chose

---

## What members create here

**One next step** · optional momentum plan — not a full project system.

---

## When Estate Intelligence should recommend this room

- Overwhelm · stuck · can't start · what should I do today
- High confidence for paralysis — **distinct from** Decision Compass (named options) and Clear My Mind (capture)

**Invitation:** "The Momentum Builder™ can help us create a simple path forward. Shall we head over?"

---

## Conversation examples

**Good:** "What's the smallest thing that would count as forward today?"

**Bad:** "Here are 12 tasks you missed."
