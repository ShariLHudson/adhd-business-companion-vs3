# Synthesized Options and Recommendations

## Strategic question

When the surface ask is incomplete, synthesis produces **one** integrated question. Preserve the member’s language. Stay concise. Stay tentative when the reframe is material.

## Next question

The next-thinking-move selector remains authoritative. Synthesis may contribute the single most important gap — never one question per domain.

## Options

Up to three integrated options via **pattern candidates** only. Live `StrategicOption` generation stays in the shared engine and must still pass:

- option readiness  
- diversity  
- capacity fit  
- epistemic safety  
- risk proportionality  
- recommendation boundary  

Example (Pricing + Capacity): raise for new customers · keep price and reduce scope · test a smaller premium with clearer boundaries.

## Trade-offs, risks, experiments

- One integrated trade-off view  
- Few material risks (calm language)  
- One experiment when uncertainty spans domains  
- Do not auto-create Projects  

## Recommendation

One warm recommendation that:

- names the strongest current path  
- explains fit across selected domains  
- acknowledges the main trade-off and uncertainty  
- distinguishes recommendation from decision  
- preserves agency  

Never: “Pricing recommends…” / “Capacity recommends…”

## Destination

May recommend one next destination. Does not perform destination work or auto-route.

## Decision Record

Enrich existing fields (`whatYouWereDeciding`, assumptions, risks, trade-offs, experiments, companion recommendation). No domain-by-domain sections, no internal IDs, no conflict mechanics. Confirmation gate unchanged.
