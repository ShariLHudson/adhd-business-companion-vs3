# Strategy Synthesis Engine — Phase 5

**Runtime:** `lib/strategyChamber/intelligence/synthesis/`  
**Audit:** `00_SYNTHESIS_AUDIT.md`  
**Engine authority:** shared Phase 1–3 judgment pipeline (unchanged)

## Product rule

Multiple domains may contribute intelligence, but Shari gives the user **one connected strategic experience**.

Never surface:
- “Pricing says…”
- “Growth says…”
- “Capacity says…”

## Behavior

1. Identify primary strategy domain  
2. Load **at most one** secondary when materially useful  
3. Extract relevant contributions  
4. Dedupe and resolve conflicts  
5. Return one coherent package (question, evidence, assumptions, constraints, option-pattern candidates, trade-offs, conflict notes)  
6. Shared option / readiness / recommendation / confirmation / Decision Record remain final authority  

## Classic pairs

| Ask | Primary | Secondary | Integrated question (example) |
|-----|---------|-----------|--------------------------------|
| Doing too much for what I charge | Pricing | Capacity | Raise price, reduce scope, or both? |
| Plenty of customers, low revenue | Growth | Pricing | Value/pricing path — not more volume by default |
| Want to grow but overwhelmed | Growth | Capacity | Stabilize before healthy growth? |
| New program, unclear audience | Offer | Market | Offer vs who it is for? |
| Hire VA to grow | Hiring | Growth | Is hiring required — or simplify/test first? |

## Member experience

One Strategy Chamber conversation. Synthesis is quiet. Adaptive Companion still changes presentation only.
