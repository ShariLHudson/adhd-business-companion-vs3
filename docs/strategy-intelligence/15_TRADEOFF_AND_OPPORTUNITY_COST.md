# Trade-off and Opportunity Cost

**Runtime:** `frameworks/tradeoffs.ts` · `frameworks/opportunityCost.ts` · `engine/compareOptions.ts`

## Trade-offs

Select only the few dimensions that materially affect the decision (never an exhaustive list).

Possible dimensions include revenue, cash, time, energy, focus, complexity, speed, quality, customer trust, retention, reputation, team burden, personal fit, sustainability, learning, flexibility, future opportunity, delivery capacity, maintenance burden, and risk.

Each option identifies:

- primary benefit
- primary trade-off
- what it protects
- what it makes harder
- what it delays or prevents
- what capacity it consumes

## Opportunity cost

Ask or infer carefully when it affects the decision:

- What would this replace?
- What would receive less attention?
- What future option could close?
- What useful flexibility would remain?
- What becomes possible if this is not pursued?
- What must stop to make room?

Do not frame every choice as loss.
