# Strategy Summary Rules — “View My Thinking So Far”

**Runtime:** `lib/strategyChamber/thinkingSummary.ts`

## Purpose

A calm mirror of the conversation — not a consultant report.

## Section rules

Only include sections with meaningful content. Prefer member language.

Possible sections:

- What you’re trying to decide
- What seems to be happening
- What matters most
- What we know
- What may still be an assumption
- What concerns you
- What opportunity you see
- What limits we need to respect
- What is still unknown
- Options being considered
- What may need to be tested
- Current readiness (plain language — never raw enums)

## Avoid

- Empty sections
- Duplicate wording (especially repeating the opening line)
- Long consultant-style essays
- Presenting tentative content as confirmed
- Folding feelings or assumptions into “what we know”

## False certainty

Assumptions, interpretations, and feelings stay labeled as such. Unknowns stay visible. Readiness copy describes the thinking posture, not a scoreboard.
