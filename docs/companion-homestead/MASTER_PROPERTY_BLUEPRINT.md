# Part I вҖ” First Arrival

## Where it is

**Rolling Henry County, Iowa** вҖ” not a resort corridor, not a suburb. County gravel road on the **north** boundary. Neighbor corn and soy visible south and west. Oak-hickory grove on the homestead itself. **Fourteen acres** вҖ” homestead scale, not estate scale.

## How you approach

You slow on the gravel road because the tires ask you to. A **white split-rail gate** stands open or latched-never-locked. You walk or drive a **curving lane** through native grasses and old maples вҖ” the house is not visible all at once. That is intentional.

## What you see first

Maples. Then roofline вҖ” white painted wood, **barn-red** door. Then **the porch swing** вҖ” still. Then garden color left of the house, **greenhouse glass** catching light right, **barn** silhouette west, a **horse** possibly at the far fence. South of everything: **fields to the horizon**.

## What you hear

Gravel underfoot. Wind in oak leaves. **Wind chimes** вҖ” one note, not a song. A cardinal if morning. Distant вҖ” a tractor maybe, miles away. Kinsey's nails on porch boards, then silence.

## What you smell

Cut grass. Soil after rain if recent. Something baking or coffee if Shari is home. Herb wall near the kitchen вҖ” thyme when you pass west.

## First emotional feeling

**Exhale.** Not excitement вҖ” relief. The world stops demanding. The property says: *You made it. You can land here.*

## Welcome before the door

The **Front Porchв„ў** is the first room вҖ” not the foyer. The swing. The welcome mat. The rocking chair angled toward you. Shari may appear вҖ” photograph-still, voice-warm вҖ” but the porch has already done the work. **Hospitality begins outdoors in Iowa.**

# Part IX вҖ” ADHD Environmental Designв„ў

Every decision passes the **ADHD Filterв„ў**:

| Question | Fail signal |
|----------|-------------|
| Reduces overwhelm? | Visual noise, too many heroes |
| Reduces cognitive load? | Competing focal points |
| Supports executive function? | No clear next physical step |
| Reduces decision fatigue? | Too many equal choices |
| Provides orientation? | Guest doesn't know where they are in home |
| Creates calm? | Alert colors, urgency copy |
| Invites creativity? | Sterile perfection |
| Rewards arrival? | Feels like login, not welcome |
| Leaves guest better? | Productivity shame |

**Environmental psychology rules:**
- **Threshold before task** вҖ” porch, kitchen, then plan
- **Body before brain** вҖ” garden path before afternoon call
- **One surface ready** вҖ” planner open, not blank page terror
- **Escape valves** вҖ” window seat always available if plan floods
- **Closure built-in** вҖ” deck sunset ends day without app reminder
