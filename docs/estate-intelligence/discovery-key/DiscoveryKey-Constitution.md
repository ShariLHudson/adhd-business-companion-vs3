---

## Purpose

The Discovery Key™ helps members discover meaningful things throughout Spark Estate that they may not find on their own.

It may help them discover:

- Estate rooms and spaces
- Features and tools
- Settings and shortcuts
- Hidden stories
- Estate history
- Positive personal patterns the system has noticed
- New possibilities
- Helpful ways to use the app

The Discovery Key™ is a **gentle guide**, not a reward system.

---

## What It Is

The Discovery Key™ is:

- A quiet invitation
- A discovery guide
- A way to reveal helpful features
- A way to introduce rooms and tools
- A way to share hidden Estate details
- A way to offer positive, simple reflection
- A way to reduce friction by taking members directly to what was discovered

---

## What It Is Not

The Discovery Key™ is **NOT**:

- A game
- A badge system
- A point system
- A checklist
- A competition
- A productivity score
- A pop-up notification system
- A pressure system
- A place to criticize, diagnose, or correct the member

---

## Emotional Experience

The member should feel:

- Curious
- Encouraged
- Gently guided
- Pleasantly surprised
- More aware of what Spark Estate can do
- More hopeful about themselves
- In control of whether they explore or ignore the key

The member should **never** feel:

- Interrupted
- Judged
- Pressured
- Behind
- Watched in a creepy way
- Like they failed to notice something
- Like they have homework

---

## Core Principle

The Discovery Key™ should feel like Spark quietly left a thoughtful note for the member to find.

It should **not** feel like software documentation or an app alert.

---

## Discovery Types

The Discovery Key™ may reveal these types of discoveries:

### 1. Estate Discovery

Introduces rooms, places, and spaces inside the Estate.

### 2. Feature Discovery

Explains a live feature, tool, setting, or shortcut.

### 3. Estate Story

Shares a story, meaning, or history behind part of the Estate.

### 4. Hidden Treasure

Reveals something delightful, seasonal, or unexpected.

### 5. Positive Personal Discovery

Shares something encouraging the system has noticed about how the member uses Spark Estate.

### 6. New Possibility

Suggests something the member may want to try next.

---

## Personal Discovery Guardrails

If the Discovery Key™ shares something the system has noticed about the member, it must be **positive, gentle, and optional**.

It may say things like:

- "I noticed you often return to quieter spaces after busy work sessions."
- "You seem to enjoy starting with a simple reflection before planning."
- "Music appears to help you settle into focused work."

It must **NOT** say things like:

- "You procrastinate."
- "You get distracted."
- "You are inconsistent."
- "You struggle with…"
- "You failed to…"

Personal Discovery language should invite reflection, not label the person.

---

## Food for Thought

Discovery Notes may include a short **Food for Thought** section.

This is not a required question field. The member does not have to answer.

It should simply plant a positive thought, such as:

- "What small thing would make today feel lighter?"
- "What might become clearer if you gave yourself ten quiet minutes?"
- "What is one thing you no longer need to carry in your mind?"

Do **not** include a required text box or forced response.

---

## Navigation Principle

If a Discovery Note mentions a specific room, tool, feature, setting, audio, or activity, it should include a clear primary action button.

Examples:

- Take Me There
- Show Me How
- Try It Now
- Open This Room
- Save for Later

If the member clicks the primary action, the app should take them **directly** to the destination.

The member should not have to search for something the Discovery Key™ just mentioned.

---

## Truth Principle

The Discovery Key™ must **never** claim a feature exists unless it actually exists and is marked **Live** in the Estate Intelligence Library.

It must not invent features, exaggerate capabilities, or describe future functionality as if it already exists.

If a feature is not live, do not show it in normal Discovery Key rotation.

**Registry authority:** [Estate Intelligence System](../README.md) — `estate-rooms.json`, `estate-features.json`, `estate-tools.json`, and related registries. Only items marked **Live** may be recommended.

---

## Visual Principle

The key should feel like part of the Estate, not a floating app icon.

It should appear naturally inside Estate room images when a discovery is available.

There should only be **one** Discovery Key™ visible at a time.

---

## Final Summary

The Discovery Key™ exists to help members gently uncover more of Spark Estate, more of what the system can do, and more positive possibilities for themselves.

It should always feel **calm, elegant, optional, and encouraging**.
