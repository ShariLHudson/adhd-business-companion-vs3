## Rule

Every room, feature, and tool must have **exactly one official name** in the Estate Intelligence registries.

Spark, the Discovery Key™, Momentum, onboarding, and all member-facing copy must use **official names only** — unless an explicit alias is defined in this file and mirrored in registry `officialName`.

---

## Why this matters

Inconsistent terminology confuses members and causes AI to invent alternate names. One vocabulary keeps the Estate feeling like **one real place** — not a collection of labels.

## Alias policy

Alternate names are allowed **only** when:

1. Listed in this document under **Approved aliases**
2. Marked in the registry item as `aliases: []`
3. Never used as the primary label in UI or Discovery Key notes

Until aliases are explicitly defined, use the **official name only**.
