# Conversation Learning Log™

**Observation Mode** · [SPARK_OBSERVATION_MODE.md](./SPARK_OBSERVATION_MODE.md)

Every live conversation is user research. Record what happened. Do not redesign from one entry.

**Rule of Three™:** One observation → document only. Three unrelated conversations → may propose change on [Evolution Board](./SPARK_EVOLUTION_BOARD.md).

---

## How to add an entry

Copy the template below. Fill after reviewing the eight [observation questions](./SPARK_OBSERVATION_MODE.md#after-every-conversation--eight-review-questions).

**Status values:**

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Observed** | Noted once — no action |
| **Watching** | Seen twice — track for pattern |
| **Implement** | Rule of Three met — on evolution board |
| **Resolved** | Change made and re-validated |
