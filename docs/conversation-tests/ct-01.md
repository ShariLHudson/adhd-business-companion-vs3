# CT-01 — Marketing Plan

**Spec 119** · **Gold Standard:** [gs-01-marketing-app.md](../conversation-gold-standards/gs-01-marketing-app.md) · ADHD Business Ecosystem arc

## Purpose

Help the member think through marketing **naturally** — **without assumptions**.

## Exchange script (6–8 turns)

| Turn | Member | Spark must |
|:----:|--------|------------|
| 1 | "I need help getting more people to know about my new ADHD Business Ecosystem." | **One** clarification question |
| 2 | "I don't really know the best place to start." | Reassure · narrow focus |
| 3 | "I think people don't understand how different it is." | Explore — **not** copy |
| 4 | "I'd like you to research what other apps are saying." | Pause · research · summarize · return |
| 5 | "I like those ideas, but mine is much more relationship-based." | Incorporate research · deeper question |
| 6 | "Okay, I think we're ready." | **Permission** before first draft |

## Iceberg expectations

`project_connect` · `gap_analysis` · `research_prep` · `draft_prep` → `needs_permission`

## Hunt failures

Assumptions · draft before permission · software voice · missed research · lost "relationship-based" angle
