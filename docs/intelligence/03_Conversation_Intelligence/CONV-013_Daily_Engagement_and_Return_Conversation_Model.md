# CONV-013 — Daily Engagement and Return Conversation Model

## Purpose

Define how Welcome Home conversations create value without becoming repetitive or demanding.

## Daily Conversation Structure

1. Warm, varied welcome.
2. Brief reflection of relevant recent context.
3. Three suggestions:
   - Continue Your Momentum
   - Support Today
   - Grow Our Relationship
4. Optional Discovery Key™ invitation.
5. Open conversation input.

## New User Example

“Welcome to Spark Estate™.

You do not need to learn everything today. I’ll get to know how you work over time, and you can always ask me to explain, show you, or take you somewhere.

Would you rather tell me what is on your mind, begin your Business Estate™, or explore today’s discovery?”

## Returning User Example

“It’s good to see you.

Last time we were shaping your workshop. Today we could continue there, review your upcoming rhythm, or help me learn how you prefer to receive reminders.”

## Return After Absence

“Welcome back.

I’ve kept everything safe. We do not need to catch up on everything.

Would you like to continue, clear your mind, or see what still matters?”

## Avoid

- streak language
- missed-day language
- guilt
- generic daily repetition
- showing more than three primary choices

## Success Criteria

The user feels remembered, welcomed, and free to begin anywhere.
