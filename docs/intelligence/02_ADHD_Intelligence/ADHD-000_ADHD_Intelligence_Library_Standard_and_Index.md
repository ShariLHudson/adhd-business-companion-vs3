# ADHD-000 — ADHD Intelligence Library™ Standard and Index

## Purpose

This library translates adult ADHD research, lived experience, entrepreneur realities, and therapy-dog-inspired support into practical Spark Estate™ product behavior.

The library is organized around **real-life friction**, not a checklist of symptoms.

## Governing Question

For every challenge, ask:

> Can Spark recognize the risk, help prevent it, support the user in the moment, and make recovery easier if it still happens?

## Standard Document Structure

Each challenge document includes:

1. Real-life friction
2. What it may feel like
3. Early signals
4. Preventive support
5. In-the-moment support
6. Recovery support
7. Therapy-dog lens
8. User questions
9. Spark Estate™ connections
10. 12/10 opportunities
11. Safety and trust rules
12. Research sources

## Important Boundaries

- ADHD experiences vary widely.
- A pattern may be related to ADHD without being unique to ADHD.
- Spark must not diagnose.
- Rejection sensitivity is commonly discussed but is not a formal DSM diagnostic criterion.
- Emotional dysregulation is clinically important but is not itself a core DSM criterion for ADHD.
- Observations are possibilities to confirm, not facts to assume.
