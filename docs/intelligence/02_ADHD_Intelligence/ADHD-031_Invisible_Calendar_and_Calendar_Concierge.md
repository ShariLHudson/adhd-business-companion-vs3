# ADHD-031 — Invisible Calendar and Calendar Concierge Framework

## Purpose

Use the calendar as quiet infrastructure so users receive the benefits of scheduling without needing to manage another calendar.

## Three Layers

### Commitments
Fixed meetings, appointments, deadlines, and travel.

### Rhythms
Recurring responsibilities that may not need rigid appointments.

### Flexible Focus
Work the user hopes to do when time and capacity fit.

## Support Modes

- Context Only: read approved commitments, make no changes.
- Calendar Guide: suggest buffers, preparation, conflicts, and lighter days.
- Calendar Concierge: offer to create or adjust items after confirmation.

## 12/10 Experience

The user says what is happening. Shari offers to place it correctly, add preparation and transition time, connect relevant people and materials, and surface it at the right moment.
