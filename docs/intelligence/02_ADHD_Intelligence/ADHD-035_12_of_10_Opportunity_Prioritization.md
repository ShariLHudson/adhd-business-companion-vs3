# ADHD-035 — 12/10 Opportunity Prioritization Framework

## Purpose

Prioritize opportunities by user relief and preventive value, not feature novelty.

## Scoring Dimensions

Score 1–5:

- Frequency of the friction
- Severity when it happens
- Preventability
- Cognitive load reduced
- Emotional relief
- Number of estate systems improved
- Differentiation from existing tools
- Ease of discovering through Shari
- Implementation effort (reverse score)
- Privacy and trust risk (reverse score)

## Priority Categories

### Build Now
High relief, high frequency, strong architectural fit.

### Connect Now
Capability exists but is hidden or disconnected.

### Research More
Promising but evidence or user need is unclear.

### Do Not Build
Adds maintenance, duplicates external tools, or increases cognitive load.
