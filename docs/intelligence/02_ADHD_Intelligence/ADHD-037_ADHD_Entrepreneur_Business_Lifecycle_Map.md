# ADHD-037 — ADHD Entrepreneur Business Lifecycle Map

## Purpose

Identify how ADHD friction changes across the business lifecycle.

## Stages

### Idea and Opportunity
Risks: idea overload, impulsive commitment, insufficient validation.

### Validation
Risks: boredom with research, confirmation bias, premature building.

### Setup
Risks: administrative avoidance, tool switching, over-design.

### Launch
Risks: deadline crisis, perfectionism, emotional exposure.

### Delivery
Risks: overpromising, weak boundaries, follow-up gaps.

### Maintenance
Risks: novelty loss, bookkeeping avoidance, inconsistent marketing.

### Growth
Risks: too many offers, delegation resistance, process gaps.

### Scale
Risks: complexity, communication overload, loss of founder focus.

### Burnout or Disruption
Risks: shutdown, shame, abandoned systems.

### Recovery and Renewal
Needs: context restoration, priority reset, capacity-aware rebuilding.

## Spark Rule

Shari should recognize the current business stage from explicit user context and adapt questions, risks, and support after confirmation.
