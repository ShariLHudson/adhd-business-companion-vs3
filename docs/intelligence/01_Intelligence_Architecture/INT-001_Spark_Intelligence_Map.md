# Responsibility Summary

| Library | Primary Question |
|---|---|
| Conversation Intelligence | How should Shari respond? |
| ADHD Intelligence | Why might this be difficult for an ADHD user? |
| Founder Experience Intelligence | What is the founder carrying? |
| Human Experience Intelligence | What human experience may be affecting this moment? |
| Companion Intelligence | How should the interaction feel? |
| Memory & Context Intelligence | What relevant information may be used? |
| Execution Intelligence | What should happen next? |
| Capability Intelligence | What does the feature do? |
| Estate Intelligence | Where does it live? |
| Founder Intelligence | What should the product learn from this? |

# Example: "I am overwhelmed"

## Conversation Intelligence

Recognizes overwhelm and reduces choices.

## ADHD Intelligence

Supplies executive-function and cognitive-load context.

## Founder Experience Intelligence

Supplies role-overload and solo-founder burden.

## Human Experience Intelligence

Supplies emotional strain and uncertainty context.

## Companion Intelligence

Keeps Shari calm, warm, and nonjudgmental.

## Memory & Context Intelligence

Retrieves only current priorities and approved preferences.

## Execution Intelligence

Chooses settle, capture, narrow, or route.

## Capability Intelligence

Provides Clear My Mind™, Soundscapes, reminders, or Work Threads.

## Estate Intelligence

Opens the correct destination.

## Founder Intelligence

Records whether this pattern reveals a product gap.

---

# Implementation Rule

No library should operate alone.

No library should own another library's responsibility.

The user should experience the combined result as one seamless conversation.
