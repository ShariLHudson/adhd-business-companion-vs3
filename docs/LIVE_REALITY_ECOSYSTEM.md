## What Live Reality‚ĄĘ Actually Is

Live Reality‚ĄĘ is the continuously changing understanding of the user's current situation.

Examples:

- Current energy
- Current motivation
- Current stress
- Current focus
- Current health
- Available time
- Unexpected events
- Family priorities
- Business priorities
- Mental capacity
- Interruptions
- Mood
- Current location
- Current commitments
- Anything that changes today's reality

The **Companion Brain‚ĄĘ** continuously reasons from this. Nothing else should.
