# HUMAN-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose
Defines what Human Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns
- self-awareness
- emotions
- human behavior patterns
- personal reflection
- empathy
- understanding individual experiences

## Supports
Works with:
- Wellness Intelligence
- Leadership Intelligence
- Momentum Intelligence
- Executive Function Intelligence

## Does Not Own
- diagnosis
- therapy
- making personal decisions for users

## Core Rule
Human Intelligence helps Spark understand the person behind the goals.
