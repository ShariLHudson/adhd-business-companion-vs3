# EXF-054 Organization Intelligence
## Authority
Defines Organization as an Executive Function concept.
---
# Purpose
Organization Intelligence explains how people structure, categorize, arrange, and retrieve information, materials, and actions.
Organization reduces unnecessary cognitive effort by improving accessibility and clarity.
---
# Scope
Organization Intelligence owns:
- structure
- categorization
- arrangement
- retrieval support
It does not own:
- productivity systems
- project management
- knowledge architecture
---
# Organization Components
## Categorization
Creating meaningful groups.
## Structure
Creating relationships between information or items.
## Accessibility
Making needed information easier to find.
## Maintenance
Supporting continued usability.
---
# Relationship With Working Memory
Organization reduces the amount of information that must be actively maintained internally.
---
# Relationship With Knowledge Management
Organization Intelligence explains cognitive organization.
Knowledge Management owns information systems.
---
# Permanent Ownership Rule
Organization Intelligence owns understanding how structure supports executive functioning.
