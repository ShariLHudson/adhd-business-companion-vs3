# EXF-071 Executive Support Matching Intelligence
Defines Executive Support Matching as an Executive Function Intelligence concept for understanding how support types can be aligned with specific executive demands and conditions.
---
# Authority
This document defines Executive Support Matching within Executive Function Intelligence.
It does not define:
- recommendations
- coaching strategies
- productivity systems
- interventions
- personal improvement plans
It focuses only on the relationship between executive demands and appropriate support categories.
---
# Purpose
Support is most effective when it addresses the actual source of difficulty.
A person experiencing difficulty beginning a task may need different support than someone experiencing:
- information overload
- unclear structure
- attention competition
- transition difficulty
Executive Support Matching Intelligence exists to understand how executive needs connect with appropriate forms of support.
It answers the question:
**"What type of executive support best matches the demand being experienced?"**

# Executive Support Matching Defined
Executive Support Matching is the process of identifying the executive demand involved in a situation and determining what type of support may reduce unnecessary cognitive burden.
The process connects:
- executive challenge
- contributing demand
- support category
---
# Core Matching Principle
The correct support begins with understanding the demand.
Do not start with:
"What tool should be used?"
Start with:
"What executive process is creating difficulty?"
---
# Executive Demand Categories
## Initiation Demand
Related to:
- beginning action
- identifying first steps
- moving from intention to action
Potential support categories:
- clearer first steps
- visible starting cues
- reduced initial complexity
---
## Planning Demand
Related to:
- future action
- sequencing
- preparation
Potential support categories:
- structured pathways
- external planning support
- visible steps
---
## Working Memory Demand
Related to:
- holding information
- maintaining context
- remembering details
Potential support categories:
- external information storage
- reminders
- reference systems
---
## Organization Demand
Related to:
- structure
- retrieval
- accessibility
Potential support categories:
- categorization
- simplified systems
- clear locations
---
## Attention Demand
Related to:
- competing information
- focus direction
- interruptions
Potential support categories:
- reduced distractions
- clearer focus cues
- simplified environments
---
## Transition Demand
Related to:
- moving between activities
- changing states
Potential support categories:
- preparation cues
- transition awareness
- clear next actions
---
## Regulation Demand
Related to:
- managing responses during action
Potential support categories:
- awareness
- reduced unnecessary demand
- adaptive conditions
---

# Executive Support Matching Does Not Mean
Support matching is not:
- diagnosing the person
- identifying a permanent weakness
- choosing one solution for everyone
- replacing personal judgment
---
# Relationship With Adaptive Executive Support
Executive Support Matching provides:
- understanding of demand-support relationships

- adjustment of support over time
