# EXF-057 Time Awareness Intelligence
## Authority
Defines Time Awareness as an Executive Function concept.
---
# Purpose
Time Awareness Intelligence explains how people perceive, estimate, organize, and respond to time demands.
---
# Scope
Owns:
- time perception
- time estimation
- time awareness
Does not own:
- scheduling systems
- calendars
- productivity methods
---
# Time Awareness Components
## Time Estimation
Understanding how long activities require.
## Time Perspective
Understanding relationship between present and future demands.
## Time Monitoring
Recognizing passage of time.
## Time Allocation
Understanding available time resources.
---
# Relationship With Executive Function
Time Awareness influences:
- planning
- prioritization
- transitions
---
# Permanent Ownership Rule
Time Awareness Intelligence owns understanding time-related executive processes.
