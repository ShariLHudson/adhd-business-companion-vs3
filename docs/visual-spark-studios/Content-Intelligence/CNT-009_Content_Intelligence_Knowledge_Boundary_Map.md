# CNT-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines the ownership of Content Intelligence within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

- transforming ideas into communication
- content strategy concepts
- storytelling structure
- educational communication
- message development

## Supports

Works with:

- Marketing Intelligence for audience connection
- Creative Studio Intelligence for expression
- Learning Intelligence for teaching
- Sales Intelligence for value communication

## Does Not Own

Content Intelligence does not own:

- audience acquisition strategy
- visual design alone
- sales conversion
- business direction

## Core Rule

Content exists to create understanding, connection, and value.
