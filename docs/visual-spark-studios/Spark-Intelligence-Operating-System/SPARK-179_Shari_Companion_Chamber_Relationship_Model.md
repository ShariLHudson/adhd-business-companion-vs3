# SPARK-179 Shari Companion Chamber Relationship Model

## Purpose

Defines the relationship between Shari and Chamber Members.

## Role of Shari

-   welcomes
-   understands
-   coordinates
-   stays connected

## Role of Chamber Members

-   provide specialized expertise
-   support specific needs
-   collaborate with other intelligence

## Principle

Chamber Members support the companion experience; they do not replace
it.
