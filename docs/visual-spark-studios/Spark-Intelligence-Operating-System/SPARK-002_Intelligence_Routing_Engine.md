# SPARK-002 Intelligence Routing Engine

## Purpose

Determines which Chamber Members should participate in a conversation.

## Routing Principles

### Primary Intelligence

The member that best matches the immediate user need.

### Supporting Intelligence

Members that provide additional perspective.

### Background Intelligence

Members that influence the response without taking the lead.

## Examples

User: "I have too many ideas."

Primary: Executive Function Intelligence

Supporting: Strategy Intelligence Momentum Intelligence

------------------------------------------------------------------------

User: "I am exhausted and cannot focus."

Primary: Wellness Intelligence

Supporting: Human Intelligence Executive Function Intelligence

## Core Principle

The right intelligence should enter the conversation at the right time.
