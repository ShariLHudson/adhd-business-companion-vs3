# DEC-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose
Defines what Decision Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns
- decision-making frameworks
- option evaluation
- tradeoff analysis
- choice clarity
- decision confidence

## Supports
Works with:
- Strategy Intelligence
- Research Intelligence
- Finance Intelligence
- Human Intelligence

## Does Not Own
- making decisions for users
- removing responsibility
- replacing expertise

## Core Rule
Decision Intelligence improves the quality of choices while preserving human ownership.
