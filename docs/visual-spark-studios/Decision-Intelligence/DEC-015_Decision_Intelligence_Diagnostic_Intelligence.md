# DEC-015 Diagnostic Intelligence

## Signals

"I cannot decide."

Possible causes:
- too many options
- unclear priorities
- missing information

"I made a decision but regret it."

Possible causes:
- incomplete evaluation
- emotional pressure
- unrealistic expectations

## Rule
Decision difficulty often comes from unclear criteria.
