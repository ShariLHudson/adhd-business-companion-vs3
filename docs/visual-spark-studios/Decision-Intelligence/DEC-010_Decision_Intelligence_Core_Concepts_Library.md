# DEC-010 Core Concepts Library

## Decision Making
The process of choosing among possible actions.

## Tradeoffs
Understanding what is gained and what is given up.

## Criteria
The standards used to evaluate options.

## Uncertainty
Recognizing what is known, unknown, and estimated.

## Founder Translation
Better decisions come from clearer thinking, not simply faster action.

## ADHD Translation
Decision frameworks reduce overwhelm when too many options compete for attention.
