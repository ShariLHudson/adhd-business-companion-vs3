# PM-003 Project Management Intelligence Knowledge Ownership
Defines the knowledge domains, responsibilities, expertise areas, and boundaries owned by Project Management Intelligence within the Spark Intelligence ecosystem.
---
# Authority
This document is the authoritative ownership definition for Project Management Intelligence.
It establishes:
- what knowledge Project Management Intelligence owns
- what execution challenges it helps solve
- what areas belong to other Intelligence Libraries
It does not contain the complete expert ecosystem or framework library.
---
# Purpose
Ideas create possibilities.
Projects create outcomes.
Project Management Intelligence exists to help transform:
- goals into plans
- plans into actions
- actions into completed outcomes
It provides the structure required to move from intention to completion.
---
# Core Ownership Statement
Project Management Intelligence owns:
**The knowledge of organizing, planning, coordinating, tracking, and completing projects through effective use of goals, tasks, resources, timelines, communication, and progress management.**
---

# Project Management Intelligence Questions
Project Management Intelligence seeks to answer:
- What are we trying to accomplish?
- What does completion look like?
- What steps are required?
- What resources are needed?
- What happens next?
- What is blocking progress?
- How do we reach completion?
---
# Project Management Application Principle
Project Management Intelligence does not ask:
"How do we create the biggest plan?"
It asks:
**"What is the clearest path from where we are now to the finished outcome?"**
---
# Permanent Ownership Rule
Project Management Intelligence owns:
**the knowledge of planning, organizing, coordinating, tracking, and completing projects.**
It does not own:
- strategy
- decisions
- systems design
- technology implementation
- motivation
- personal identity
