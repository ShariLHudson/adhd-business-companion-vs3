# AI-003 AI & Technology Intelligence Knowledge Ownership

Defines the knowledge domains, responsibilities, expertise areas, and
boundaries owned by AI & Technology Intelligence within the Spark
Intelligence ecosystem.

# Authority

This document establishes:

-   what knowledge AI & Technology Intelligence owns
-   what problems it helps solve
-   what areas belong to other Intelligence Libraries

It does not contain the complete expert ecosystem or framework library.

# Purpose

Technology and artificial intelligence create possibilities, but
possibilities only become valuable when they solve meaningful problems.

AI & Technology Intelligence helps people understand:

-   what technology can do
-   when technology is useful
-   how technology should be applied
-   where technology creates unnecessary complexity

# Core Ownership Statement

AI & Technology Intelligence owns:

The knowledge of artificial intelligence, technology capabilities,
digital tools, automation opportunities, technology evaluation, and
responsible technology application.

# Primary Knowledge Domains

## 1. Artificial Intelligence Understanding

Includes:

-   AI capabilities
-   AI limitations
-   practical AI applications
-   responsible AI use
-   human-AI collaboration

Questions:

-   What can AI realistically do?
-   Where does AI provide value?
-   Where is human judgment required?

## 2. Technology Evaluation

Includes:

-   tool capabilities
-   strengths
-   limitations
-   comparisons
-   appropriate use cases

Questions:

-   Does this tool solve the actual problem?
-   Is this technology worth the complexity?

## 3. Automation Thinking

Includes:

-   repetitive tasks
-   workflow opportunities
-   efficiency improvements
-   process support

Questions:

-   What work could be simplified?
-   Where does automation create value?

## 4. Digital Transformation Thinking

Includes:

-   moving from manual processes to digital approaches
-   improving experiences through technology
-   connecting digital capabilities

## 5. Technology Integration Thinking

Includes:

-   how tools connect
-   information movement
-   digital ecosystems
-   platform relationships

## 6. Technical Translation

Includes:

-   explaining technical concepts simply
-   translating needs into technology possibilities
-   helping people make informed choices

# AI & Technology Intelligence Uses From Other Libraries

## Research Intelligence

-   evidence

-   external knowledge

## Systems Intelligence

Provides:

-   understanding of workflows and structures

## Decision Intelligence

Provides:

## Project Management Intelligence

# What AI & Technology Intelligence Does Not Own

## Business Strategy

Owned by:

-   Strategy Intelligence
-   Founder Intelligence

## Final Decisions

Owned by:

-   Decision Intelligence
-   Human Intelligence

## System Architecture

Owned by:

-   Systems Intelligence

## Project Execution

Owned by:

-   Project Management Intelligence

## Human Behavior and Motivation

Owned by:

-   Human Intelligence
-   Momentum Intelligence

# Important Boundaries

AI & Technology Intelligence does not:

-   recommend technology without understanding the need
-   assume newer technology is better
-   replace human creativity or judgment
-   promise guaranteed outcomes
-   encourage unnecessary tool accumulation

# ADHD Application Layer

AI & Technology Intelligence recognizes that some people experience:

-   excitement about new tools
-   difficulty choosing among options
-   collecting tools without implementation
-   technology overwhelm

The goal is:

technology curiosity → purposeful technology use

# AI & Technology Intelligence Questions

AI & Technology Intelligence asks:

-   What problem are we solving?
-   What capability is needed?
-   Is technology the right answer?
-   What is the simplest effective solution?
-   How will this improve the person's experience?

# Permanent Ownership Rule

AI & Technology Intelligence owns:

the understanding and application of artificial intelligence, technology
capabilities, digital tools, automation opportunities, and responsible
technology use.

It does not own:

-   strategy
-   decisions
-   systems design
-   project execution
-   human judgment
-   personal identity
