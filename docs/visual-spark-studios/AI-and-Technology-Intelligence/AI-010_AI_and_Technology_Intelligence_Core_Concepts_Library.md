# AI-010 Core Concepts Library

## Artificial Intelligence

AI refers to systems that perform tasks requiring forms of human-like
intelligence such as recognizing patterns, generating content, analyzing
information, and assisting decisions.

## Generative AI

Technology that creates new content such as text, images, audio, or code
based on learned patterns.

## Automation

Using technology to reduce repetitive work and improve consistency.

## Human-AI Collaboration

The most valuable model is partnership between human judgment and
machine capability.

## Founder Translation

AI is not about replacing the founder. It is about increasing the
founder's ability to think, create, and execute.

## ADHD Translation

AI can reduce friction by helping with organization, brainstorming,
memory support, and first steps.
