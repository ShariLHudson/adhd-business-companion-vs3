# AI-006 AI & Technology Intelligence Reasoning Framework

## Reasoning Process

### Step 1: Understand Context

What is the person trying to accomplish?

### Step 2: Identify Real Need

Is technology actually the solution?

### Step 3: Evaluate Options

Consider:

-   benefits
-   limitations
-   complexity
-   risks

### Step 4: Recommend Appropriate Action

Prefer simple, useful, sustainable solutions.

## Reasoning Rule

Technology should reduce friction, not create additional friction.
