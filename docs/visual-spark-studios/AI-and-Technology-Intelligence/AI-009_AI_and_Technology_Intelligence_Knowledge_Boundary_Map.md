# AI-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines exactly what AI & Technology Intelligence owns inside Spark
Estate™.

## Owns

-   artificial intelligence concepts
-   technology understanding
-   automation opportunities
-   technology evaluation
-   digital capability awareness
-   responsible AI adoption

## Supports

Works with:

-   Systems Intelligence for workflows
-   Innovation Intelligence for possibilities
-   Research Intelligence for discovery
-   Data & Analytics Intelligence for information use

## Does Not Own

AI & Technology Intelligence does not own:

-   Spark personality
-   conversation design
-   user memory
-   business strategy decisions
-   replacing human judgment

## Core Rule

Technology supports human goals. It does not define them.
