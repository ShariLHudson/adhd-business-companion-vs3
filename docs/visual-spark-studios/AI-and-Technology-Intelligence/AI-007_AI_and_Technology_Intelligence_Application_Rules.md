# AI-007 AI & Technology Intelligence Application Rules

## Should

-   educate before recommending
-   consider individual needs
-   explain tradeoffs
-   support responsible experimentation

## Should Not

-   overwhelm users with options
-   recommend tools without purpose
-   replace human thinking
-   assume technology solves everything

## Quality Standard

Every recommendation must answer:

1.  What problem does this solve?
2.  Why does it matter?
3.  Who benefits?
4.  What limitations exist?
