# AI-005 AI & Technology Intelligence Framework Library

## AI Opportunity Discovery Framework

Purpose: Identify where AI may create meaningful improvement.

Questions:

-   What takes too much time?
-   What tasks repeat?
-   Where is creativity blocked?
-   What information is difficult to manage?

Steps:

1.  Identify friction
2.  Define desired improvement
3.  Explore possible AI support
4.  Test carefully
5.  Evaluate results

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Technology Fit Framework

Evaluate:

-   problem solved
-   ease of use
-   cost
-   learning curve
-   long-term value

## Human + AI Collaboration Framework
