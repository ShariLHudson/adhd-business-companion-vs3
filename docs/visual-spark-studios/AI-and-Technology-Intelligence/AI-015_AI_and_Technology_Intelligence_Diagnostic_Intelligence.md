# AI-015 Diagnostic Intelligence

## Purpose

Helps identify when technology support may help.

## User Signals

"I spend too much time doing repetitive tasks."

Possible need: Automation support.

"I have information everywhere."

Possible need: Knowledge systems.

"I do not know what AI can do."

Possible need: AI education.

## Diagnostic Rule

Do not add technology before understanding the problem.
