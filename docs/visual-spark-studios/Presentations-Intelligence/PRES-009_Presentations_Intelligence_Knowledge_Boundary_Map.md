# PRES-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose
Defines what Presentations Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns
- presentation creation
- storytelling structure
- audience engagement
- visual communication
- speaking preparation
- message delivery

## Supports
Works with:
- Marketing Intelligence
- Learning Intelligence
- Leadership Intelligence
- Creative Intelligence

## Does Not Own
- the entire business message
- sales strategy alone
- graphic design alone

## Core Rule
A great presentation transforms information into understanding and action.
