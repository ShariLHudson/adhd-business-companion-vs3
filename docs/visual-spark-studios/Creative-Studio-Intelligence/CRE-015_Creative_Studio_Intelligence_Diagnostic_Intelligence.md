# CRE-015 Diagnostic Intelligence

## Purpose

Identifies creative challenges.

## Signals

"I have an idea but cannot visualize it."

Possible causes:

- unclear concept
- missing creative direction

"My design feels wrong."

Possible causes:

- audience mismatch
- unclear purpose
- inconsistent style

## Diagnostic Rule

Creative problems often begin with unclear intention.
