# CRE-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Creative Studio Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

- creative expression
- visual thinking
- design concepts
- creative direction
- aesthetics
- creative problem solving

## Supports

Works with:

- Content Intelligence for message expression
- Marketing Intelligence for brand connection
- Presentations Intelligence for visual communication
- Innovation Intelligence for idea development

## Does Not Own

Creative Studio Intelligence does not own:

- business strategy
- audience research
- sales decisions
- technical implementation

## Core Rule

Creativity transforms ideas into experiences people can see, feel, and understand.
