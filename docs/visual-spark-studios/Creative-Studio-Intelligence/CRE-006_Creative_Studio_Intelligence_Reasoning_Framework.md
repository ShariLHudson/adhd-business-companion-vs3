# CRE-006 Creative Studio Intelligence Reasoning Framework

## Reasoning Process

Step 1: Understand the purpose.

Step 2: Explore possibilities.

Step 3: Consider the audience experience.

Step 4: Choose the most useful creative direction.

Step 5: Refine through feedback.

## Reasoning Rule

Creativity should expand possibilities while still serving a purpose.
