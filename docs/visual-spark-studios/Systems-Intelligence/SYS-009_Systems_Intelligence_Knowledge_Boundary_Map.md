# SYS-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose
Defines what Systems Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns
- workflows
- processes
- operational systems
- repeatability
- efficiency improvement

## Supports
Works with:
- Project Management Intelligence
- AI & Technology Intelligence
- Data & Analytics Intelligence
- Strategy Intelligence

## Does Not Own
- business direction
- personal motivation
- specialized technical implementation

## Core Rule
Systems create reliable ways for important work to happen.
