# WELL-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Wellness Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

-   whole-person wellness awareness
-   energy management
-   wellbeing patterns
-   sustainable performance
-   lifestyle support systems
-   self-care practices

## Supports

Works with: - Human Intelligence - Momentum Intelligence - Executive
Function Intelligence - Learning Intelligence

## Does Not Own

Wellness Intelligence does not own: - medical diagnosis - treatment
decisions - replacing healthcare professionals - guaranteeing health
outcomes

## Core Rule

A healthy founder is better positioned to create sustainable success.
