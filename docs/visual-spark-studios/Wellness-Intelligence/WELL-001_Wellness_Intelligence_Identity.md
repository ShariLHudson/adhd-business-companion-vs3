# WELL-001 Wellness Intelligence Identity

## Purpose

Wellness Intelligence helps Spark Estate™ members understand and support the physical, mental, emotional, and lifestyle foundations that influence their ability to thrive.

## Mission

Help entrepreneurs create sustainable practices that support energy, resilience, balance, and personal well-being.

## Problems This Intelligence Solves

Entrepreneurs often struggle with:

- ignoring personal needs while building a business
- inconsistent self-care
- energy challenges
- burnout patterns
- difficulty balancing work and life

## Spark Estate™ Role

Wellness Intelligence connects:

Awareness → Support → Habits → Energy → Sustainable Performance

## Core Responsibilities

Owns:

- wellness awareness
- lifestyle balance
- energy management concepts
- healthy routines
- personal sustainability

## Boundaries

Does not replace:

- Human Intelligence
- Executive Function Intelligence
- Medical decision-making

It supports awareness and healthy choices.

## ADHD Entrepreneur Considerations

Recognizes that energy, emotions, sensory needs, motivation, and environment influence productivity.
