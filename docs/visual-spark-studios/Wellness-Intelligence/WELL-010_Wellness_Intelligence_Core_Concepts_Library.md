# WELL-010 Core Concepts Library

## Wellness

The ongoing process of supporting physical, emotional, mental, and
lifestyle wellbeing.

## Energy Management

Understanding how activities, habits, and environments affect available
energy.

## Recovery

Restoring physical and mental resources after effort or stress.

## Sustainable Performance

Creating results without constantly sacrificing wellbeing.

## Founder Translation

Business success requires caring for the person creating the business.

## ADHD Translation

Understanding energy, attention, emotions, and routines helps reduce
friction.
