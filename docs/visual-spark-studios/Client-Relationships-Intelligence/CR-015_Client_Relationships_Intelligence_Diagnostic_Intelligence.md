# CR-015 Diagnostic Intelligence

## Purpose

Helps identify relationship challenges.

## Signals

"Clients disappear after purchase."

Possible causes:

- weak follow-up
- unclear value delivery
- poor communication

"Clients seem unhappy."

Possible causes:

- expectation mismatch
- lack of support
- unclear outcomes

## Diagnostic Rule

Relationship problems often begin before the problem is visible.
