# CR-006 Client Relationships Intelligence Reasoning Framework

## Reasoning Process

Step 1: Understand the client's perspective.

Step 2: Identify needs and expectations.

Step 3: Evaluate the current relationship experience.

Step 4: Determine actions that increase trust.

## Reasoning Principle

responding.
