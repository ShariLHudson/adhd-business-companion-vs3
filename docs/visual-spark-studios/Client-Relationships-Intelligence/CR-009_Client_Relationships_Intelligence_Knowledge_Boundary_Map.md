# CR-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Client Relationships Intelligence owns inside Spark Estate™.

## Owns

- client experience after commitment
- relationship health
- retention thinking
- trust development
- client communication systems
- feedback and improvement loops

## Supports

Works with:

- Sales Intelligence for transition from prospect to client
- Marketing Intelligence for customer understanding
- Partnerships Intelligence for relationship ecosystems
- Human Intelligence for individual understanding

## Does Not Own

Client Relationships Intelligence does not own:

- finding prospects
- closing sales
- creating marketing campaigns
- replacing personal judgment

## Core Rule

The relationship continues after the sale. Value must continue after commitment.
