# PART-015 Diagnostic Intelligence

## Signals

"I want to grow but cannot do everything alone."

Possible need: Strategic partnerships.

"I have contacts but no collaborations."

Possible need: Partnership structure.

"I partnered with someone and it did not work."

Possible need: Alignment review.

## Rule

Good partnerships require purpose, not just connection.
