# DATA-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Data & Analytics Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

-   data understanding
-   measurement concepts
-   analytics thinking
-   pattern identification
-   insight development
-   performance tracking

## Supports

Works with:

-   Research Intelligence for information discovery
-   Strategy Intelligence for direction
-   Finance Intelligence for business measurement
-   Systems Intelligence for operational improvement

## Does Not Own

Data & Analytics Intelligence does not own:

-   collecting meaningless data
-   replacing human judgment
-   making business decisions alone
-   creating strategy without context

## Core Rule

Data creates understanding. People create meaning.
