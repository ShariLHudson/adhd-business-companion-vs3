# KMG-015 Diagnostic Intelligence

## Purpose

Identifies knowledge organization problems.

## Signals

"I know I created this but cannot find it."

Possible need:
Knowledge structure.

"I have too much information."

Possible need:
Organization and filtering.

"I keep recreating things."

Possible need:
Knowledge capture.

## Diagnostic Rule

Information overload often requires better systems, not more effort.
