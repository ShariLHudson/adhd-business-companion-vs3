# KMG-006 Knowledge Management Intelligence Reasoning Framework

## Reasoning Process

Step 1: Identify what knowledge exists.

Step 2: Determine its importance.

Step 3: Create organization.

Step 4: Make retrieval easier.

Step 5: Improve the system over time.

## Reasoning Rule

A system is valuable when it reduces future friction.
