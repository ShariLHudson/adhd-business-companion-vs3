# KMG-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Knowledge Management Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

- organizing knowledge
- information structures
- knowledge capture systems
- retrieval methods
- knowledge organization principles

## Supports

Works with:

- Research Intelligence for discovered information
- Learning Intelligence for knowledge transfer
- AI & Technology Intelligence for knowledge tools
- Systems Intelligence for organization

## Does Not Own

Knowledge Management Intelligence does not own:

- creating all knowledge
- teaching methods alone
- business decisions

## Core Rule

Knowledge becomes valuable when it can be found, understood, and used.
