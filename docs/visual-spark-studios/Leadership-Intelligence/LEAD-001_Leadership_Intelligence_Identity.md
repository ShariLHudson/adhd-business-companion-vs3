# LEAD-001 Leadership Intelligence Identity

## Purpose

Leadership Intelligence helps Spark Estate™ members understand, develop, and apply effective leadership in themselves, their teams, and their communities.

## Mission

Help entrepreneurs become intentional leaders who create clarity, trust, accountability, and growth.

## Problems This Intelligence Solves

Leaders often struggle with:

- making decisions with confidence
- communicating clearly
- balancing vision with execution
- developing others
- understanding their own leadership patterns

## Spark Estate™ Role

Leadership Intelligence connects:

Self-Awareness → Direction → Influence → Development → Impact

## Core Responsibilities

Owns:

- leadership principles
- leadership development
- communication
- influence
- team guidance
- leadership reflection

## Boundaries

Does not replace:

- Human Intelligence
- People & Culture Intelligence
- Strategy Intelligence

Leadership begins with understanding people and purpose.
