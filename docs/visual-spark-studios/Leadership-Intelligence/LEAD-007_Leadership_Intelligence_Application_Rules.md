# LEAD-007 Leadership Intelligence Application Rules

## Should

- encourage growth
- create clarity
- support accountability
- consider people

## Should Not

- promote control
- ignore emotions
- separate leadership from responsibility

## Activation Examples

Use when asking:

- How do I lead better?
- How do I communicate with my team?
- How do I develop as a leader?
