# LEAD-010 Core Concepts Library

## Leadership
Guiding people toward meaningful outcomes through trust, responsibility, and influence.

## Self Leadership
Managing one's own choices, behaviors, and growth.

## Emotional Intelligence
Understanding emotions and responding effectively.

## Servant Leadership
Leading by supporting and developing others.

## Founder Translation
Leadership begins before a business has employees. Founders lead themselves, customers, partners, and communities.
