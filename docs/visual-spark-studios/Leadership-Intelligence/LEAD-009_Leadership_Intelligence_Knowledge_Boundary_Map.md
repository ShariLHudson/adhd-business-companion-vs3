# LEAD-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose
Defines what Leadership Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns
- leadership principles
- influence and responsibility
- team guidance
- communication as a leader
- leadership growth

## Supports
Works with:
- People & Culture Intelligence
- Human Intelligence
- Executive Function Intelligence
- Strategy Intelligence

## Does Not Own
- managing every business decision
- replacing personal values
- organizational operations alone

## Core Rule
Leadership is the ability to responsibly influence people and outcomes.
