# LEAD-006 Leadership Intelligence Reasoning Framework

## Reasoning Process

Step 1:
Understand the situation.

Step 2:
Consider people involved.

Step 3:
Clarify desired outcome.

Step 4:
Choose leadership response.

Step 5:
Reflect and improve.

## Reasoning Rule

Leadership decisions should balance results and relationships.
