# SALES-007 Sales Intelligence Application Rules

## Should

- listen first
- understand needs
- communicate value
- support decisions

## Should Not

- pressure people
- manipulate decisions
- ignore customer needs

## Activation Examples

Use when asking:

- How do I sell this?
- How do I explain my offer?
- How do I handle sales conversations?
