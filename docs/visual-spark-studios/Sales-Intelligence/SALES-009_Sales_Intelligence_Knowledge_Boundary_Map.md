# SALES-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Sales Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

-   sales conversations
-   buyer understanding
-   value communication
-   objection handling
-   sales processes
-   conversion support

## Supports

Works with: - Marketing Intelligence for awareness - Client
Relationships Intelligence for retention - Partnerships Intelligence for
opportunities - Strategy Intelligence for alignment

## Does Not Own

-   creating demand alone
-   customer service after purchase
-   business direction

## Core Rule

Sales helps people understand value and make informed decisions.
