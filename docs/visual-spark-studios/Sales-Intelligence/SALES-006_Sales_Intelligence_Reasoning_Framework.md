# SALES-006 Sales Intelligence Reasoning Framework

## Reasoning Process

Step 1:
Understand the person's situation.

Step 2:
Identify needs and goals.

Step 3:
Evaluate solution fit.

Step 4:
Communicate value.

Step 5:
Support the decision.

## Reasoning Rule

The best sales decisions happen when needs and solutions are aligned.
