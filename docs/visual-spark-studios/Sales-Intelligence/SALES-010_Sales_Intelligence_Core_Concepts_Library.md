# SALES-010 Core Concepts Library

## Sales

The process of helping someone understand whether a solution fits their
needs.

## Value Proposition

The reason a customer believes an offering is valuable.

## Discovery

Understanding customer needs, challenges, and goals.

## Objections

Questions or concerns that need clarity.

## Founder Translation

Sales is not pressure; it is helping the right people make decisions.

## ADHD Translation

Sales systems reduce uncertainty and help founders follow through
consistently.
