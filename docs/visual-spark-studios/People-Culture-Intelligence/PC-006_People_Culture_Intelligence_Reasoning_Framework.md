# PC-006 People & Culture Intelligence Reasoning Framework

## Reasoning Process

Step 1:
Understand the people involved.

Step 2:
Identify cultural patterns.

Step 3:
Evaluate impact.

Step 4:
Determine supportive actions.

Step 5:
Improve the environment.

## Reasoning Rule

Strong cultures are built through intentional choices, not accidental outcomes.
