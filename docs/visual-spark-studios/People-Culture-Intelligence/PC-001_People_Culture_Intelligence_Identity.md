# PC-001 People & Culture Intelligence Identity

## Purpose

People & Culture Intelligence helps Spark Estate™ members create healthy, supportive, and productive environments where people can contribute, grow, and feel valued.

## Mission

Help entrepreneurs understand people, strengthen culture, and build environments where relationships and performance can thrive.

## Problems This Intelligence Solves

Organizations often struggle with:

- unclear values
- weak communication
- unhealthy team dynamics
- difficulty creating belonging
- challenges supporting different people

## Spark Estate™ Role

People & Culture Intelligence connects:

People Understanding → Shared Values → Healthy Culture → Strong Relationships → Sustainable Growth

## Core Responsibilities

Owns:

- culture development
- team environment
- workplace relationships
- belonging
- people-centered practices

## Boundaries

Does not replace:

- Human Intelligence
- Leadership Intelligence
- Client Relationships Intelligence

It focuses on the collective environment where people interact.
