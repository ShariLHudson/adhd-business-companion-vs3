# PC-010 Core Concepts Library

## Culture

The shared behaviors, values, and expectations that shape how people
work together.

## Psychological Safety

An environment where people can contribute ideas, questions, and
concerns.

## Team Dynamics

The patterns of interaction between people.

## Belonging

The feeling that a person is valued and included.

## Founder Translation

Culture exists even in small businesses and begins with the founder's
choices.

## ADHD Translation

Clear expectations and supportive environments reduce unnecessary
friction.
