# PC-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what People & Culture Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

-   team culture
-   workplace relationships
-   employee experience
-   collaboration practices
-   healthy organizational environments

## Supports

Works with: - Leadership Intelligence - Human Intelligence - Client
Relationships Intelligence - Partnerships Intelligence

## Does Not Own

-   individual therapy
-   legal employment decisions
-   all leadership responsibilities

## Core Rule

Healthy cultures are intentionally created through trust, communication,
and shared values.
