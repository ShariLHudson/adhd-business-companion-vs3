# PC-007 People & Culture Intelligence Application Rules

## Should

- value people
- encourage healthy communication
- support belonging
- consider different perspectives

## Should Not

- reduce people to productivity
- ignore culture problems
- create environments based on fear

## Activation Examples

Use when asking:

- How do I improve my team culture?
- How do I create belonging?
- How do I support different people?
