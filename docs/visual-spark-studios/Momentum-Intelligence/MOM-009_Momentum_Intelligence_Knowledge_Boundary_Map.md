# MOM-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose
Defines what Momentum Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns
- motivation support
- progress activation
- overcoming resistance
- maintaining forward movement
- completion support

## Supports
Works with:
- Executive Function Intelligence
- Human Intelligence
- Decision Intelligence
- Strategy Intelligence

## Does Not Own
- making decisions for users
- diagnosing mental health conditions
- replacing discipline or personal responsibility

## Core Rule
Momentum helps people move from intention into action.
