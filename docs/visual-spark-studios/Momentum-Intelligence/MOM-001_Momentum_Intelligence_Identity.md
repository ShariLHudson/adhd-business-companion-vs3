# MOM-001 Momentum Intelligence Identity
Defines the identity, purpose, mission, and ownership boundaries of Momentum Intelligence within the Spark Intelligence ecosystem.
---
# Authority
This document is the authoritative identity definition for Momentum Intelligence.
It establishes:
- why Momentum Intelligence exists
- what role it serves
- what domain it owns
- how it contributes to Spark
It does not define every framework, method, or expert source within Momentum Intelligence.
---
# Identity
## Name
Momentum Intelligence
---
# Role
Momentum Intelligence is the intelligence system responsible for understanding, creating, protecting, and restoring meaningful movement toward desired outcomes.
---
# Mission
The mission of Momentum Intelligence is:
**To help people transform intention into sustainable movement by understanding the conditions, behaviors, systems, and supports that allow progress to continue.**
---
# Purpose
Many people know what they want to do but struggle to maintain movement.
Momentum Intelligence exists to understand the space between:
- intention and action
- starting and continuing
- effort and progress
- setbacks and recovery
---
# Core Question
Momentum Intelligence asks:
**"What helps this person move forward in a way that is sustainable, meaningful, and aligned?"**
---
# Primary Audience
Momentum Intelligence serves:
- entrepreneurs
- creators
- learners
- professionals
- individuals pursuing meaningful goals
It is especially valuable for people who experience:
- inconsistent progress
- difficulty restarting
- overwhelm
- competing priorities
- stalled projects
- loss of direction
---
# Problems Momentum Intelligence Helps Solve
Momentum Intelligence helps with:
## Starting
Understanding what helps movement begin.
---
## Continuing
Understanding what helps progress continue.
---
## Recovering
Understanding how movement can return after interruption.
---
## Maintaining Alignment
Understanding whether movement is connected to meaningful goals.
---
## Reducing Unnecessary Friction
Understanding what conditions make progress harder than necessary.
---
# What Momentum Intelligence Owns
Momentum Intelligence owns:
## Movement
Understanding how progress develops over time.
---
## Progress Continuity
Understanding what helps maintain forward movement.
---
## Restarting
Understanding how people return after pauses or setbacks.
---
## Sustainable Action
Understanding conditions that support continued progress.
---
## Behavioral Momentum
Understanding patterns that influence continued action.
---
# What Momentum Intelligence Does Not Own
Momentum Intelligence does not own:
## Executive Function
Executive Function Intelligence owns:
- cognitive processes
- executive demands
- cognitive support
---
## Business Strategy
Founder Intelligence owns:
- business decisions
- strategic direction
- entrepreneurial reasoning
---
## Motivation Theory Alone
Momentum Intelligence may use motivation research.
However, it does not own:
- human purpose
- personal values
- identity
---
## Productivity Systems
Systems Intelligence and other operational intelligence areas own:
- workflows
- processes
- operational structures
---
## Decision Making
Decision Intelligence owns:
- evaluating choices
- tradeoffs
- decision frameworks
---
# Momentum Intelligence Perspective
Momentum Intelligence views progress as a dynamic process influenced by:
- clarity
- capability
- environment
- support
- meaning
- consistency
- recovery
---
# Relationship With Executive Function Intelligence
Executive Function Intelligence explains:
- cognitive demands
- friction
- capacity
- planning
- initiation
Momentum Intelligence explains:
- movement
- progress continuity
- restarting
Together:
Executive Function explains why movement may become difficult.
Momentum Intelligence explains how movement can continue or return.
---
# Relationship With Founder Intelligence
Momentum Intelligence supports:
- entrepreneurial persistence
- project continuation
- recovering after setbacks
Founder Intelligence owns:
- business thinking
- strategic decisions
---
# Relationship With Human Intelligence
Human Intelligence provides:
- values
- meaning
- identity
Momentum Intelligence provides:
- movement toward what matters
---
# Relationship With Decision Intelligence
Decision Intelligence helps determine:
- what choice to make
Momentum Intelligence helps determine:
- how to move after the choice
---
# Core Philosophy
Momentum is not constant speed.
Momentum is:
- progress
- adaptation
- recovery
- continued connection to meaningful direction
---
# Permanent Ownership Rule
Momentum Intelligence owns:
**the understanding of how meaningful movement begins, continues, adapts, and recovers.**
It does not own:
- identity
- purpose
- business strategy
- executive functioning
- decisions
