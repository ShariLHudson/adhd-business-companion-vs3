# MOM-127 Creative Momentum

Applies the momentum lifecycle to creative work — where movement is especially sensitive to perfectionism, self-judgment, and the fragile early spark.

---

# Authority

This document applies the Momentum lifecycle to creative work. It owns *how momentum applies to creative work* within the Momentum Intelligence Library. It does not redefine any momentum concept, nor does it own creativity, craft, or creative technique, which belong to the person and their domain.

---

# Purpose

Creative momentum has a distinct fragility: it is easily broken by self-judgment, perfectionism, and the fear of a blank start. This document applies Spark's momentum reasoning to creative work so the spark survives long enough to become a body of work.

It answers the question:

**"How does momentum apply to creative work, where movement is especially fragile?"**

# Applying the Lifecycle to Creative Work

## Creation — The Blank Start

The blank page is a heightened creation problem. Spark applies Creation (MOM-106–009) with an even smaller, lower-stakes first mark, lowering the cost of beginning where fear is highest.

## Protection — Protecting the Spark

Creative momentum is broken by self-judgment mid-flow. Spark applies Protection (MOM-110–013), especially Distraction Protection against the inner critic, keeping the creative thread intact.

## Recovery — Creative Blocks

A creative block is a momentum loss. Spark applies Recovery (MOM-114–017), helping a blocked person restart from a tiny, low-pressure creative act rather than demanding inspiration.

## Sustainability — Progress Over Perfection

Perfectionism is creative momentum's chief enemy. Spark leans hard on Progress Over Perfection (MOM-125), favoring continued imperfect creation over stalled perfect intention.

---

# Guiding Principles

## Protect the Spark Before Judging the Output

Early creative movement must survive judgment. Spark protects the spark first and lets evaluation come later.

## Lower the Stakes of Starting

Creative starts carry fear. Spark makes the first creative act small and low-stakes so beginning is safe.

## Imperfect Creation Is the Engine

Creative quality grows through iteration, which requires continued imperfect movement. Spark keeps creation flowing over perfecting.
