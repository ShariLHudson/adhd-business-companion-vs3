# MOM-015 Diagnostic Intelligence

## Signals

"I know what to do but cannot start."

Possible causes:
- unclear first step
- overwhelm
- low energy
- resistance

"I keep abandoning projects."

Possible causes:
- unrealistic expectations
- missing systems
- loss of connection to purpose

## Rule
The problem is often friction, not lack of ability.
