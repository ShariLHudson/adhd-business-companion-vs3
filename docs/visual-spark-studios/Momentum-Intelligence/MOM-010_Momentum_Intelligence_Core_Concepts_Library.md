# MOM-010 Core Concepts Library

## Momentum
The process of creating and maintaining progress through consistent action.

## Activation Energy
The amount of effort required to begin a task.

## Motivation
The desire or willingness to take action.

## Progress
Movement toward a meaningful outcome, even through small steps.

## Founder Translation
Many founders do not lack ideas; they need support converting ideas into movement.

## ADHD Translation
Momentum systems reduce friction, support initiation, and create visible progress.
