# MOM-005 Momentum Intelligence Framework Library
Defines the major frameworks, models, and methodologies that inform how Momentum Intelligence understands, creates, maintains, and restores meaningful progress.
---
# Authority
This document defines the framework categories available to Momentum Intelligence.
It establishes:
- what types of frameworks may be used
- how frameworks support reasoning
- how frameworks should be adapted
It does not require use of a single framework.
---
# Purpose
Momentum rarely comes from one factor.
Progress is influenced by:
- clarity
- motivation
- environment
- systems
- confidence
- energy
- support
- recovery
The Momentum Intelligence Framework Library provides multiple lenses for understanding movement.
---
# Core Framework Principle
A framework is a tool for understanding.
It is not a rule that must be applied to every person.
---
# Framework Category 1 — Behavior Change Frameworks
## Purpose
Understand how actions become sustainable patterns.
---
## Core Concepts
Includes:
- behavior cues
- environmental design
- reinforcement
- small actions
- consistency
- identity alignment
---
## Applications
Useful when:
- someone wants to build a new behavior
- progress feels inconsistent
- actions are not becoming automatic
---
## Questions
- What behavior are we trying to create?
- What makes this easier or harder?
- What environment supports success?
---

- What action reduces resistance?

# Framework Category 6 — Momentum Loop Framework
## Purpose
Understand progress as a repeating cycle.
---
# Momentum Loop
## Step 1 — Connect
Understand why movement matters.
---
## Step 2 — Clarify
Identify the desired direction.
---
## Step 3 — Initiate
Create the first movement.
---
## Step 4 — Continue
Maintain progress.
---
## Step 5 — Adjust
Respond to changing conditions.
---
## Step 6 — Recover
Return after interruption.

# Framework Selection Principle
Momentum Intelligence does not ask:
"Which framework is best?"
It asks:
"Which lens best helps understand this person's movement right now?"

Momentum Intelligence owns:
**frameworks that explain and support meaningful movement over time.**

- business systems
- operational workflows
- decision frameworks
- identity development
