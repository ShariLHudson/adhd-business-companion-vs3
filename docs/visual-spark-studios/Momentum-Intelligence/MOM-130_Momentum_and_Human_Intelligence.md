# MOM-130 Momentum and Human Intelligence

Defines the collaboration between Momentum Intelligence and Human Intelligence. This is a relationship document. It defines how the two libraries work together and never redefines either.

---

# Authority

This document describes a collaboration. It owns neither library's concepts. Momentum concepts remain owned by the Momentum Intelligence Library; the person's broader experience remains owned by Human Intelligence. Where the two appear to overlap, this document clarifies the boundary rather than merging it.

---

# Why These Libraries Collaborate

Momentum is carried by a person, and it can only be supported well if the person is understood. Human Intelligence understands the whole person — their state, energy, values, and life. Momentum Intelligence understands meaningful movement. Movement cannot be paced, protected, or recovered without reading the person carrying it, which is why these two libraries are among the closest collaborators in the ecosystem.

---

# What Information Is Exchanged

- **Human Intelligence → Momentum:** the person's current energy and capacity, emotional state, values (what makes movement meaningful), and life context. Momentum consumes this to pace, protect, and recover movement appropriately.
- **Momentum → Human Intelligence:** observations of movement, stalling, rhythm, and momentum health that contribute to the broader understanding of how the person is doing.

Information flows both ways; ownership does not.

---

# What Each Library Owns

- **Momentum Intelligence owns:** meaningful movement, momentum creation, protection, recovery, continuity, sustainable momentum, momentum patterns and recommendations.
- **Human Intelligence owns:** the whole person — identity, values, energy, emotional experience, wellbeing, and life context.

---

# What Each Library Never Owns

- **Momentum never owns:** the person's values, energy, emotional state, or wellbeing. It reads these from Human Intelligence and never defines them.
- **Human Intelligence never owns:** the definition or reasoning of meaningful movement. It references Momentum for that.

---

# How the Collaboration Improves Reasoning

Reading the person makes momentum support humane and accurate: pace matches real capacity, recovery matches real depletion, and meaning is drawn from the person's actual values rather than assumed. In return, momentum observations enrich the picture of how the person is doing. Neither library could serve the person well alone; together they let Spark support movement in a way that fits the human carrying it.

---

# Guiding Principles

## Read the Person Before Pacing the Movement

Momentum defers to Human Intelligence for the person's state and never overrides it with a movement goal.

## Meaning Comes From the Person

What makes movement meaningful is the person's values, owned by Human Intelligence. Momentum applies that meaning; it does not define it.

## The Person Outranks the Progress

When the person's wellbeing and the work's progress conflict, the person (via Human Intelligence) takes precedence.

---

# Common Mistakes

Avoid:

- Momentum defining the person's energy, emotion, or values
- Human Intelligence redefining meaningful movement
- optimizing movement at the expense of the person
- merging the two libraries instead of clarifying the boundary
