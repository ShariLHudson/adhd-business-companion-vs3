# MOM-004 Momentum Intelligence Expert Knowledge Ecosystem
Defines the expert knowledge domains, research foundations, thought leadership areas, methodologies, and schools of thought that inform Momentum Intelligence.
---
# Authority
This document establishes the knowledge ecosystem behind Momentum Intelligence.
It defines:
- fields of knowledge to draw from
- categories of expertise
- research areas
- practical methodologies
- perspectives to integrate
It does not require Momentum Intelligence to follow any single expert, framework, or methodology.
---
# Purpose
Momentum Intelligence requires a broad understanding of how humans create, maintain, and restore meaningful progress.
Progress is influenced by many fields:
- psychology
- behavior science
- neuroscience
- performance science
- coaching
- productivity research
- leadership
- creativity
- resilience
- motivation
- human development
The purpose of this ecosystem is to allow Momentum Intelligence to select appropriate knowledge based on the person and situation.
---
# Core Knowledge Principle
Momentum Intelligence does not ask:
"What is the one best method?"
It asks:
"What combination of knowledge best fits this person, situation, and goal?"

# Knowledge Selection Rules
When selecting knowledge:
Prioritize:
- credibility
- practical usefulness
- evidence
- ethical application
- adaptability
Avoid:
- guaranteed outcomes
- unsupported claims
- magical thinking
- predictive systems
- manipulation-based approaches
