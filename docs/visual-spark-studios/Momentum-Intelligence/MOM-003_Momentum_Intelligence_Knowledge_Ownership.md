# MOM-003 Momentum Intelligence Knowledge Ownership
Defines the knowledge domains, expertise areas, responsibilities, and boundaries owned by Momentum Intelligence within the Spark Intelligence ecosystem.
---
# Authority
This document is the authoritative ownership definition for Momentum Intelligence.
It establishes:
- what knowledge Momentum Intelligence contains
- what areas it supports
- what areas belong to other Intelligence Libraries
It does not contain the complete expert knowledge ecosystem or framework library.
---
# Purpose
Momentum Intelligence requires a clear understanding of its responsibility.
Its role is not simply to encourage action.
Its role is to understand the systems, conditions, behaviors, and experiences that influence sustainable movement toward meaningful outcomes.
---
# Core Ownership Statement
Momentum Intelligence owns:
**The knowledge of how people create, maintain, restore, and adapt meaningful progress over time.**
---
# Primary Knowledge Domains
Momentum Intelligence owns the following areas:
---
# 1. Progress Psychology
Purpose:
Understand the human experience of moving toward desired outcomes.
Includes:
- progress perception
- forward movement
- achievement patterns
- progress tracking
- recognizing advancement
- maintaining connection to goals
---
# 2. Behavioral Change
Purpose:
Understand how actions become consistent patterns.
Includes:
- behavior formation
- behavior reinforcement
- environmental influence
- action consistency
- reducing resistance to action
Momentum Intelligence uses behavior knowledge to support movement.
It does not own habit systems as a productivity replacement.
---
# 3. Motivation and Activation
Purpose:
Understand factors that influence willingness and readiness for action.
Includes:
- intrinsic motivation
- meaningful goals
- engagement
- interest
- purpose alignment
- activation barriers
Momentum Intelligence recognizes motivation as a dynamic condition.
It does not reduce movement problems to motivation alone.
---
# 4. Goal Pursuit
Purpose:
Understand how intentions become progress.
Includes:
- goal connection
- goal clarity
- action pathways
- progress milestones
- adjustment during pursuit
---
# 5. Momentum Creation
Purpose:
Understand how movement begins.
Includes:
- overcoming inertia
- reducing barriers
- creating early wins
- establishing traction
---
# 6. Momentum Maintenance
Purpose:
Understand how progress continues.
Includes:
- consistency
- sustainable pace
- maintaining engagement
- adapting systems
---
# 7. Momentum Recovery
Purpose:
Understand how people return after interruption.
Includes:
- restarting
- recovering after setbacks
- rebuilding confidence
- reconnecting with goals
---
# 8. Progress Systems
Purpose:
Understand structures that support continued movement.
Includes:
- progress tracking
- accountability structures
- reflection systems
- feedback loops
---
# 9. Resilience and Adaptation
Purpose:
Understand continuing movement through difficulty.
Includes:
- persistence
- recovery
- adjustment
- learning from setbacks
---
# 10. Human Performance Principles
Purpose:
Understand conditions that support effective action.
Includes:
- energy management
- focus conditions
- sustainable performance
- recovery cycles
---

Momentum Intelligence collaborates with other intelligence systems.

Momentum supports continued execution.
---

---
# What Momentum Intelligence Does Not Own
---
# Productivity Systems
Does not own:
- task management tools
- workflow design
- operational systems
Owned by:
- Systems Intelligence
- Project Management Intelligence
---
# Business Strategy
Does not own:
- market decisions
- business models
- competitive strategy
Owned by:
- Founder Intelligence
- Strategy Intelligence
---
# Personal Identity
Does not own:
- values
- personality
- life purpose
Owned by:
- Human Intelligence
---
# Clinical or Mental Health Treatment
Does not own:
- diagnosis
- treatment models
- medical decisions
---
# Decision Ownership
Does not own:
- choosing between options
- evaluating risks
Owned by:
- Decision Intelligence
---
# Momentum Intelligence Knowledge Questions
Momentum Intelligence seeks to answer:
- What creates movement?
- What interrupts movement?
- What helps movement return?
- What conditions support consistency?
- What makes progress sustainable?
- How can effort become meaningful progress?
---
# Knowledge Application Principle
Momentum Intelligence does not ask:
"How do we force productivity?"
It asks:
"What conditions allow meaningful progress to happen?"
---
# Permanent Ownership Rule
Momentum Intelligence owns:
**the understanding of progress, movement, activation, continuation, recovery, and sustainable action.**
It does not own:
- executive processes
- business strategy
- identity
- decisions
- operational systems
