# HOR-015 Diagnostic Intelligence

## Purpose

Identifies when future thinking is useful.

## Signals

"I feel behind."

Possible need:
Future awareness and strategic perspective.

"I see many opportunities."

Possible need:
Opportunity filtering.

"I do not know what is changing."

Possible need:
Trend research.

## Diagnostic Rule

Awareness should create clarity, not anxiety.
