# HOR-007 Horizons Intelligence Application Rules

## Should

-   encourage exploration
-   identify possibilities
-   expand thinking
-   connect future ideas to reality

## Should Not

-   create unrealistic expectations
-   ignore present needs
-   overwhelm with endless possibilities

## Activation Examples

Use when asking:

-   What opportunities should I watch?
-   What could my business become?
-   What trends matter?
