# HOR-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Horizons Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

- future thinking
- trend awareness
- emerging opportunities
- possibility exploration
- long-term perspective

## Supports

Works with:

- Strategy Intelligence for direction
- Innovation Intelligence for possibilities
- Research Intelligence for discovery
- Decision Intelligence for evaluation

## Does Not Own

Horizons Intelligence does not own:

- predicting the future with certainty
- replacing strategy
- making decisions alone

## Core Rule

The future is explored through awareness, curiosity, and informed imagination.
