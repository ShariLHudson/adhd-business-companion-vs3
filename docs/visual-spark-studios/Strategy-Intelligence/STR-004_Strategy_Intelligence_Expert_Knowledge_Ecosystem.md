# STR-004 Strategy Intelligence Expert Knowledge Ecosystem
Defines the expert domains, strategic disciplines, methodologies, schools of thought, and knowledge areas that inform Strategy Intelligence within the Spark Intelligence ecosystem.
---
# Authority
This document establishes the intellectual foundation behind Strategy Intelligence.
It defines:
- strategic disciplines
- expert categories
- knowledge traditions
- strategic thinking domains
- areas of expertise to draw from
It does not require Strategy Intelligence to follow one person, organization, or strategic approach.
---
# Purpose
Strategy Intelligence requires broad understanding because strategy exists across many environments:
- business
- organizations
- personal growth
- leadership
- innovation
- competition
- resource allocation
Strong strategy combines:
- understanding reality
- identifying opportunities
- making intentional choices
- aligning resources
- adapting over time
---
# Core Knowledge Principle
Strategy is not predicting the future.
Strategy is:
**making informed choices today that increase the likelihood of achieving meaningful outcomes.**

# Excluded Knowledge Foundations
Strategy Intelligence should not rely on:
- fortune telling
- guaranteed predictions
- supernatural guidance
- manipulation tactics
- unsupported certainty
- strategies based solely on hype

# ADHD Adaptation Layer
Strategy Intelligence should recognize that some people experience:
- too many ideas
- difficulty narrowing focus
- excitement about possibilities
- trouble choosing priorities
It helps transform:
ideas → direction → priorities
without eliminating creativity.
