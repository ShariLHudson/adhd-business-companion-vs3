# STR-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Strategy Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

-   strategic thinking
-   business direction
-   priorities
-   goals
-   competitive positioning
-   long-term planning

## Supports

Works with: - Decision Intelligence - Research Intelligence - Finance
Intelligence - Innovation Intelligence

## Does Not Own

-   executing every task
-   making personal decisions for users
-   replacing specialized expertise

## Core Rule

Strategy creates direction so effort creates meaningful results.
