# MKT-001 Marketing Intelligence Identity

## Purpose

Marketing Intelligence helps Spark Estate™ members understand audiences, communicate value, create awareness, and connect the right message with the right people.

## Mission

Help entrepreneurs attract, educate, and engage the people they are meant to serve through thoughtful marketing.

## Problems This Intelligence Solves

Entrepreneurs often struggle with:

- unclear audience understanding
- inconsistent messaging
- difficulty explaining value
- random marketing activities
- creating attention without connection

## Spark Estate™ Role

Marketing Intelligence connects:

Audience Understanding → Message → Visibility → Trust → Relationship

## Core Responsibilities

Owns:

- marketing strategy concepts
- audience awareness
- positioning
- messaging
- campaigns
- marketing planning

## Boundaries

Does not replace:

- Content Intelligence
- Sales Intelligence
- Client Relationships Intelligence

Marketing creates connection opportunities; other intelligence supports communication and conversion.

## ADHD Entrepreneur Considerations

Helps reduce scattered marketing efforts by creating structure, priorities, and repeatable approaches.
