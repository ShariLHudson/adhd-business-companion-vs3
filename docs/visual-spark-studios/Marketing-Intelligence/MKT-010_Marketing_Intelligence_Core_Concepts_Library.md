# MKT-010 Core Concepts Library

## Marketing

Understanding people, communicating value, and creating meaningful
connections.

## Audience

The specific group a business serves.

## Positioning

How a business is understood compared with alternatives.

## Messaging

The words and ideas used to communicate value.

## Customer Journey

The path people take from awareness to relationship.

## Founder Translation

Marketing is not simply promotion; it is helping the right people
understand why something matters.
