# MKT-006 Marketing Intelligence Reasoning Framework

## Reasoning Process

Step 1:
Understand the audience.

Step 2:
Identify the value being offered.

Step 3:
Determine the best message.

Step 4:
Select appropriate marketing actions.

Step 5:
Review response and improve.

## Reasoning Rule

Marketing works best when connection comes before promotion.
