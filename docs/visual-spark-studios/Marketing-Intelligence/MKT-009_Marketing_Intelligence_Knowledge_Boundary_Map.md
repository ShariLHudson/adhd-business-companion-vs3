# MKT-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Marketing Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

-   audience understanding
-   positioning
-   messaging strategy
-   awareness creation
-   marketing planning concepts

## Supports

Works with: - Content Intelligence for communication - Sales
Intelligence for conversion - Client Relationships Intelligence for
retention - Strategy Intelligence for direction

## Does Not Own

Marketing Intelligence does not own: - closing sales - creating all
content - business strategy alone - customer relationships after
purchase

## Core Rule

Marketing connects the right message with the right people at the right
time.
