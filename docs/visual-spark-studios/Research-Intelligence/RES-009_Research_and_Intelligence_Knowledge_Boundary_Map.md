# RES-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Research Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

-   information discovery
-   research planning
-   source evaluation
-   evidence gathering
-   investigation methods

## Supports

Works with: - Knowledge Management Intelligence - Data & Analytics
Intelligence - Strategy Intelligence - Decision Intelligence

## Does Not Own

-   final decisions
-   storing all knowledge
-   creating strategy alone

## Core Rule

Research creates understanding through reliable information.
