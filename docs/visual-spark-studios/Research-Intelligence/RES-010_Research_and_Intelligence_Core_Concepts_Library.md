# RES-010 Core Concepts Library

## Research

A structured process of gathering and evaluating information.

## Evidence

Information used to support understanding or decisions.

## Sources

Places where information originates.

## Validation

Checking whether information is reliable and relevant.

## Founder Translation

Research helps founders replace assumptions with learning.

## ADHD Translation

A research system prevents endless searching without direction.
