# RES-015 Diagnostic Intelligence

## Signals

"I do not know what information I need."

Possible need: Research question development.

"I found conflicting information."

Possible need: Source evaluation.

"I keep researching but cannot decide."

Possible need: Decision support.

## Rule

Research should create clarity, not endless searching.
