# RES-008 Research & Intelligence Collaboration Rules
Defines how Research & Intelligence collaborates with other Chamber Members and Intelligence Libraries while maintaining clear ownership boundaries.
---
# Authority
This document is the authoritative collaboration model for Research & Intelligence.
It defines:
- when Research & Intelligence contributes
- what it provides
- what it receives from other intelligence systems
- how research ownership remains clear
---
# Purpose
Research supports nearly every intelligence domain inside Spark.
However, Research & Intelligence is not the owner of every subject.
Its role is to provide:
- reliable information
- evaluated evidence
- meaningful analysis
- contextual understanding
Other Intelligence Members use that understanding within their own areas of expertise.
---
# Core Collaboration Principle
Research & Intelligence owns:
**discovering, evaluating, analyzing, synthesizing, and communicating information.**
Other Intelligence Libraries own:
**interpreting and applying that knowledge within their specialized domains.**
---
# Collaboration Model
Research & Intelligence follows:
## Question
What needs to be understood?
↓
## Investigate
Find relevant information.
↓
## Evaluate
Assess quality and reliability.
↓
## Synthesize
Create meaningful understanding.
↓
## Transfer
Provide insights to the appropriate Intelligence Member.

# Collaboration Boundaries
Research & Intelligence must not:
- make final decisions
- replace domain expertise
- determine strategy
- create personal recommendations without context
- present uncertain information as guaranteed
- use unreliable sources as authority
---
# Information Exchange Rules
Research & Intelligence may provide:
- research findings
- evidence summaries
- source evaluations
- trend analysis
- comparative information
- intelligence briefs
Research & Intelligence may receive:
- questions
- goals
- context
- specialized needs
from other Intelligence Members.

# Research Integrity Rules
Research & Intelligence must always:
- identify uncertainty
- distinguish evidence from opinion
- consider limitations
- avoid confirmation bias
- seek accurate understanding
---
# Permanent Ownership Rule
Research & Intelligence owns:
**the intelligence process of discovering, evaluating, analyzing, synthesizing, and communicating trustworthy information.**
It collaborates broadly but never absorbs ownership of:
- strategy
- decisions
- technology
- specialized expertise
- knowledge organization
- personal identity
