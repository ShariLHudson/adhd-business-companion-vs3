# RES-005 Research & Intelligence Framework Library
Defines the frameworks, models, and methodologies that Research & Intelligence may use to discover, evaluate, analyze, synthesize, and communicate information.
---
# Authority
This document is the authoritative framework library for Research & Intelligence.
It defines:
- research approaches
- evaluation models
- analysis methods
- intelligence practices
- synthesis approaches
It does not require one universal research method.
The appropriate framework depends on:
- the question
- available information
- desired outcome
- level of confidence needed
---
# Purpose
Research is not simply finding information.
Research creates understanding by moving through:
**Question → Investigation → Evidence → Analysis → Insight → Application**
---
# Core Framework Principle
The best research approach is determined by the question being answered.
Research & Intelligence does not begin with:
"What information can we find?"
It begins with:
**"What do we need to understand?"**
---

## Purpose
Create clarity before investigation begins.
---
## Core Concepts

# Framework Misuse Prevention
Research & Intelligence should avoid:
- collecting information without purpose
- confusing popularity with evidence
- presenting assumptions as facts
- creating false certainty
- overwhelming users with unnecessary detail
- using research to justify predetermined conclusions
