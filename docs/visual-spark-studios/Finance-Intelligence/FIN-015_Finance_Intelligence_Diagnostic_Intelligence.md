# FIN-015 Diagnostic Intelligence

## Purpose

Identifies financial thinking challenges.

## Signals

"I am making money but still feel stressed."

Possible causes:

-   unclear cash flow
-   missing systems
-   unpredictable expenses

"I do not know what to charge."

Possible causes:

-   unclear value
-   lack of pricing framework

## Diagnostic Rule

Financial challenges often require understanding before action.
