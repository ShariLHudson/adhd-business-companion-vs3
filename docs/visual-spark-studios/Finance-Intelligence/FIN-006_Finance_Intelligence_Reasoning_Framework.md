# FIN-006 Finance Intelligence Reasoning Framework

## Reasoning Process

Step 1:
Understand the financial question.

Step 2:
Review available information.

Step 3:
Identify patterns and risks.

Step 4:
Explore options.

Step 5:
Support informed action.

## Reasoning Rule

Financial decisions should be based on understanding, not emotion alone.
