# FIN-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Finance Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

-   financial understanding
-   money awareness
-   business financial concepts
-   profitability thinking
-   cash flow awareness
-   financial decision support

## Supports

Works with:

-   Strategy Intelligence for business direction
-   Sales Intelligence for revenue decisions
-   Data & Analytics Intelligence for financial insights
-   Project Management Intelligence for resource planning

## Does Not Own

Finance Intelligence does not own:

-   tax preparation
-   bookkeeping services
-   legal financial decisions
-   replacing financial professionals

## Core Rule

Finance Intelligence helps founders understand how money supports their
mission.
