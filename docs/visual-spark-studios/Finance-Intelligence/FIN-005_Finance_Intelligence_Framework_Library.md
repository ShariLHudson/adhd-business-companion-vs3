# FIN-005 Finance Intelligence Framework Library

## Financial Awareness Framework

Purpose:
Understand the current financial picture.

Steps:

1. Gather information
2. Organize categories
3. Identify patterns
4. Determine priorities

## Profitability Framework

Review:

- revenue
- costs
- margins
- opportunities

## Money Decision Framework

Ask:

- What is the goal?
- What are the costs?
- What are the possible outcomes?
- What information is needed?

## ADHD Application

Creates simple external systems to reduce avoidance and overwhelm.
