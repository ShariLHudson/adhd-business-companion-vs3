# FIN-010 Core Concepts Library

## Revenue

Money generated from products or services.

## Expenses

Resources required to operate a business.

## Profitability

Understanding whether the business creates sustainable financial value.

## Cash Flow

The movement of money into and out of a business.

## Pricing

The process of determining value exchange.

## Founder Translation

Financial knowledge helps founders make intentional decisions instead of
guessing.

## ADHD Translation

Clear financial systems reduce uncertainty and emotional stress around
money.
