# NET-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Networking Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

- relationship building
- professional connections
- networking strategy
- community development
- relationship opportunities

## Supports

Works with:

- Partnerships Intelligence for strategic relationships
- Client Relationships Intelligence for relationship growth
- Marketing Intelligence for visibility
- Sales Intelligence for business development

## Does Not Own

Networking Intelligence does not own:

- selling directly
- manipulating relationships
- replacing genuine connection

## Core Rule

Networking is about creating meaningful relationships, not collecting contacts.
