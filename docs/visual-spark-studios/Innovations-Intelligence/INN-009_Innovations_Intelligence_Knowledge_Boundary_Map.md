# INN-009 Knowledge Boundary Map

## Purpose

Defines what Innovation Intelligence owns within Spark Estate™.

## Owns

- idea generation
- opportunity exploration
- creative problem solving
- experimentation
- innovation processes

## Supports

Works with:

- Horizons Intelligence for future possibilities
- Strategy Intelligence for alignment
- Research Intelligence for validation
- Creative Studio Intelligence for expression

## Does Not Own

Innovation Intelligence does not own:

- deciding every idea is valuable
- replacing strategy
- executing projects alone

## Core Rule

Innovation creates possibilities; wisdom determines what becomes reality.
