# 409 — AI / Intelligence Canonical Knowledge Manifest

## Purpose

Define the authoritative knowledge domains, retrieval ownership, and maintenance boundaries for the AI / Intelligence Chamber member.

## Canonical scope

AI / Intelligence owns knowledge and reasoning about:

- AI capabilities and limits
- model classes and use cases
- prompting and instruction design
- retrieval-augmented generation
- context design
- agentic workflows
- automation with AI
- multimodal AI
- evaluation and benchmarking
- hallucination and confidence
- human-in-the-loop design
- privacy and data handling in AI use
- AI governance and responsible deployment
- AI adoption and workflow redesign
- current AI product and model developments
- practical AI literacy
- AI-assisted creation and decision support

## Explicit exclusions

AI / Intelligence does not own:

- infrastructure architecture — Technology Intelligence
- enterprise data governance — Data & Analytics
- product strategy — Strategy or Product Intelligence
- innovation portfolio management — Innovations Intelligence
- legal determinations — Legal Intelligence
- cybersecurity ownership — Technology or Risk & Resilience
- general research methodology — Research Intelligence
- platform-wide memory — canonical memory authority
- platform-wide retrieval runtime — shared retrieval authority

## Knowledge package types

- stable principles
- evolving practices
- current tool intelligence
- risk guidance
- decision frameworks
- diagnostic heuristics
- implementation patterns
- case patterns
- creation blueprints
- evaluation criteria

## Required metadata

Every canonical knowledge object must include:

```yaml
knowledge_id:
title:
domain:
subdomain:
knowledge_type:
stability:
evidence_quality:
freshness_required:
source_policy:
owner:
review_cycle:
known_limits:
related_capabilities:
```

## Stability classes

- durable
- slowly_evolving
- fast_evolving
- live_only

## Retrieval rule

Durable and slowly evolving knowledge may be retrieved from the member library.

Fast-evolving and live-only claims require current verification before presentation as fact.

## Maintenance

Review cycles:

- durable: annually
- slowly evolving: quarterly
- fast evolving: monthly
- live only: verified at use time
