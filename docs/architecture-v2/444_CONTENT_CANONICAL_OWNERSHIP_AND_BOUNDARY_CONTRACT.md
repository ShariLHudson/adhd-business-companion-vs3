# 444 — Content Canonical Ownership & Boundary Contract

## Content Intelligence owns

- written and spoken content structure
- clarity
- teaching quality
- narrative logic
- copy quality
- editing
- content architecture
- audience-fit of the content itself
- voice preservation
- content repurposing logic
- content systems
- content diagnostics
- content maintenance
- content-specific discoverability
- content performance learning

## Marketing Intelligence owns

- market understanding
- audience strategy
- positioning
- demand creation
- campaign strategy
- channel strategy
- acquisition goals
- marketing measurement

Marketing determines:

> What content should exist, for whom, and why?

Content determines:

> How should that content be structured, written, taught, edited, and maintained?

## Creative Studio owns

- visual design
- graphics
- layout
- imagery
- presentation production
- visual identity execution

Content defines message and structure.
Creative Studio defines visual treatment.

## Brand Intelligence owns

- canonical brand identity
- approved voice principles
- visual standards
- brand consistency

Content applies brand direction without becoming the brand owner.

## Research Intelligence owns

- research method
- information gathering
- evidence collection
- source evaluation

Content Intelligence uses verified research to create understandable material.

## Data & Analytics owns

- measurement systems
- metric validity
- analytics
- reporting
- attribution

Content interprets content performance only within approved measurement inputs.

## Knowledge Intelligence or Library Authority owns

- canonical reusable knowledge
- knowledge lifecycle
- authoritative business truth

Content may prepare knowledge candidates but does not create a second knowledge authority.

## Universal Create owns

- generation runtime
- saving
- editing
- versioning
- resume
- adaptation
- output identity

Content Intelligence supplies content-specific reasoning and quality rules.
