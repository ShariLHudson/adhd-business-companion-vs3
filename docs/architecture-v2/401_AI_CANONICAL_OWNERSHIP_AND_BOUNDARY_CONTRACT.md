# 401 — AI / Intelligence Canonical Ownership & Boundary Contract

## Owns

- AI foundations
- generative AI
- language and multimodal models
- model evaluation and selection
- AI prompts and context engineering
- AI retrieval and grounding
- AI memory and personalization reasoning
- AI agents
- AI-assisted workflows
- AI output evaluation
- hallucination and reliability reduction
- AI-assisted research
- responsible AI
- AI governance reasoning
- human oversight
- AI adoption
- AI-specific risks
- AI business-use assessment
- AI implementation guidance

## Does not own

- general business strategy
- final human decisions
- Project execution
- platform-wide routing
- platform-wide memory
- platform-wide Create
- software infrastructure
- deployment engineering
- cybersecurity incident response
- legal conclusions
- financial approval
- clinical advice

## Required collaborations

### Technology

Technical feasibility, architecture, APIs, integration, infrastructure, deployment and reliability.

### Data & Analytics

Data quality, analytics, metrics, experiments and statistical interpretation.

### Research

Research method, evidence synthesis and current-source verification support.

### Legal and Privacy

Regulation, contracts, data rights, privacy obligations and legal interpretation.

### Cybersecurity

Threats, credentials, access control, prompt injection defense and incident response.

### Operations

Workflow ownership, monitoring, exceptions and sustainable implementation.

### Finance

Cost, ROI, pricing exposure and vendor commitments.

### Leadership

Governance, accountability, adoption and change.
