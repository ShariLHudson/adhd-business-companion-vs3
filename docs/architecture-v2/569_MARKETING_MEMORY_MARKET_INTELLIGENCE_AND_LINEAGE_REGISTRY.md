# 569 — Marketing Memory, Market Intelligence & Lineage Registry

## Purpose

Preserve what Marketing has learned so campaigns, messages, audiences, channels, and offers improve over time.

## Remember

- target audiences
- audience language
- customer problems
- objections
- alternatives
- positioning decisions
- message variants
- channel performance
- campaign hypotheses
- experiments
- conversion evidence
- attribution limitations
- creative learnings
- seasonal effects
- customer-quality differences
- decisions and reasons
- review dates

## Do not store as permanent truth

- “This audience never buys.”
- “Email does not work.”
- “Social media is our best channel.”
- “This message failed.”
- “People only care about price.”

Store the test conditions, audience, timing, execution, evidence, confidence, and limitations.

## Market intelligence states

- signal
- observation
- hypothesis
- supported pattern
- validated insight
- context-specific insight
- disputed
- stale
- superseded

## Lineage

Connect:

- market insight
- positioning
- message
- campaign
- creative
- channel
- audience
- experiment
- result
- decision
- downstream customer quality

## Review triggers

- market shift
- new competitor
- changing customer language
- channel change
- declining performance
- offer change
- pricing change
- regulation change
- platform change
- repeated contradictory evidence
