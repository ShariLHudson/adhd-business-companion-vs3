# 420 — Client Relationship Ownership & Boundary Contract

## Client Relationship Intelligence owns

- how to build trust
- how to prevent expectation breakdown
- how to prepare for and recover from difficult interactions
- how to improve client experience
- how to notice relationship risk
- how to personalize communication
- how to strengthen follow-through
- how to design meaningful check-ins
- how to repair trust after mistakes
- how to create loyalty and referrals through genuine value

## Boundary with Sales Intelligence

Sales owns:

- qualification
- opportunity progression
- objections tied to purchase
- conversion strategy
- pipeline logic
- closing

Client Relationship owns:

- trust
- communication
- expectations
- relationship continuity
- post-sale experience
- repair
- retention

## Boundary with Marketing Intelligence

Marketing owns:

- positioning
- audience communication at scale
- campaigns
- messaging systems
- content distribution

Client Relationship owns:

- individual and small-group relationship experience
- communication fit
- trust and expectation management
- follow-up quality

## Boundary with Projects

Projects own:

- tasks
- milestones
- dates
- dependencies
- delivery execution

Client Relationship may identify a commitment or follow-up need, but does not become task management.

## Boundary with CRM

External CRM systems may store records and activity.

Client Relationship Intelligence interprets relationship meaning and guides better human interaction.
