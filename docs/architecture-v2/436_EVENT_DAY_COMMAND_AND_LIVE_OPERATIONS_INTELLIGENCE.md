# 436 — Event-Day Command & Live Operations Intelligence

## Purpose

Support calm, coordinated event delivery without turning Shari into an unsafe emergency authority.

## Event-day views

- now
- next
- later
- active issue
- owner
- backup
- attendee impact
- decision required

## Command roles

The Event Record may define:

- event lead
- host
- producer
- technology lead
- venue lead
- speaker liaison
- volunteer lead
- registration lead
- safety contact
- communications lead

## Live issue protocol

1. identify what happened
2. determine immediate safety implications
3. identify affected people and assets
4. activate the responsible owner
5. choose the least disruptive safe response
6. communicate clearly
7. record the decision
8. return to the run of show
9. debrief later

## Safety boundary

For emergencies or serious safety incidents:

- direct users to appropriate emergency services and venue authorities
- do not substitute platform guidance for trained responders
- preserve incident facts
- avoid speculative diagnosis

## ADHD event-day support

- one issue at a time
- short scripts
- visible anchors
- reduced choices
- sensory and recovery pauses
- exact re-entry point
