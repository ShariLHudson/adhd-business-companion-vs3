# 434 — Event Readiness, Dependency & Decision Intelligence

## Purpose

Make invisible event dependencies visible without overwhelming the user.

## Readiness dimensions

- purpose
- audience
- format
- date
- venue or platform
- budget
- program
- speakers
- registration
- promotion
- technology
- staffing
- accessibility
- safety
- attendee communication
- follow-up

## Readiness states

- unknown
- not_started
- in_progress
- blocked
- needs_decision
- ready
- not_applicable
- complete

## Dependency types

- decision dependency
- asset dependency
- external confirmation
- vendor dependency
- financial approval
- technical dependency
- legal or compliance review
- timing dependency
- people dependency

## Recommendation behavior

Show:

- what matters now
- the highest-impact unresolved decision
- what is blocked by it
- one recommended next step

Do not expose the full dependency graph by default.

## Decision deadline rule

Every time-sensitive decision should include:

- latest safe decision date
- consequence of delay
- fallback
- owner
