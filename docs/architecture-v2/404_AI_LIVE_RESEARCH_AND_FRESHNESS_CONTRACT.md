# 404 — AI Live Research & Freshness Contract

## Always research current information

- current models
- model capabilities
- pricing
- context limits
- APIs
- product availability
- integrations
- benchmarks
- laws
- regulations
- vendor policies
- security threats
- standards updates
- company claims
- recent research
- current OpenAI product guidance

## Stable knowledge may be retrieved from canonical files

- probabilistic generation
- general model limitations
- evaluation principles
- human oversight principles
- retrieval fundamentals
- context design principles
- durable governance concepts

## Research hierarchy

1. official government and regulator sources
2. standards bodies
3. original papers
4. official technical documentation
5. recognized research institutions
6. qualified practitioner material
7. vendor claims with bias identified
8. lived experience, labeled accordingly

## Required behavior

- verify exact source
- compare publication and event dates
- distinguish current findings from durable knowledge
- cite claims
- state uncertainty
- avoid remembered URLs
- avoid current recommendations from static knowledge
