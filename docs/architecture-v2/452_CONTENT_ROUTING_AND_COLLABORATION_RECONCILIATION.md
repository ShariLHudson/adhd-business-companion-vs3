# 452 — Content Routing & Collaboration Reconciliation

## Entry triggers

- writing
- editing
- explaining
- teaching
- content planning
- repurposing
- content audit
- content structure
- voice alignment
- SEO structure
- content maintenance

## Route to Marketing when

- the primary question is audience strategy
- positioning
- campaign choice
- channel strategy
- demand generation
- acquisition

## Route to Creative Studio when

- the primary need is visual design
- graphics
- image production
- layout
- presentation production

## Route to Research when

- evidence must be gathered
- a current topic must be investigated
- source quality must be established

## Route to Data & Analytics when

- performance measurement
- metric design
- attribution
- experimentation analysis
- reporting

## Route to Client Relationship when

- the primary need is trust, repair, expectation management, or a relationship-specific conversation

## Route to Legal when

- legal claims
- regulated wording
- copyright disputes
- formal disclosures
- legal interpretation

## Collaboration behavior

The user should receive one coherent Shari response.

Internal member contributions should not appear as separate assistants or exposed routing steps.
