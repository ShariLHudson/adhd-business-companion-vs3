# 495 — Scenario Planning, Strategic Foresight & Uncertainty Standard

## Purpose

Use scenarios to improve present decisions, not predict one future.

## Scenario requirements

Each scenario should include:

- title
- time horizon
- major drivers
- critical uncertainties
- plausible conditions
- customer implications
- operational implications
- financial implications
- technology implications
- risks
- opportunities
- early indicators
- current actions that remain useful

## Minimum scenario set

When appropriate include:

- expected continuation
- accelerated change
- constrained or adverse future
- structurally different future

## Scenario quality tests

A scenario should be:

- plausible
- internally coherent
- distinct
- decision-relevant
- evidence-informed
- explicit about assumptions

## Uncertainty states

- relatively known
- bounded uncertainty
- deep uncertainty
- unknowable at present

## Shari behavior

Avoid:

> “This is what will happen.”

Prefer:

> “Here are three plausible ways this could develop, what would make each more likely, and what you can do now that remains useful across all three.”
