# 555 — Practice, Reinforcement & Knowledge Transfer Standard

## Practice progression
1. observation
2. guided attempt
3. supported repetition
4. varied practice
5. independent use
6. delayed retrieval
7. real-world transfer

## Practice types
Recall, worked examples, completion exercises, simulations, decision scenarios, error correction, comparison, role play, teach-back, creation, and real-world application.

## Feedback structure
1. what worked
2. what needs adjustment
3. why it matters
4. what to try next

Feedback must be timely, specific, actionable, proportional, and encouraging without false praise.

## Reinforcement variables
- difficulty
- importance
- forgetting risk
- frequency of use
- confidence
- recent errors
- time since practice

## Transfer standard
Vary examples, change context, practice exceptions, connect to current work, and ask the user to identify when the skill should be used.

## Recovery
When practice goes poorly, reduce task size, restore support, explain differently, revisit prerequisites, change modality, and preserve evidence of what worked.
