# 392 — Started Member Status & Finish Order

## Status language

- Defined — mission or handoff exists
- Built — substantial knowledge files exist
- Integrated — routing, schemas, runtime, and creation behavior exist
- Certified — repository implementation and evidence pass
- Complete — certified and maintained

## Recommended finish order

### 1. AI / Intelligence

Why first:

- foundational collaborator across nearly every member
- establishes retrieval, confidence, model-use, and AI-boundary behavior
- errors here can propagate platform-wide

### 2. Client Relationship Intelligence

Why next:

- connects business knowledge, communication, service delivery, and Create
- has a strong existing source concept that must be fully integrated without becoming CRM

### 3. Events Intelligence

Why next:

- broadest real-world orchestration member already substantially developed
- excellent test of lifecycle, stakeholder, timeline, creation, and visual intelligence

### 4. Execution Manager

Why next:

- converts decisions and plans into forward movement
- must remain distinct from Projects and productivity systems

### 5. Marketing Intelligence

Why next:

- substantial bundle volume already exists
- requires consolidation, overlap review, and upload selection

### 6. Momentum Intelligence

Why next:

- closely related to execution but must retain distinct purpose
- must avoid duplicating motivation, productivity, and celebration behavior
