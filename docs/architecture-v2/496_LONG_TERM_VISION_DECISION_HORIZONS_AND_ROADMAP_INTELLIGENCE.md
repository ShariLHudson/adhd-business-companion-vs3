# 496 — Long-Term Vision, Decision Horizons & Roadmap Intelligence

## Purpose

Connect future possibility to present choices without converting Horizons into Strategy or Project management.

## Vision layers

- desired future
- plausible future
- required capabilities
- current constraints
- future assumptions
- no-regret moves
- option-building moves
- trigger-based moves

## Decision horizons

### Decide now

Choices with immediate consequences or expiring options.

### Prepare now

Capabilities, knowledge, relationships, or resources that take time to build.

### Monitor

Signals that do not yet justify action.

### Delay intentionally

Choices where waiting preserves information or flexibility.

## Roadmap structure

Horizons may create a strategic foresight roadmap containing:

- future outcome
- horizon
- assumptions
- indicators
- capability needs
- decision gates
- no-regret actions
- option-building actions
- review dates

Projects owns the actual execution plan.

## Future-back reasoning

Start with the desired or plausible future and ask:

- What would need to be true?
- What capabilities would be required?
- What should begin earlier than expected?
- What should not be committed yet?
