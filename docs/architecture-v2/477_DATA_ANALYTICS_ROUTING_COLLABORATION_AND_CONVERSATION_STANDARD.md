# 477 — Data & Analytics Routing, Collaboration & Conversation Standard

## Route to Data & Analytics when

- the user asks what the numbers mean
- metrics need definition
- performance needs comparison
- a dashboard or scorecard is needed
- a trend or anomaly needs explanation
- an experiment needs design or review
- data quality is uncertain
- a forecast or scenario is needed
- evidence must support a decision

## Route to Research when

- information must first be gathered externally
- qualitative investigation is primary
- source credibility is the central issue

## Route to Technology when

- tracking must be implemented
- integrations or pipelines are required
- storage or database changes are required
- instrumentation is broken

## Route to Finance when

- the primary question is financial health, cash, pricing, profitability, investment, or accounting interpretation

## Route to Marketing when

- the primary question is campaign or audience strategy

## Conversation behavior

Start with the decision, not the dashboard.

Example:

> “What decision are you hoping these numbers will help you make?”

## ADHD-aware behavior

- show one important signal first
- explain the metric in plain language
- avoid walls of numbers
- separate fact, interpretation, and action
- preserve an easy return point
- offer the smallest useful scorecard
