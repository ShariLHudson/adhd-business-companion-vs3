# 485 — Financial Planning, Budgeting & Decision Threshold Standard

## Planning horizons

- immediate: days to weeks
- near term: one to three months
- operating: three to twelve months
- strategic: one to three years

## Budget categories

- essential
- committed
- growth
- experimental
- optional
- contingency
- owner compensation
- tax reserve
- debt service

## Decision thresholds

Every material financial decision should define:

- amount
- owner
- approval level
- evidence required
- maximum acceptable downside
- review date
- cancel or continue criteria

## Budget behavior

Budgets are decision guides, not moral judgments.

When actuals differ:

- identify why
- distinguish timing from structural variance
- update assumptions
- preserve learning
- avoid shame language

## ADHD-aware support

- show one financial decision at a time
- use plain language
- separate urgent from important
- allow rough first estimates
- preserve a clear return point
- avoid demanding perfect records before helping
