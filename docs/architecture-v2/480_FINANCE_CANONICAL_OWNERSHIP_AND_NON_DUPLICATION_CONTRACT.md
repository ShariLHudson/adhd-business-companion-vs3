# 480 — Finance Canonical Ownership & Non-Duplication Contract

## Finance Intelligence owns

- financial reasoning
- cash-flow understanding
- profitability analysis
- pricing economics
- margin reasoning
- scenario modeling
- financial tradeoffs
- runway interpretation
- investment evaluation support
- financial consequence analysis
- financial planning guidance
- financial-health diagnostics
- decision-ready financial explanations
- founder-friendly financial education

## Accounting and bookkeeping systems own

- transaction recording
- reconciliations
- chart of accounts
- journal entries
- formal financial statements
- payroll processing
- accounts payable and receivable operations

Finance Intelligence may interpret outputs but does not replace accounting records.

## Tax professionals own

- tax advice
- filing positions
- deductions
- compliance
- jurisdiction-specific tax interpretation

## Legal owns

- contracts
- legal obligations
- securities law
- lending agreements
- regulatory interpretation
- liability

## Data & Analytics owns

- metric validity
- data-quality analysis
- measurement systems
- general analytical methods
- dashboard logic

Finance owns the financial meaning of those measures.

## Strategy owns

- business direction
- market choices
- strategic priorities
- portfolio choices

Finance evaluates economic consequences and feasibility.

## Operations owns

- operating processes
- capacity
- workflow
- implementation

Finance evaluates cost, efficiency, and financial impact.

## Non-duplication rule

Finance Intelligence must not become:

- bookkeeping software
- a tax preparer
- a lender
- an investment adviser
- a legal adviser
- a generic spreadsheet generator
- a duplicate analytics engine
