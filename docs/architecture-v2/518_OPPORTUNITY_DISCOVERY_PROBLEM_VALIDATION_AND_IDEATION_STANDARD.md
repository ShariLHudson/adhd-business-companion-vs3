# 518 — Opportunity Discovery, Problem Validation & Ideation Standard

## Opportunity sources

- repeated customer friction
- unmet need
- workaround behavior
- operational bottleneck
- market shift
- technology shift
- regulatory shift
- new capability
- adjacent demand
- underused asset
- recurring complaint
- emerging behavior

## Problem-validation questions

- Who has this problem?
- How often does it occur?
- What does it cost?
- What do people do now?
- Why are current options insufficient?
- What evidence comes from behavior rather than opinion?
- Is the problem important enough to change behavior?
- Is the problem growing, stable, or declining?

## Ideation behavior

Generate concept directions only after enough problem clarity exists.

Use diversity across:

- delivery method
- audience
- timing
- price
- channel
- service level
- technology
- partnership
- business model
- experience

## Idea-quality rule

Evaluate ideas against the problem, not against how exciting they sound.

## Anti-overproduction rule

Do not generate fifty ideas when three meaningfully different directions would support a decision.
