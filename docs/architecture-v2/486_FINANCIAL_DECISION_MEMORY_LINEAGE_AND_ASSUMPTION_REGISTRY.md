# 486 — Financial Decision Memory, Lineage & Assumption Registry

## Purpose

Preserve why financial decisions were made so users can learn rather than repeatedly reconstruct context.

## Remember

- approved assumptions
- price decisions
- cost estimates
- scenario ranges
- risk tolerance
- decision thresholds
- review dates
- actual outcomes
- lessons
- source documents
- unresolved questions

## Do not remember as fact

- “I am bad with money”
- “This product is a failure”
- “That client is never profitable”
- “Growth always creates cash problems”

Store evidence, conditions, and time period instead of permanent labels.

## Lineage requirements

Each analysis should reference:

- source numbers
- date range
- version
- assumptions
- formulas
- scenario
- decision
- approver
- actual outcome

## Change propagation

When a major assumption changes, identify affected:

- budgets
- forecasts
- pricing
- runway
- investment decisions
- cash-flow plans
- Project commitments

Do not silently overwrite approved plans.
