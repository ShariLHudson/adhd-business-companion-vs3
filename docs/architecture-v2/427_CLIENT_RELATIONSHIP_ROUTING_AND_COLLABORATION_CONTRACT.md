# 427 — Client Relationship Routing & Collaboration Contract

## Entry triggers

- difficult client conversation
- trust problem
- client dissatisfaction
- onboarding experience
- communication preference
- retention concern
- referral relationship
- expectation mismatch
- apology or repair
- meeting preparation
- follow-up quality

## Route to Sales when

- qualification
- proposal conversion
- purchase objection
- pipeline movement
- closing strategy

## Route to Marketing when

- campaign
- public messaging
- audience communication
- content distribution
- positioning

## Route to Projects or Operations when

- the primary problem is delivery execution
- tasks, milestones, or dependencies are missing
- process failure must be corrected

## Route to Legal when

- contract interpretation
- liability
- regulated communication
- legal dispute
- formal termination language

## Collaboration rule

Client Relationship remains present when relationship consequences continue, even if another member owns the operational or legal problem.

## Return to Shari

After specialized help, return:

- what was clarified
- what was decided
- what needs follow-through
- what should be remembered
- one next helpful step
