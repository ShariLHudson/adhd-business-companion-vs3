# 302 — Chamber Member Canonical Architecture

## Purpose

Define the mandatory architecture for every Spark Estate Chamber Member.

Existing Member bundles must be mapped to this structure without discarding valuable work.

## Required Member file families

### 1. Charter

- mission

## Lead versus contributor behavior

### Lead Member

A Member leads when:

- the user's primary intent falls inside its domain
- the Collection declares it as owner
- it owns the decisive capability
- no more specific certified owner exists

### Contributor Member

A Member contributes when:

- a Collection requests one or more published capabilities
- the requested output fits its scope
- its contribution does not replace the Collection owner
- attribution and provenance are retained

### Deferral

A Member must defer when:

- the request is outside scope
- another Member owns the capability
- evidence is insufficient
- the user requires regulated professional advice outside platform boundaries
- the request would duplicate another source of truth

## Member boundaries

Members must not:

- create private Work identity
- create competing task infrastructure
- copy another Member's knowledge to simulate integration
- claim ownership of a cross-functional Collection without registry authority
- bypass capability contracts
- silently mutate Work
- invent user capability levels
- treat all users as the same experience level
- overwhelm users to display expertise

## Adaptive requirements

Every Member must support guidance based on:

- user knowledge
- user experience
- user confidence
- current energy
- available time
- decision fatigue
- urgency
- active Work
- preferred help style

Examples:

Advanced knowledge + low confidence:
- affirm evidence
- provide review and decision support
- avoid unnecessary basic teaching

Low knowledge + high confidence:
- preserve dignity
- surface missing risks
- verify assumptions
- provide concise teaching

Low energy + urgent:
- reduce options
- prioritize essentials
- offer a small executable next step

High capacity + exploratory:
- support deeper comparison
- invite strategic thinking
- provide tradeoffs

## Certification verdicts

- MEMBER PRODUCTION CERTIFIED
- MEMBER CONDITIONALLY CERTIFIED
- MEMBER NOT CERTIFIED

A Member cannot be production certified without a Capability Manifest and Collection Ownership Manifest.
