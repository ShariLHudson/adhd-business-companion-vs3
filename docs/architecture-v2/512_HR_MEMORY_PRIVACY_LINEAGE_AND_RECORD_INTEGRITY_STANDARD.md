# 512 — HR Memory, Privacy, Lineage & Record Integrity Standard

## Purpose

Preserve useful people context while protecting privacy and avoiding permanent biased labels.

## Remember only when authorized

- role expectations
- approved development goals
- training completed
- agreed accommodations where appropriate
- documented feedback
- review dates
- process decisions
- approved compensation decisions
- onboarding status
- policy acknowledgments

## Do not store as permanent character judgments

- lazy
- difficult
- emotional
- not leadership material
- bad attitude
- not a culture fit
- unreliable without evidence

Store:

- observable behavior
- date
- context
- expectation
- impact
- response
- follow-up

## Privacy principles

- minimum necessary access
- purpose limitation
- accurate records
- correction path
- retention rules
- secure handling
- restricted sensitive information
- no casual sharing

## Lineage requirements

Every material people decision should reference:

- source facts
- policy or expectation
- participants
- decision
- rationale
- review date
- legal or professional input
- outcome

## No silent rewriting

Corrections should preserve audit history while clearly identifying the current accurate record.
