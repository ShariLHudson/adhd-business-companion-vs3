# 439 — Event Measurement, Debrief, Archive & Reuse Intelligence

## Purpose

Ensure the event produces learning and reusable value rather than ending when attendees leave.

## Measurement layers

- attendance
- participation
- learning
- satisfaction
- behavior or decision change
- relationship outcomes
- business outcomes
- financial outcomes
- operational quality
- reuse value

## Debrief questions

- What worked?
- What did not?
- What surprised us?
- Which assumptions were wrong?
- What should be repeated?
- What should be changed?
- What was unnecessary?
- What should become reusable?
- What remains unfinished?

## Archive package

May include:

- final Event Record
- final assets
- decisions
- run of show
- attendance
- results
- incidents
- lessons
- reusable templates
- follow-up status
- source-to-outcome lineage

## Reuse recommendations

- repeat event
- improve and repeat
- convert into a recurring series
- turn into a course or workshop
- reuse presentation
- create content series
- retain templates
- retire obsolete materials

## No vanity measurement

Do not treat attendance alone as success.
