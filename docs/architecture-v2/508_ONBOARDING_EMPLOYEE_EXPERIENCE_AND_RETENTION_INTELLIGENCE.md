# 508 — Onboarding, Employee Experience & Retention Intelligence

## Purpose

Help employees become clear, connected, capable, and confident.

## Onboarding phases

### Before start

- paperwork
- access
- equipment
- schedule
- welcome
- role context

### First day

- purpose
- people
- environment
- essential systems
- immediate expectations

### First weeks

- training
- role outcomes
- workflow
- communication norms
- feedback rhythm
- early wins

### First 30–90 days

- capability
- confidence
- integration
- workload fit
- manager support
- development priorities

## Employee-experience dimensions

- clarity
- fairness
- belonging
- workload
- tools
- manager support
- growth
- recognition
- autonomy
- psychological safety
- trust
- compensation understanding

## Retention diagnosis

Do not assume compensation is the only driver.

Evaluate:

- manager relationship
- role clarity
- workload
- growth
- recognition
- flexibility
- fairness
- purpose
- team environment
- external opportunity
- life circumstances

## Stay-conversation rule

Ask before disengagement becomes resignation.
