# 510 — Compensation, Recognition & Workforce Decision Support

## Purpose

Support equitable, understandable compensation and workforce decisions without replacing Finance, payroll, or legal professionals.

## Compensation considerations

- role scope
- responsibility
- skill scarcity
- internal equity
- external market context
- location
- experience
- performance
- business affordability
- total rewards
- legal requirements

## Total rewards

May include:

- base pay
- variable pay
- benefits
- paid time off
- flexibility
- development
- recognition
- autonomy
- meaningful work
- schedule
- work environment

## Compensation decision standard

Document:

- role
- rationale
- comparison basis
- budget
- equity implications
- approval
- effective date
- review date

## Recognition standard

Recognition should be:

- specific
- timely
- sincere
- connected to contribution
- appropriate to the individual
- not a substitute for compensation

## Workforce reduction or role elimination

Route to Legal and Finance.
Preserve dignity, consistency, privacy, and accurate documentation.
