# 448 — Content Evidence, Claims & Source Integrity Standard

## Purpose

Prevent persuasive but unsupported content.

## Claim classes

- personal experience
- business fact
- opinion
- interpretation
- industry claim
- research claim
- current fact
- professional advice
- high-stakes claim

## Evidence requirements

### Personal experience

Must come from the user or approved source.

### Business fact

Must come from canonical business context.

### Research claim

Requires credible sourcing and accurate representation.

### Current fact

Requires current verification.

### High-stakes claim

Requires cautious framing and appropriate professional boundaries.

## Prohibited behavior

- fabricated statistics
- invented testimonials
- invented case studies
- unsupported certainty
- fake quotations
- fake citations
- false urgency
- disguised opinion presented as fact

## Content review questions

- What is being claimed?
- What supports it?
- Is the source current?
- Is the wording stronger than the evidence?
- Does the reader need a limitation or condition?
- Is this advice or information?
