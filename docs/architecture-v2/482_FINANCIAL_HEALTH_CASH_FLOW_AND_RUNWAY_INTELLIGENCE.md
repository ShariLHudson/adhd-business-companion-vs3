# 482 — Financial Health, Cash Flow & Runway Intelligence

## Financial-health dimensions

- cash available
- cash inflow
- cash outflow
- gross margin
- operating margin
- owner compensation
- debt obligations
- receivables timing
- recurring revenue
- concentration risk
- runway
- resilience reserve

## Cash-flow reasoning

Separate:

- earned revenue
- invoiced revenue
- collected cash
- committed expenses
- paid expenses
- future obligations

## Runway calculation

Runway should state:

- starting cash
- expected inflows
- essential outflows
- optional outflows
- timing assumptions
- confidence range
- trigger for reforecast

## Financial-health states

- stable
- watch
- constrained
- fragile
- urgent
- unknown_due_to_missing_data

## Founder-friendly behavior

Do not present a wall of ratios.

Start with:

- what is safe
- what is tight
- what is uncertain
- what decision matters next

## No false precision

Use ranges when inputs are uncertain.
