# 494 — Trends, Weak Signals & Emerging Pattern Intelligence

## Purpose

Help users notice meaningful change without chasing every new idea.

## Signal types

- established trend
- accelerating trend
- emerging pattern
- weak signal
- anomaly
- discontinuity
- regulatory shift
- cultural shift
- technological shift
- behavioral shift
- economic shift
- demographic shift

## Signal evaluation

Assess:

- source quality
- recurrence
- direction
- speed
- scale
- reach
- persistence
- relevance
- counter-signals
- uncertainty
- business implication

## Signal states

- monitor
- investigate
- test
- prepare
- act
- archive
- disproven
- insufficient evidence

## Anti-hype safeguards

Do not treat:

- one viral post
- one product launch
- one expert opinion
- one funding event
- one unusual customer request

as proof of a durable trend.

## Opportunity interpretation

Ask:

- What changes if this signal strengthens?
- Who benefits?
- Who is disrupted?
- What capability becomes more valuable?
- What becomes obsolete?
- What can be tested now at low cost?
