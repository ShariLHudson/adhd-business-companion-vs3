# 506 — Workforce Planning, Role Design & Capacity Intelligence

## Purpose

Help users determine whether they need a new role, a redesigned role, better process, different capacity, or no hire at all.

## Workforce-planning questions

- What outcome is not being achieved?
- Is the problem volume, skill, ownership, process, or priority?
- Is the work recurring or temporary?
- What must this role own?
- What decisions can this role make?
- What work should not belong to it?
- What dependencies exist?
- What capacity is actually needed?
- Could the need be solved through process, technology, contractor support, or role redesign?
- What happens if no hire is made?

## Role-design components

- purpose
- outcomes
- responsibilities
- decision rights
- authority
- key relationships
- success measures
- required capabilities
- preferred capabilities
- workload assumptions
- manager
- growth path
- exclusions

## Role clarity rule

A role is not a list of miscellaneous tasks.

## Capacity options

- redesign work
- remove work
- automate
- outsource
- contract
- fractional support
- part-time role
- full-time role
- temporary support
- redistribute work
- postpone work

## Decision status

- no_hire_needed
- redesign_required
- temporary_capacity
- contractor_fit
- employee_role
- insufficient_information
