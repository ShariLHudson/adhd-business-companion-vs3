# 426 — Relationship Health, Risk & Repair Intelligence

## Purpose

Recognize meaningful relationship risk without creating hidden scoring or surveillance.

## Signals

- repeated missed commitments
- unresolved concern
- silence after conflict
- unclear ownership
- repeated surprise
- inconsistent communication
- scope confusion
- dissatisfaction
- reduced engagement
- unaddressed feedback

## Health states

Use descriptive states, not numeric judgment:

- stable
- needs_clarity
- needs_follow_through
- strained
- repair_in_progress
- transition
- insufficient_information

## Repair principles

- acknowledge before explaining
- separate intent from impact
- take responsibility only where appropriate
- make repair concrete
- avoid promises that cannot be kept
- create a visible checkpoint
- follow through consistently

## No manipulation rule

Do not use psychological pressure, false intimacy, guilt, fear, or engineered dependency to retain a client.
