# 468 — Data & Analytics Canonical Ownership & Non-Duplication Contract

## Data & Analytics owns

- measurement design
- metric definition
- KPI architecture
- data-quality evaluation
- analytical method selection
- descriptive analysis
- diagnostic analysis
- trend interpretation
- segmentation
- forecasting support
- experiment analysis
- dashboard logic
- visualization meaning
- uncertainty communication
- evidence-based decision support
- measurement lineage
- analytical documentation

## Research owns

- external investigation
- source discovery
- qualitative and quantitative evidence gathering
- literature and market research methodology
- source credibility assessment

Research gathers evidence.
Data & Analytics analyzes structured evidence and measurement results.

## Technology owns

- instrumentation implementation
- data pipelines
- databases
- integrations
- event tracking infrastructure
- storage
- access controls
- system reliability

Data & Analytics defines what should be measured and how it should be interpreted.
Technology implements the collection and delivery infrastructure.

## Finance owns

- financial planning
- cash flow
- profitability
- budgeting
- accounting interpretation
- investment and financial-risk reasoning

Data & Analytics may analyze financial data but does not replace Finance's domain judgment.

## Marketing owns

- audience strategy
- positioning
- campaigns
- channels
- demand creation
- acquisition strategy

Data & Analytics measures and interprets performance.
Marketing decides strategic action within its domain.

## AI / Intelligence owns

- cross-domain reasoning
- AI design
- model and workflow intelligence
- broader synthesis

Data & Analytics owns analytical validity, metric integrity, and evidence interpretation.

## Knowledge Management owns

- canonical knowledge storage
- reusable organizational truth
- knowledge lifecycle

Analytics outputs may become knowledge candidates but do not become canonical automatically.

## Non-duplication rule

Data & Analytics must not become:

- a second data warehouse
- a tracking implementation team
- a finance advisor
- a marketing strategist
- a research agent
- a generic chart generator
- an AI forecasting oracle
