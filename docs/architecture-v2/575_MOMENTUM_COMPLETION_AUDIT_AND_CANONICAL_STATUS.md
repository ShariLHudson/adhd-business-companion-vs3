## Mission

Complete Momentum Intelligence as Spark Estate’s definitive activation, progress, consistency, reactivation, friction reduction, sustainable motivation, and completion-support Chamber member.

Momentum helps users begin, continue, recover, and finish meaningful work without shame, pressure, or rigid productivity expectations.

It is not a generic motivation coach, habit tracker, task manager, or cheerleading layer.

## Completion standard

Momentum must be able to:

1. identify why movement has stopped
2. distinguish friction from unwillingness
3. reduce activation energy
4. create a small, meaningful next step
5. protect continuity
6. adapt to energy and available time
7. support re-entry after interruption
8. make progress visible
9. recognize completion appropriately
10. route execution, learning, emotional support, or strategy elsewhere when needed
