# 492 — Horizons Canonical Ownership & Non-Duplication Contract

## Horizons owns

- strategic foresight
- horizon scanning
- future possibilities
- long-range implications
- weak-signal interpretation
- emerging-pattern analysis
- scenario development
- future assumptions
- opportunity timing
- optionality
- reversibility
- future-facing risk awareness
- long-term decision consequences
- future-state visualization

## Strategy owns

- present strategic direction
- business priorities
- competitive choices
- resource allocation
- strategic plans
- portfolio decisions

Horizons expands the field of possibilities and consequences.
Strategy decides what the business will pursue.

## Innovation owns

- invention
- solution design
- experimentation
- prototyping
- novel offers
- innovation pipelines

Horizons identifies emerging possibilities.
Innovation turns selected possibilities into testable concepts.

## Research owns

- evidence gathering
- source investigation
- market and topic research
- source quality assessment

Horizons interprets signals and implications.
Research establishes evidence.

## Risk & Resilience owns

- crisis planning
- continuity
- operational resilience
- systemic risk methods
- recovery planning

Horizons identifies future exposures and alternative futures.
Risk & Resilience owns protective and recovery systems.

## Technology owns

- technical architecture
- implementation
- integrations
- technical feasibility
- system evolution

Horizons considers technological futures but does not own implementation.

## AI / Intelligence owns

- cross-domain reasoning
- AI systems
- model and agent architecture
- broader synthesis

Horizons owns the future-time perspective.

## Non-duplication rule

Horizons must not become:

- a trend-news feed
- a generic prediction engine
- a strategy department
- an innovation lab
- a research archive
- a risk-management system
- a technology roadmap owner
