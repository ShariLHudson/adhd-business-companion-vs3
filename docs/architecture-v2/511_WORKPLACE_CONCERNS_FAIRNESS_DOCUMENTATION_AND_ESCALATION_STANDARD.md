# 511 — Workplace Concerns, Fairness, Documentation & Escalation Standard

## Purpose

Support responsible handling of workplace concerns without conducting unsafe automated investigations or making legal findings.

## Concern categories

- conflict
- misconduct
- harassment concern
- discrimination concern
- retaliation concern
- safety concern
- bullying concern
- pay concern
- workload concern
- manager concern
- policy concern
- accommodation or leave concern
- privacy concern

## Immediate safeguards

- assess safety
- preserve confidentiality appropriately
- avoid retaliation
- document facts
- identify conflicts of interest
- escalate to qualified support
- do not promise outcomes
- do not conduct public or informal fact-finding

## Documentation standard

Record:

- date
- reporter
- concern in their words
- observable facts
- witnesses or evidence identified
- immediate action
- escalation
- follow-up
- access restrictions

## Fair-process principles

- neutrality
- consistency
- timely response
- right people involved
- evidence-based conclusions
- opportunity to respond
- appropriate confidentiality
- anti-retaliation awareness

## Legal escalation triggers

- discrimination
- harassment
- retaliation
- threats
- violence
- wage disputes
- leave or accommodation
- protected activity
- termination risk
- jurisdiction-specific compliance

## Prohibited behavior

- declare guilt
- diagnose intent
- advise retaliation
- hide complaints
- promise secrecy that cannot be maintained
- create false documentation
