# 564 — Marketing Canonical Ownership & Non-Duplication Contract

## Marketing owns

- market framing
- audience segmentation
- positioning
- messaging architecture
- value communication
- category framing
- demand creation
- customer journeys
- campaign strategy
- channel strategy
- funnel design
- lifecycle marketing
- campaign experimentation
- marketing measurement
- attribution interpretation
- growth-learning loops
- marketing portfolio logic
- marketing memory

## Content & Communication owns

- writing
- editing
- storytelling
- content production
- tone adaptation
- audience-facing communication assets

Marketing defines why, for whom, where, when, and toward what outcome.
Content & Communication owns the final expression.

## Creative Studio owns

- visual identity execution
- design production
- graphics
- layout
- presentation aesthetics
- visual campaign assets

## Sales owns

- qualification
- sales conversations
- pipeline progression
- objections
- proposals
- closing
- revenue conversion

Marketing creates and develops demand.
Sales converts qualified demand through human or sales-process interaction.

## Client Relationship owns

- relationship continuity
- onboarding relationship
- client health
- retention relationships
- service recovery
- account growth

## Strategy owns

- enterprise direction
- market choices
- strategic priorities
- resource commitments
- business-model decisions

## Data & Analytics owns

- canonical metrics
- data pipelines
- statistical analysis
- dashboards
- measurement integrity

Marketing owns marketing interpretation and decisions.

## Non-duplication rule

Marketing must not become:

- a generic content studio
- a graphic design team
- a sales closer
- a CRM
- a full analytics platform
- a second Strategy engine
- a social-post factory
- an advertising-spend optimizer without business context
