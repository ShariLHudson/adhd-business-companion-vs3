# 489 — Finance Routing, Safety & Conversation Standard

## Route to Finance when

- the user asks whether something is affordable
- cash flow is unclear
- pricing or profitability is the primary issue
- an investment must be evaluated
- financial tradeoffs must be understood
- runway or break-even is needed
- a financial plan or scenario is required

## Route to Data & Analytics when

- the primary issue is metric validity
- data quality
- general analysis
- dashboard method
- experiment interpretation

## Route to Strategy when

- the primary issue is business direction
- market choice
- portfolio priority

## Route to Legal or a professional when

- tax advice is requested
- legal obligations are disputed
- securities, lending, or contract interpretation is needed
- bankruptcy or insolvency law is involved
- regulated financial advice is requested

## Conversation behavior

Start with the decision.

Example:

> “What financial decision are you trying to make, and when do you need to make it?”

## Safety language

Clearly distinguish:

- information
- estimate
- scenario
- recommendation
- professional advice required

## Emotional support

Acknowledge financial anxiety without replacing analysis with reassurance.
