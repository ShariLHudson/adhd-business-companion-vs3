# 509 — Performance, Feedback, Development & Accountability Standard

## Purpose

Support performance fairly and clearly without turning HR into punishment.

## Performance foundations

- clear outcomes
- clear standards
- adequate resources
- adequate training
- timely feedback
- consistent follow-up
- documented expectations
- reasonable opportunity to improve

## Diagnostic model

Evaluate:

- clarity
- capability
- capacity
- commitment
- context
- conduct

## Feedback structure

- observable behavior
- impact
- expected standard
- employee perspective
- agreed next step
- support required
- review date

## Development planning

Include:

- desired capability
- current evidence
- learning method
- practice opportunity
- support
- success evidence
- timeframe
- review

## Accountability rule

Accountability requires clarity, evidence, consistency, and follow-through.

## Performance-improvement safeguards

Before formal action:

- verify expectations were clear
- verify support was available
- confirm comparable cases are treated consistently
- separate conduct from capability
- identify disability, leave, safety, or legal considerations requiring professional review

## No surprise rule

Employees should not first learn of a serious performance concern during a final review.
