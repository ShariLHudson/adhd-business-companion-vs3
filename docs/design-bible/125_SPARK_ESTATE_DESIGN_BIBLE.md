# 125 — Spark Estate™ Design Bible

## The Definitive Product & Experience Reference

## Purpose

This document is the highest-level product reference for Spark Estate™.

It unifies the Constitution (**113–117**), Design System (**118–124**), Universal Work Engine, Blueprint Platform, Business Pulse, Chamber, Round Table, and future experiences into one product vision.

---

## 1. Product Promise

Spark Estate helps people build meaningful businesses without becoming overwhelmed.

The platform should always feel:

- calm
- intelligent
- trustworthy
- connected
- encouraging
- capable
- human

---

## 2. Core Philosophy

Spark Estate is not:

- a dashboard
- a collection of AI tools
- a document editor
- a project manager

Spark Estate is a **business companion**.

It helps people think, decide, create, execute, learn, recognize progress, and continue moving forward.

---

## 3. The Experience Pyramid

| Level | Layer | Role |
|-------|-------|------|
| **1** | Shari (the companion) | Primary relationship |
| **2** | Work (one identity viewed many ways) | Durable source of truth |
| **3** | Estate (purpose-built environments) | Places that support focus |
| **4** | Intelligence (Chamber, Round Table, Business Pulse) | Quiet, connected awareness |
| **5** | Growth (learning, recognition, evidence, accomplishments) | Progress made visible with care |

---

## 4. Universal Rules

Every feature must:

- reduce cognitive load
- preserve context
- preserve Work identity
- preserve return location
- use relationships instead of copies
- support accessibility
- be ADHD-friendly
- support Business Pulse
- fit Shari's conversational style

---

## 5. Navigation Standard

Conversation is always the preferred navigation method.

Menus support discovery.

Rooms support focus.

Users should rarely need to remember where something lives.

---

## 6. Blueprint Standard

Blueprints are reusable business systems.

| Mode | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Guided Mode** | Complete work |
| **Builder Mode** | Improve the system |
| **Blueprint Home** | Understand and manage the system |

---

## 7. Business Awareness

Business Pulse quietly answers:

- What moved forward?
- What deserves attention?
- What deserves recognition?
- What's the next helpful step?

Never become a widget-heavy dashboard.

---

## 8. Recognition

| Surface | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Wins** | Meaningful momentum |
| **Hall of Accomplishments** | Durable achievements |
| **Evidence Vault** | Discoveries, patterns, lessons, problems solved, contributors |

Each has a unique purpose.

---

## 9. Estate Intelligence

The platform understands relationships between:

Work · Projects · Blueprints · Goals · Calendar · Visual Thinking · Research · Tasks · People · Evidence · Wins · Accomplishments

The user should experience **one connected business**.

---

## 10. Accessibility & ADHD

Default to:

- simplicity
- progressive disclosure
- readable typography
- reduced motion
- sensory-safe sounds
- one primary action
- calm transitions

---

## 11. Product Review Questions

Before approving any feature ask:

1. Does it help users move forward?
2. Does it simplify rather than complicate?
3. Does it fit the companion model?
4. Does it preserve trust?
5. Does it connect through relationships?
6. Does it reinforce Business Pulse?
7. Does it have one obvious primary action?
8. Can users ignore it safely?

If not, redesign.

---

## 12. Success Definition

Spark Estate succeeds when users consistently feel:

- "I know what I'm working on."
- "I know why it matters."
- "I can always continue where I left off."
- "I don't feel overwhelmed."
- "I've made meaningful progress."
- "The platform remembers what helps me."
- "I trust Shari."

---

## Governance

This Design Bible is the **primary product reference**.

If implementation, UX, prompts, or architecture conflict:

1. Preserve user trust.
2. Preserve companion continuity.
3. Preserve Work identity.
4. Preserve calm.
5. Preserve accessibility.
6. Preserve relationships.

Everything else is secondary.
