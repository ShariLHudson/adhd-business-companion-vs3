## Purpose

The Universal Experience Standards™ define the design principles, interaction behaviors, and experiential qualities that **every member-facing experience** inside Spark must follow.

These standards ensure that Spark feels like **one coherent entrepreneurial ecosystem** rather than a collection of disconnected features.

Every room, conversation, workflow, and creation experience inherits these standards.

---

## Mission

Every interaction inside Spark should help members think more clearly, make better decisions, build confidence, and move forward with less cognitive effort.

Members should never feel like they are learning different software.

They should feel like they are continuing **one relationship**.

## Universal Design Principles™

### 1. One Relationship™

Members should always feel they are interacting with the **same Spark**.

Whether they are creating · planning · learning · reflecting · exploring · asking questions — the personality, tone, memory, and behavior remain consistent.

### 2. Orientation Before Action™

Members should immediately understand:

- Where they are
- What this space is for
- What they can accomplish here
- What the next logical step is

Never make members figure out where to begin.

### 3. Calm Before Complexity™

Simple things should remain simple.

Complexity should appear only when it provides value.

Spark should **progressively reveal** advanced capabilities rather than presenting everything at once.

### 4. Executive Function First™

Every experience should intentionally reduce executive function demands.

**Examples:** fewer decisions · clear priorities · one obvious next step · limited simultaneous choices · visual breathing room · progressive disclosure.

**Spark carries complexity. Members carry creativity.**

### 5. Confidence Before Completion™

Members should feel more capable **even before** finishing the task.

Progress should feel meaningful. Small wins matter. Confidence creates momentum.

### 6. Guidance Before Automation™

Spark should guide members — not replace them.

The objective is better thinking — not complete automation.

Members remain the author, strategist, and decision-maker.

### 7. Ownership Always™

Every completed asset should feel like: **"My work."**

Never: **"The AI's work."**

Spark collaborates. Members create.

### 8. Connection Everywhere™

Nothing meaningful exists alone.

Every experience should naturally connect to: Business Brain™ · Business Assets™ · Guidance Engine™ · Gallery™ · Spark Cards™ · Momentum Builders™ · Community™ · Estate™.

Connections should feel natural. Never forced.

**Type:** `UniversalDesignPrinciple` in `lib/sparkUniversalExperience/types.ts`

## Cognitive Load Standards™

Spark should intentionally **minimize:**

- Decision fatigue
- Context switching
- Repetition
- Information overload
- Navigation complexity
- Memory burden

Spark should **maximize:**

- Recognition over recall
- Visual clarity
- Progressive learning
- Immediate orientation
- Obvious next actions

**Type:** `CognitiveLoadMinimize` · `CognitiveLoadMaximize` in `lib/sparkUniversalExperience/types.ts`

---

## Information Density™

Members should never feel overwhelmed by information.

**Default principle:**

Show only what helps the **next decision**.

Everything else remains available — but hidden until needed.

Aligns with T-003 progressive disclosure and max-three-choices rule.

## Guidance Standards™

Spark should:

- Offer possibilities
- Explain trade-offs
- Encourage thinking
- Respect autonomy

**Never** pressure · manipulate · shame.

Aligns with [Spec 102 — Trust Experience](./TRUST_EXPERIENCE_FRAMEWORK.md) and [T-008 Decision Experience](./DECISION_EXPERIENCE_FRAMEWORK.md).

---

## Celebration Standards™

**Celebrate:**

- Growth · Learning · Reflection · Implementation · Capability

**Not** streaks · addiction · endless engagement.

Spark celebrates **transformation**. Not usage.

---

## Reflection Standards™

Every major experience should create opportunities for reflection.

Reflection strengthens: learning · memory · decision quality · confidence · transformation.

Spark is not a collection of features.

It is **one continuous entrepreneurial experience**.

Every interaction should feel like another step in the member's transformation.

Every room should strengthen that journey.

Every experience should leave the member **more capable** than when they arrived.
