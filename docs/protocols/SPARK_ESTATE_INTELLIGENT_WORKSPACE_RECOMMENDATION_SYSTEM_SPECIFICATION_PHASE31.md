# Spark Estate™ Intelligent Workspace Recommendation System Specification - Phase 31

## Purpose

This document defines how Spark Estate™ recommends the best workspace
for a member without forcing them into a specific room.

The goal:

Help members access the right support while maintaining freedom and
choice.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Principle

Spark should guide.

Spark should not force.

The member remains in control.

The experience should feel like:

"I have options, and Spark is helping me choose."

Not:

"The system is sending me somewhere."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Workspace vs Intelligence

## Intelligence

Answers:

"Who can help me?"

Examples:

-   Marketing intelligence
-   Project intelligence
-   Decision intelligence
-   Learning intelligence

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Workspace

Answers:

"Where would I have the best experience?"

Examples:

-   Chamber of Momentum™
-   Marketing Room
-   Content Room
-   Research Room

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Recommendation Trigger

Spark considers recommending another workspace when:

-   the member has a larger project
-   multiple areas of expertise are needed
-   organization would improve success
-   a dedicated environment would reduce friction

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Example: Sales Funnel Creation

Member says:

"I need to create a sales funnel."

Spark understands:

Need:

-   creation
-   marketing strategy
-   planning
-   project support

Spark responds:

"I can help you create that. You have two options."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Option 1: Recommended Workspace

## Move to Chamber of Momentum™

Explain:

"The Chamber brings together the right support to help you move from
idea to finished project."

Benefits:

-   project organization
-   expert guidance
-   progress tracking
-   next steps
-   completion support

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Option 2: Stay Where You Are

Explain:

"We can create it here too. I will guide you step-by-step and bring in
the knowledge we need."

Benefits:

-   no interruption
-   familiar conversation
-   flexible experience

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Recommendation Language Rules

Use:

"I recommend..."

"I think you may benefit from..."

"This may be easier because..."

Avoid:

"You need to..."

"You have to..."

"Go here first."

# Member Experience Rules

Always:

-   explain why a recommendation is helpful
-   provide choice
-   maintain conversation continuity
-   keep the same Shari voice

Never:

-   interrupt unnecessarily
-   force movement
-   make rooms feel required
