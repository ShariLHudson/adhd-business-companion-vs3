## The Purpose

The Momentum Institute‚ĄĘ exists to help entrepreneurs become **more capable**, **more confident**, **more strategic**, and **more successful**.

It is **not** a course platform.  
It is **not** a training library.  
It is **not** a university in the traditional sense.

It is a **living Entrepreneur Development Center** where members continually strengthen the knowledge, thinking, skills, and habits required to build extraordinary businesses.

The Institute does **not** measure success by completed lessons.  
It measures success by **real-world transformation**.

## Make It Mine‚ĄĘ

Every learning experience eventually asks:

> **"Would you like to make this work for YOUR business?"**

This becomes **coaching** ‚ÄĒ not another lesson.

Make It Mine‚ĄĘ is **permission-gated**. Shari adapts the concept to the member's context, offers, stage, and constraints. The member owns the outcome.

**Implementation:** `Make It MineIntent` ¬∑ Coach With Shari‚ĄĘ lifecycle stage ¬∑ `lib/momentumInstitute/makeItMine/`

## The Return‚ĄĘ

Every lesson ends with invitation, not closure:

> **"When you have a chance to use this, come back and tell me what happened."**

Real growth happens **after implementation**.

The Return‚ĄĘ:

- Acknowledges progress without pressure  
- Opens the door for Evidence Vault‚ĄĘ (with permission)  
- Feeds Growth Profile‚ĄĘ quietly  
- Suggests one natural next card or experience ‚ÄĒ never five  

**No finish lines.** Only continuation.

## My Institute Cabinet‚ĄĘ
