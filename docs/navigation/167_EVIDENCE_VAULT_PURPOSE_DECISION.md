# Evidence Vault Purpose Decision

## Core Purpose

The Evidence Vault preserves meaningful discoveries about:

- the user
- their work
- people they helped
- problems they solved
- lessons they learned
- patterns they noticed
- ways they grew
- things they figured out or created

## Not the Primary Purpose

The Vault is not primarily:

- an accomplishments archive
- a strengths inventory
- an achievement tracker
- a portfolio
- proof of worth

## Meaning Over Achievement

An accomplishment may be included when it reveals something meaningful.

The discovery is the important part.

## Separation

Achievement Library owns formal accomplishments and portfolio items.

Evidence Vault owns private discoveries, learning, impact, and problem-solving.
