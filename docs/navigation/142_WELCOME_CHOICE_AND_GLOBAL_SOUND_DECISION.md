# Welcome Choice and Global Sound Decision

## Start With What Matters Most

This is not Plan My Day.

It is a lightweight path that recommends one meaningful next action without reopening prior work or requiring full-day planning.

The user may intentionally continue into Plan My Day through **Plan More of My Day**.

## Plan or Adapt My Day

This remains the full daily planning workflow.

## Help Me Choose

This remains the broader route for deciding among several directions or Spark Estate destinations.

## Global Sound

Sound Off must be globally visible and available in one click.

Users must never have to hunt through rooms or settings to stop audio.

## Default

All audio is opt-in.

Playback never resumes automatically.
