# My Business Estate and My Profile Reliability Decision

## Required Navigation

The SH/Profile menu must reliably open:

- My Business Estate
- My Profile

Both must work on the first click and remain independent destinations.

## Profile Readability

All text in My Profile must use dark, accessible font colors on light backgrounds.

This includes every heading, label, value, control, helper message, and expandable section.
