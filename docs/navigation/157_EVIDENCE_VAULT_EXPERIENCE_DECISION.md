# Evidence Vault Experience Decision

## Locked State

The locked Evidence Vault shows:

- the Vault doors
- the moving key
- one instruction:
  - Click the moving key to open the Vault.

No other action buttons are required.

## Open State

After opening:

- remove the Discovery File image
- show Today’s Discovery directly
- remove all ghosted or duplicate Vault content
- provide a readable discovery panel
- add How Do I…

## Conversation

Show Conversation is not part of the Evidence Vault experience and must be removed.

## State Rule

Locked, opening, discovery, and open states must not be mounted simultaneously.
